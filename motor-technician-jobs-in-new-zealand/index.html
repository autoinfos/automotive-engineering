Motor Technician Jobs In New Zealand A Lucrative Career Choice

Written by Petter Sep 26, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you passionate about cars and have a knack for fixing them? Do you want to pursue a career as a motor technician in New Zealand? If yes, then this blog post is for you.

Many individuals who aspire to become motor technicians face several challenges, including lack of resources, guidance, and career opportunities. Moreover, finding a job as a motor technician in New Zealand is not easy, and the competition is fierce.

However, the good news is that the demand for motor technicians in New Zealand is on the rise, and the job market is expected to grow in the coming years. According to the New Zealand government, the automotive industry is one of the country's largest employers, with over 70,000 people working in the sector.

In this blog post, we will discuss the target of motor technician jobs in New Zealand, the qualifications and skills required, job opportunities, and the benefits of pursuing a career as a motor technician in New Zealand.

Target of Motor Technician Jobs in New Zealand

Motor technician jobs in New Zealand target individuals who have a passion for cars and a keen eye for detail. The job involves repairing, servicing, and maintaining cars, trucks, and other vehicles. Motor technicians need to be knowledgeable in various areas, including engine mechanics, braking systems, electrical systems, and computer diagnostics.

As a motor technician, you will be responsible for diagnosing and fixing problems with vehicles, ensuring they are roadworthy and safe to drive. You will work with a range of different tools and equipment, and you will need to have excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.

Qualifications and Skills Required

To become a motor technician in New Zealand, you will need to have a National Certificate in Motor Industry (Automotive Electrical and Mechanical Engineering) (Level 3 or 4) or equivalent qualification. Additionally, you will need to have practical experience working with vehicles and have good communication skills.

Other skills that are essential for a motor technician include:

  • Good problem-solving skills
  • Attention to detail
  • Good communication skills
  • Ability to work under pressure
  • Ability to work as part of a team

Job Opportunities

Job opportunities for motor technicians in New Zealand are expected to grow over the coming years. According to Careers NZ, there is a shortage of qualified motor technicians in New Zealand, and this is expected to continue in the future.

Motor technicians can work in a range of different settings, including car dealerships, auto repair shops, and manufacturing plants. The average salary for a motor technician in New Zealand is around $50,000 per year, but this can vary depending on the employer and the location.

Benefits of Pursuing a Career as a Motor Technician in New Zealand

There are many benefits to pursuing a career as a motor technician in New Zealand. Firstly, there is a growing demand for qualified motor technicians, which means that there are plenty of job opportunities available.

Secondly, the automotive industry in New Zealand is constantly evolving, which means that there is always something new to learn. This makes the job interesting and challenging.

Finally, working as a motor technician can be a rewarding experience. You get to help people by fixing their cars and ensuring they are safe to drive. It's a great feeling to know that you have made a positive difference in someone's life.

Personal Experience

As a motor technician in New Zealand, I can attest to the fact that it is a rewarding and exciting career choice. I have had the opportunity to work with a range of different vehicles and have learned a lot about engine mechanics, electrical systems, and computer diagnostics.

One of the things I love about my job is that there is always something new to learn. The automotive industry is constantly evolving, and new technologies and techniques are being developed all the time.

If you have a passion for cars and enjoy working with your hands, then I would highly recommend pursuing a career as a motor technician in New Zealand.

Conclusion of Motor Technician Jobs in New Zealand

Motor technician jobs in New Zealand are a lucrative career choice for individuals who have a passion for cars and enjoy working with their hands. The job market for motor technicians is expected to grow in the coming years, and there is a shortage of qualified motor technicians in the country.
