Car AC systems can develop several issues over time, such as refrigerant leaks, faulty compressors, clogged filters, and broken condenser fans. These issues can cause your car's AC system to blow warm air or not work at all. It's essential to get these issues fixed as soon as possible to avoid further damage to your car's AC system.
