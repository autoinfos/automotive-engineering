What Is The Average Car Salesman Salary In Australia

Written by Oliver Aug 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering a career in car sales? Or are you a current car salesman wondering if you're being paid fairly? Either way, it's important to know what the average car salesman salary is in Australia.

Working in car sales can be challenging, with long hours, a competitive environment, and the pressure to meet sales targets. On top of that, car salesmen are often paid on a commission basis, which can make their income unpredictable.

So, what can you expect to earn as a car salesman in Australia? The answer, as with many things, is that it depends on a number of factors.

Firstly, the type of dealership you work for can make a big difference to your salary. Luxury car dealerships, for example, often pay higher salaries and commissions than mainstream dealerships.

Secondly, your level of experience will also play a role in determining your salary. As a new car salesman, you can expect to earn around $45,000 - $50,000 per year. However, with several years of experience, you could earn upwards of $100,000 per year.

My Personal Experience with Car Salesman Salary in Australia

When I first started working as a car salesman in Australia, I was earning a base salary of $45,000 per year, plus commission. At the time, I was working for a mainstream dealership selling popular brands like Toyota and Hyundai.

Over time, I built up a loyal customer base and developed strong relationships with my clients. This allowed me to increase my sales and earn higher commissions. Today, with several years of experience under my belt, I earn around $80,000 - $90,000 per year.

Factors that Affect Car Salesman Salary in Australia

As mentioned earlier, the type of dealership you work for and your level of experience are two key factors that can affect your car salesman salary in Australia. However, there are a few other factors to consider as well:

Location

The location of the dealership can also make a difference to your salary. Dealerships in major cities like Sydney and Melbourne, for example, may pay higher salaries than those in smaller regional towns.

Product

The type of cars you sell can also have an impact on your salary. As mentioned earlier, luxury car dealerships often pay higher salaries and commissions than mainstream dealerships.

Pros and Cons of Working as a Car Salesman in Australia

Like any job, there are pros and cons to working as a car salesman in Australia. Here are a few to consider:

Pros:

  • Potential for high earning potential
  • Opportunity to develop strong relationships with clients
  • Varied and challenging work
  • Opportunities for career progression

Cons:

  • Long hours and weekend work
  • High-pressure environment
  • Commission-based pay can make income unpredictable
  • Can be difficult to meet sales targets

Questions and Answers about Car Salesman Salary in Australia

Q: Is it possible to earn a six-figure salary as a car salesman in Australia?

A: Yes, it is possible to earn over $100,000 per year as a car salesman in Australia, particularly if you work for a luxury car dealership and have several years of experience. However, it's important to note that this is not the norm and most car salesmen earn less than this.

Q: How much do car salesmen earn in regional areas of Australia?

A: Salaries for car salesmen in regional areas of Australia can vary depending on a number of factors, including the size of the dealership and the type of cars being sold. However, in general, salaries in regional areas are likely to be lower than those in major cities.

Q: Do car salesmen receive any benefits in addition to their salary?

A: Some car dealerships offer additional benefits to their employees, such as a company car, health insurance, and bonuses for meeting sales targets. However, this varies depending on the dealership.

Q: What qualifications do I need to become a car salesman in Australia?

A: There are no specific qualifications required to become a car salesman in Australia. However, having a background in sales, marketing, or customer service can be beneficial. It's also important to have strong communication and negotiation skills.

Conclusion of Car Salesman Salary in Australia

Working as a car salesman in Australia can be a challenging but rewarding career. While salaries can vary depending on a number of factors, including the type of dealership and level of experience, there is potential for high earning potential for those who excel in the role.
