The Different Types of Car Paint Jobs

When it comes to car paint jobs, there are several different types to choose from. The most common types include a single stage paint job, a two-stage paint job, and a custom paint job. A single stage paint job involves a one-step process where the paint and clear coat are applied at the same time. A two-stage paint job is a two-step process that involves a base coat and a clear coat. Finally, a custom paint job involves a unique design or color scheme that is tailored to your specific preferences.
