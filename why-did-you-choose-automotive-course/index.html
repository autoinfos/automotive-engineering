Job Opportunities in the Automotive Industry

The automotive industry offers a wide range of job opportunities. Some of the most common jobs in the automotive industry include:

Automotive Technician

An automotive technician is responsible for diagnosing and repairing cars. They use diagnostic equipment to identify problems and perform repairs on everything from engines to brakes.

Automotive Engineer

An automotive engineer is responsible for designing and developing cars. They use computer-aided design software to create 3D models of cars and test them for safety and performance.
