Top Automotive Engineering Colleges In India

Written by Christine Aug 24, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you interested in pursuing a career in automotive engineering? Do you want to know about the best automotive engineering colleges in India? If yes, then this post is for you. In this post, we will discuss the top automotive engineering colleges in India and how you can choose the best one for yourself.

As a student looking to pursue a degree in automotive engineering, you may face various challenges in selecting the right college. Choosing the right college is essential for a successful career in this field. Therefore, it is imperative to consider various factors such as the college's reputation, infrastructure, faculty, and placements.

The target of this post is to provide you with information about the best automotive engineering colleges in India. Our aim is to make your decision-making process easier by providing you with all the necessary details.

In summary, the best automotive engineering colleges in India are IIT Delhi, IIT Bombay, Vellore Institute of Technology, PSG College of Technology, and SRM Institute of Science and Technology. These colleges have excellent infrastructure, experienced faculty, and offer excellent placement opportunities to their students.

IIT Delhi

My personal experience with IIT Delhi was excellent. The college has world-class infrastructure, and the faculty is highly experienced. The placement opportunities are also outstanding, with many reputed companies visiting the campus for placements.

IIT Bombay

IIT Bombay is another top college for automotive engineering in India. The college has a reputation for providing quality education and has many experienced faculty members. The campus is well-equipped with modern technology, and the placement opportunities are excellent.

Vellore Institute of Technology

Vellore Institute of Technology is a private college that offers a degree in automotive engineering. The college has a beautiful campus, and the faculty is highly experienced. The placement opportunities are also excellent, with many reputed companies visiting the campus for placements.

How to Choose the Best Automotive Engineering College in India?

Choosing the best automotive engineering college in India can be daunting. However, you can make the right decision by considering various factors such as the college's reputation, infrastructure, faculty, and placements. It would help if you also looked at the college's accreditation and the curriculum before making a decision.

SRM Institute of Science and Technology

SRM Institute of Science and Technology is another top college for automotive engineering in India. The college has a beautiful campus, and the faculty is highly experienced. The placement opportunities are also excellent, with many reputed companies visiting the campus for placements.

Question and Answer

Q. Which college is the best for automotive engineering in India?

A. IIT Delhi, IIT Bombay, Vellore Institute of Technology, PSG College of Technology, and SRM Institute of Science and Technology are the best colleges for automotive engineering in India.

Q. What is the eligibility criteria for pursuing automotive engineering in India?

A. To pursue automotive engineering in India, a student must have completed their 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as the main subjects. They must also pass the entrance exam conducted by the respective college or university.

A. The average salary of an automotive engineer in India is around Rs. 6 lakhs per annum.
