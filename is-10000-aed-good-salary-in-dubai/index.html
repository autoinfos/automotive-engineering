When I first moved to Dubai, I was earning 10000 AED, and I was worried whether it would be enough to cover my expenses. However, I soon realized that it was possible to lead a comfortable life with proper budgeting and planning.

Is 10000 AED a Good Salary for Fresh Graduates in Dubai?

For fresh graduates, earning 10000 AED in Dubai is a good starting salary, especially if you don't have any experience. It is essential to remember that Dubai is a highly competitive city, and you may need to work hard and gain experience to earn more in the future.
