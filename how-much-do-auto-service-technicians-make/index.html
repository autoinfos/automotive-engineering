How Much Do Auto Service Technicians Make

Written by Daniel Jun 23, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you considering becoming an auto service technician? If so, one of the most pressing questions on your mind is likely how much you can expect to make in this field. It's a valid concern, as everyone wants to ensure that they are making enough money to support themselves and their families. In this blog post, we'll take a look at the average salaries of auto service technicians and what factors can affect their earnings.

Working as an auto service technician can be a physically demanding job that requires long hours on your feet. Additionally, the work can be dirty and require dealing with difficult customers at times. All of these factors can make it a challenging field to work in, but for those who have a passion for cars and enjoy working with their hands, it can be a rewarding career. However, with all of that said, one of the biggest pain points for those considering this field is how much they can expect to make.

The average salary for an auto service technician in the United States is around $42,000 per year, but this can vary based on a variety of factors. For example, those who work for dealerships or specialty repair shops may earn more than those who work for independent garages. Additionally, those who specialize in certain types of vehicles or systems may also be able to command higher salaries.

Overall, it's important to keep in mind that your salary as an auto service technician will depend on a variety of factors, including your experience, education, and certifications. The more you invest in your training and education, the more opportunities you will have to earn higher salaries and advance in your career.

What Affects the Salaries of Auto Service Technicians?

There are several key factors that can affect the salaries of auto service technicians. One of the biggest is the type of employer you work for. As we mentioned earlier, those who work for dealerships or specialty repair shops may be able to earn more than those who work for independent garages.

Another factor is your level of experience and education. Those who have completed formal training programs or who have earned certification from organizations like the National Institute for Automotive Service Excellence (ASE) may be able to command higher salaries. Additionally, those who specialize in certain areas, such as hybrid or electric vehicles, may also be able to earn more.

Finally, your geographic location can also impact your salary as an auto service technician. Those who work in areas with higher costs of living, such as major metropolitan areas, may be able to earn more than those who work in more rural areas.

The Importance of Continuing Education for Auto Service Technicians

As we mentioned earlier, one of the key factors that can impact your salary as an auto service technician is your level of education and training. Investing in your education and continuing to learn about new technologies and techniques can help you stay competitive in the job market and earn more money.

One way to do this is by earning certification from organizations like ASE. These certifications demonstrate to employers and customers that you have a high level of expertise and skill in your field. Additionally, attending training programs and workshops can help you stay up to date on the latest trends and technologies in the automotive industry.

The Future of Auto Service Technician Salaries

Overall, the outlook for auto service technician salaries is positive. According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, employment in this field is expected to grow by 6% from 2019 to 2029, which is faster than the average for all occupations. This means that there will continue to be a demand for skilled technicians, which should help to drive up salaries over time.

Question and Answer

Q: Do auto service technicians make a good living?

A: While salaries can vary based on a variety of factors, the average salary for an auto service technician in the United States is around $42,000 per year, which is a good living for many people.

Q: Is it necessary to have certification to work as an auto service technician?

A: It's not strictly necessary to have certification to work as an auto service technician, but it can help you to earn higher salaries and advance in your career. Additionally, many employers prefer to hire technicians who have completed formal training programs or who have earned certification from organizations like ASE.

Q: What is the job outlook for auto service technicians?

A: The job outlook for auto service technicians is positive. Employment in this field is expected to grow by 6% from 2019 to 2029, which is faster than the average for all occupations.

Q: How can I earn more money as an auto service technician?

A: To earn more money as an auto service technician, you can invest in your education and training, earn certification from organizations like ASE, specialize in certain areas, and seek out employment with dealerships or specialty repair shops.

Conclusion of How Much Do Auto Service Technicians Make

Overall, working as an auto service technician can be a rewarding career that offers opportunities for growth and advancement. While salaries can vary based on a variety of factors, investing in your education and continuing to learn about new technologies and techniques can help you to stay competitive in the job market and earn higher salaries over time.
