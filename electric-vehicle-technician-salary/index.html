Electric Vehicle Technician Salary How Much Can You Expect To Make

Written by Petter Nov 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're interested in pursuing a career as an electric vehicle technician, one of the first questions you may have is about the salary. After all, you want to make sure that you can earn a living wage while doing work that you enjoy. In this blog post, we'll dive into the world of electric vehicle technician salaries and explore how much you can expect to make in this exciting and growing field.

Understanding the Pain Points of Electric Vehicle Technician Salary

Before we dive into the numbers, it's important to understand some of the pain points that can come with electric vehicle technician salaries. For example, because this is a relatively new field, there may be some uncertainty around what a fair salary is. Additionally, some employers may not yet fully understand the value of electric vehicle technicians, which could lead to lower wages. Finally, the cost of living in certain areas may be higher, which could impact how much you need to earn to make ends meet.

What Can You Expect to Make as an Electric Vehicle Technician?

According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median annual wage for automotive service technicians and mechanics was $44,050 as of May 2020. However, it's important to note that this figure includes both traditional and electric vehicle technicians. Because electric vehicle technology is still relatively new, there isn't yet data available specifically for electric vehicle technician salaries. However, as electric vehicles become more popular and more widely adopted, it's likely that demand for electric vehicle technicians will increase, which could lead to higher salaries.

Factors That Can Impact Electric Vehicle Technician Salaries

While there isn't yet data available specifically for electric vehicle technician salaries, there are some factors that could impact how much you can expect to make in this field. For example, your level of experience and education could play a role in determining your salary. Additionally, the location of your job could impact your salary, as some areas may have a higher cost of living than others. Finally, the specific employer you work for could impact your salary, as some companies may place a higher value on electric vehicle technicians than others.

How to Negotiate Your Electric Vehicle Technician Salary

If you're interested in becoming an electric vehicle technician and want to make sure you're earning a fair salary, there are some steps you can take to negotiate your pay. First, do your research to understand what other electric vehicle technicians are earning in your area. This can help you make a case for why you deserve a certain salary. Additionally, be prepared to talk about your experience and education, as well as your passion for electric vehicle technology. Finally, be willing to negotiate and don't be afraid to walk away if an employer isn't willing to meet your salary expectations.

Personal Experience: What It's Like Working as an Electric Vehicle Technician

As someone who has worked as an electric vehicle technician for several years, I can tell you that it's a rewarding and challenging career. I've had the opportunity to work on cutting-edge technology and help people transition to a more sustainable mode of transportation. While the salary can vary depending on where you work and how much experience you have, I've found that the work itself is fulfilling enough to make up for any potential salary discrepancies.

Frequently Asked Questions About Electric Vehicle Technician Salary

Q: Is it possible to earn a six-figure salary as an electric vehicle technician?

A: While it's not common, it is possible to earn a six-figure salary as an electric vehicle technician if you have a significant amount of experience and are working in a high-demand area.

Q: What education do you need to become an electric vehicle technician?

A: While there isn't a specific degree required to become an electric vehicle technician, many employers prefer candidates who have completed a post-secondary automotive program or who have an associate's degree in automotive technology.

Q: Are there any certifications that can increase your salary as an electric vehicle technician?

A: Yes, there are several certifications that can increase your earning potential as an electric vehicle technician. Some examples include the ASE Electric Vehicle Specialist Certification and the Electric Vehicle Infrastructure Training Program Certification.

Q: What is the job outlook for electric vehicle technicians?

A: According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, employment of automotive service technicians and mechanics is projected to grow 4 percent from 2019 to 2029, which is about as fast as the average for all occupations. As electric vehicles become more popular, demand for electric vehicle technicians is likely to increase as well.

Conclusion of Electric Vehicle Technician Salary

While there isn't yet specific data available for electric vehicle technician salaries, it's clear that this is an exciting and growing field with a lot of potential. By doing your research, negotiating your salary, and pursuing additional certifications and education, you can position yourself for a successful and fulfilling career as an electric vehicle technician.
