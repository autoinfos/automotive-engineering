Question and Answer

Q. How often should I replace my car battery?

A. It is recommended to replace your car battery every three to five years, depending on usage and weather conditions.

Q. How do I know if my car battery needs to be replaced?

A. Signs of a failing battery include slow engine crank, dashboard warning lights, and a bloated battery case.

Q. Can I install the battery myself?

A. While it is possible to install the battery yourself, it is recommended to seek professional help to avoid damage to your vehicle or injury.

Q. How much does a car battery cost in UAE?

A. The cost of a car battery in UAE depends on the brand and type of battery. However, you can expect to pay anywhere between AED 200 to AED 800 for a quality battery.
