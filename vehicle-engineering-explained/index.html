Common Questions About Vehicle Engineering Explained

Q: What is the most important system in a car?

A: The engine is the most important system in a car, as it is responsible for providing power to the wheels.

Q: Can I perform my own car repairs?

A: It depends on your level of experience and knowledge. Simple repairs such as changing the oil or replacing a filter can often be done by anyone, but more complex repairs should be left to a professional.

Q: How often should I get my car serviced?

A: It's generally recommended to get your car serviced every 6,000-10,000 miles, depending on the make and model of your car.

Q: What is the difference between a transmission and a gearbox?

A: A gearbox is a type of transmission that is used in manual cars. Automatic cars use a different type of transmission called an automatic transmission.
