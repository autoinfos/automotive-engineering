Have you ever wondered how much automotive engineering managers earn? The target of automotive engineering manager salary varies depending on a number of factors, such as your level of experience, your education and qualifications, and the size and type of company you work for. However, this can range from as low as $77,000 to as high as $164,000 or more, depending on the factors mentioned above.

The Factors Impacting Automotive Engineering Manager Salary

Tips for Negotiating Your Automotive Engineering Manager Salary

If you're looking to negotiate your automotive engineering manager salary, there are a few things you can do to increase your chances of success.
