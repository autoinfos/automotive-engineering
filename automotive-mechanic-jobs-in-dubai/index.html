What are Automotive Mechanic Jobs in Dubai?

Automotive mechanic jobs in Dubai involve diagnosing and repairing a wide range of vehicles, from cars and trucks to buses and heavy machinery. Mechanics are responsible for identifying and fixing mechanical issues, as well as performing routine maintenance tasks such as oil changes, tire rotations, and brake repairs. In addition to traditional mechanical skills, automotive mechanics in Dubai must also be familiar with the latest technologies and software used in the automotive industry.
