Question and Answer

Q: What is the average cost of installing an HVAC system in Dubai?
A: The cost of installing an HVAC system in Dubai can vary depending on factors such as the size of the space and type of system. It's best to get quotes from multiple companies to compare pricing.

Q: What is district cooling?
A: District cooling is a centralized cooling system that distributes chilled water to buildings through a network of pipes. It's a more energy-efficient and sustainable alternative to traditional air conditioning systems.

Q: How often should I replace my HVAC filters?
A: It's recommended to replace your HVAC filters every 1-3 months, depending on factors such as the type of filter and the amount of use.

Q: Can I maintain my HVAC system myself?
A: While there are some maintenance tasks you can do yourself, such as changing filters, it's recommended to have your system professionally maintained to ensure its longevity and efficiency.
