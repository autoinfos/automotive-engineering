Is It Worth It To Get A Microsoft Certification

Written by Petter Jul 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering getting a Microsoft certification but wondering if it's worth the investment? With technology evolving rapidly, it's important to stay up-to-date with the latest skills and knowledge. In this post, we'll explore whether or not getting a Microsoft certification is worth it and provide insights into the benefits of doing so.

One of the biggest pain points surrounding the decision to get a Microsoft certification is the cost. It's true that obtaining a certification can be expensive, with fees ranging from a few hundred to a few thousand dollars. Additionally, the time and effort required to study and prepare for the exam can be daunting. However, it's important to consider the potential return on investment in terms of career advancement and salary increases.

The short answer is yes - getting a Microsoft certification can be worth it. Not only does it demonstrate to employers that you have expertise in a specific area, but it can also increase your earning potential. According to a study by Global Knowledge, certified IT professionals earn an average of 15% more than their non-certified counterparts. Additionally, certifications can provide opportunities for career advancement and job security.

In summary, if you're looking to advance your career in the IT industry and increase your earning potential, getting a Microsoft certification can be a smart investment. It's important to weigh the costs and benefits before making a decision, but in many cases, the return on investment can make it worthwhile.

The Benefits of Getting a Microsoft Certification

As mentioned, a Microsoft certification can lead to higher salaries and career advancement opportunities. However, there are other benefits to consider as well. Personally, obtaining a certification can boost your confidence and credibility in the workplace. It can also provide opportunities for networking and learning from other certified professionals in the industry.

From a business perspective, having certified professionals on your team can improve productivity and efficiency. Employers can also benefit from increased customer satisfaction and a competitive edge in the market.

My Experience with Microsoft Certification

As a software developer, I decided to pursue a Microsoft certification in Azure. While it required a significant investment of time and money, I found that it was well worth it. The certification not only helped me to improve my skills and knowledge in Azure, but it also opened up new job opportunities and increased my earning potential.

What to Consider Before Getting a Microsoft Certification

Before pursuing a certification, it's important to consider your career goals and the specific skills and knowledge you want to acquire. Research the different types of certifications available and determine which ones align with your interests and career path. It's also important to have a solid understanding of the exam content and format, as well as the resources available for preparation.

How to Prepare for the Microsoft Certification Exam

There are a variety of resources available to help you prepare for the Microsoft certification exam, including online courses, study guides, and practice exams. It's important to create a study plan that works for you and allows you to dedicate enough time and effort to adequately prepare for the exam. Additionally, consider joining online communities and forums to connect with other certified professionals and learn from their experiences.

Final Thoughts

Overall, getting a Microsoft certification can be a valuable investment for your career. While it can be costly and time-consuming, the potential benefits in terms of career advancement and salary increases make it worth considering. Take the time to research and prepare for the exam, and don't be afraid to reach out to other certified professionals for support and advice.

Question and Answer

Q: What types of Microsoft certifications are available?

A: There are a variety of certifications available in different areas of expertise, including Azure, Office 365, Windows Server, and more.

Q: How long does it take to prepare for the Microsoft certification exam?

A: The time required to prepare for the exam will vary depending on your level of experience and knowledge. It's important to create a study plan that allows you to adequately prepare for the exam.

Q: Can I take the Microsoft certification exam online?

A: Yes, many Microsoft certification exams can be taken online from the comfort of your own home or office.

Q: How long is a Microsoft certification valid for?

A: Microsoft certifications are typically valid for two years, after which they must be renewed to remain current.

Conclusion of Is it Worth it to Get a Microsoft Certification

While obtaining a Microsoft certification can be expensive and time-consuming, it can also lead to career advancement opportunities and increased earning potential. By weighing the costs and benefits and properly preparing for the exam, a Microsoft certification can be a valuable investment in your future.
