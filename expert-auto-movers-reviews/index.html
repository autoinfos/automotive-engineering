Question and Answer:

Q. How much does it cost to transport a car?

A. The cost of transporting a car depends on several factors such as the distance, type of vehicle, and the time of year. On average, it can cost between $500 to $1500.

Q. How long does it take to transport a car?

A. The time it takes to transport a car depends on the distance and the location. On average, it takes between 1-2 weeks for a car to be transported across the country.

Q. Do auto movers offer door-to-door service?

A. Yes, most auto movers offer door-to-door service. This means that the auto mover will pick up your car from your home and deliver it to your new location.
