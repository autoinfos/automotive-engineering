Question and Answer:

Q: How long does an oil change take?
A: An oil change usually takes about 30 minutes to an hour, depending on the type of vehicle you have and the amount of oil needed.

Q: How often should I get my brakes checked?
A: It's recommended to get your brakes checked every 12,000 miles or at least once a year, whichever comes first.

Q: How much does it cost to replace an engine?
A: The cost to replace an engine can vary greatly depending on the make and model of your vehicle. It can range from $2,500 to $10,000 or more.

Q: Do I need an appointment for car repairs?
A: It's always best to make an appointment for car repairs to ensure that the shop has enough time to diagnose and repair your vehicle. However, some shops may offer walk-in service for minor repairs.
