Requirements to Become a Pharmacist Tech in Florida

In order to become a pharmacist tech in Florida, you'll need to complete a training program and pass a certification exam. Additionally, you'll need to pass the Pharmacy Technician Certification Exam (PTCE) offered by the Pharmacy Technician Certification Board (PTCB) or the Exam for the Certification of Pharmacy Technicians (ExCPT) offered by the National Healthcareer Association (NHA).
