What is an Unlicensed Aircraft Mechanic Job in Canada?

Unlicensed aircraft mechanic jobs in Canada refer to positions that do not require a formal license from Transport Canada. Question and Answer

Q: Are unlicensed aircraft mechanics allowed to work on all types of aircraft in Canada?

Q: Can unlicensed aircraft mechanics still advance their careers?
