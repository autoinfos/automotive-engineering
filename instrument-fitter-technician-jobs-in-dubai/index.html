Instrument fitter technician jobs in Dubai require professionals who can install, troubleshoot, repair and maintain various instruments and equipment. My Experience as an Instrument Fitter Technician in Dubai

As someone who has worked as an instrument fitter technician in Dubai for over a decade, I can say that it is a challenging yet fulfilling job. The job requires extensive knowledge of different instruments and equipment, and the ability to work under pressure in a dynamic environment.

Skills and Qualifications Required for Instrument Fitter Technician Jobs in Dubai

To succeed as an instrument fitter technician in Dubai, you need to have an in-depth understanding of different instruments and equipment, and the ability to troubleshoot and repair them. You should also have excellent critical thinking and problem-solving skills to diagnose and fix issues quickly.

Most employers in Dubai require candidates to have a technical diploma or degree in instrumentation or a related field. You should also have experience working in a similar role and a strong track record of delivering quality work.

Job Responsibilities of an Instrument Fitter Technician in Dubai

The primary responsibility of an instrument fitter technician in Dubai is to install, maintain, troubleshoot, and repair various instruments and equipment. Some of the common job responsibilities of an instrument fitter technician include:

  • Installing and maintaining instruments such as gauges, meters, and controllers
  • Diagnosing and fixing issues with instruments and equipment
  • Testing and calibrating instruments to ensure accurate readings
  • Reading and interpreting technical drawings and schematics
  • Collaborating with other technicians and engineers to complete projects

How to Find Instrument Fitter Technician Jobs in Dubai
