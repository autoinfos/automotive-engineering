How Much Does A Theatre Technologist Earn In Kenya

Written by Bardi Jul 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering a career in theatre technology in Kenya but wondering about the potential earnings? You're not alone. Many aspiring theatre technologists are curious about the earning potential in this field.

Theatre technologists are responsible for ensuring that the technical aspects of a theatre production run smoothly. They oversee lighting, sound, props, and other technical aspects of a performance. While the job can be exciting and fulfilling, it's important to understand the earning potential before pursuing a career in this field.

In Kenya, the average salary for a theatre technologist is around KES 60,000 per month. However, this can vary depending on factors such as experience, location, and the size of the theatre company. Some theatre technologists may earn as little as KES 30,000 per month, while others may earn upwards of KES 100,000 per month.

So, what does this mean for aspiring theatre technologists in Kenya? It's important to be realistic about the earning potential in this field. While salaries can vary, it's unlikely that you'll earn a six-figure income as a theatre technologist in Kenya. That being said, many people find the work fulfilling and rewarding, and the potential to earn a decent income is certainly possible.

My Experience as a Theatre Technologist in Kenya

As a theatre technologist in Kenya, I can attest to the fact that the earning potential in this field can be unpredictable. When I first started out, I was earning around KES 40,000 per month. However, as I gained more experience and honed my skills, I was able to negotiate for higher pay. Now, I earn around KES 80,000 per month, which is a comfortable salary for me.

That being said, I know other theatre technologists who earn less than I do, as well as those who earn more. It really depends on a variety of factors, including your skill level, the size of the theatre company you work for, and the location of the company.

Challenges of Earning a Decent Income as a Theatre Technologist in Kenya

One of the biggest challenges of earning a decent income as a theatre technologist in Kenya is the lack of opportunities. There are relatively few theatre companies in the country, and competition for jobs can be fierce. Additionally, many theatre companies have limited budgets, which can impact the amount they're able to pay their employees.

Another challenge is that the theatre industry in Kenya is still developing. While there are certainly talented and dedicated theatre professionals in the country, there's still a long way to go in terms of developing a thriving theatre scene. This can impact the earning potential for theatre technologists, as there may be fewer opportunities available.

The Importance of Networking in the Theatre Industry

One of the ways to overcome some of the challenges of earning a decent income as a theatre technologist in Kenya is to focus on networking. Building relationships with other theatre professionals can help you stay informed about job opportunities and can also lead to referrals for future work.

Attending theatre events and workshops is a great way to meet other theatre professionals and expand your network. Additionally, joining theatre associations and organizations can provide you with access to resources and information that can help you advance your career.

How to Negotiate for Higher Pay as a Theatre Technologist in Kenya

If you're looking to increase your earning potential as a theatre technologist in Kenya, it's important to be proactive. One way to do this is to negotiate for higher pay when you're offered a job. Research the average salary for theatre technologists in your area and come prepared with a counter-offer if you feel that the initial offer is too low.

Additionally, consider taking on freelance work to supplement your income. Many theatre technologists work on a project-by-project basis, which can provide additional income opportunities.

Conclusion of How Much Does a Theatre Technologist Earn In Kenya

While the earning potential for theatre technologists in Kenya may not be as high as some other professions, it's important to remember that job satisfaction and fulfillment can be just as important as salary. By building your skills, expanding your network, and negotiating for higher pay, you can increase your earning potential and enjoy a fulfilling career in theatre technology.
