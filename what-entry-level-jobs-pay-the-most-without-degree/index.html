What Entry Level Jobs Pay The Most Without Degree

Written by Daniel Aug 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you someone who is looking for a job without a college degree? Are you struggling to find well-paying entry-level jobs? Then, you have come to the right place. In this article, we will discuss the top entry-level jobs that pay well without requiring a college degree.

Not having a college degree can be a major roadblock in finding a high-paying job. Many people opt out of college due to various reasons such as financial constraints, lack of interest, or family responsibilities. However, not having a degree does not mean you cannot get a good-paying job.

There are several jobs that pay well without a college degree. These jobs can offer a good salary, benefits, and opportunities for growth. Here are some of the top entry-level jobs that pay well without a degree:

1. Web Developer

As a web developer, you will be responsible for designing and creating websites. The demand for web developers has increased in recent years, and it is a job that pays well. According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median annual wage for web developers was $73,760 in May 2019. Additionally, this job offers opportunities for growth and advancement.

2. Commercial Pilot

If you enjoy flying and are interested in aviation, becoming a commercial pilot might be the right choice for you. As a pilot, you will be responsible for flying planes and ensuring the safety of passengers. The median annual wage for airline and commercial pilots was $121,430 in May 2019, according to the Bureau of Labor Statistics. However, this job requires extensive training and certification.

3. Electrician

Electricians are responsible for installing and maintaining electrical systems in homes, businesses, and factories. This job requires technical skills and knowledge, and it pays well. The median annual wage for electricians was $56,180 in May 2019, according to the Bureau of Labor Statistics. Additionally, this job offers opportunities for growth and advancement.

4. Plumber

Plumbers are responsible for installing and maintaining plumbing systems in homes, businesses, and factories. This job requires technical skills and knowledge, and it pays well. The median annual wage for plumbers was $55,160 in May 2019, according to the Bureau of Labor Statistics. Additionally, this job offers opportunities for growth and advancement.

5. Real Estate Broker

As a real estate broker, you will be responsible for helping clients buy, sell, or rent properties. This job requires knowledge of the real estate market and good communication skills. The median annual wage for real estate brokers was $59,720 in May 2019, according to the Bureau of Labor Statistics. Additionally, this job offers opportunities for growth and advancement.

Question and Answer Section

Q: Do you need a college degree to become a web developer?

A: No, you do not need a college degree to become a web developer. However, you will need to have technical skills and knowledge in programming languages.

Q: What is the median annual wage for commercial pilots?

A: The median annual wage for airline and commercial pilots was $121,430 in May 2019, according to the Bureau of Labor Statistics.

Q: What skills are required to become an electrician?

A: Electricians require technical skills and knowledge in electrical systems and wiring.

Q: Is real estate brokerage a good career choice?

A: Real estate brokerage can be a good career choice for those interested in the real estate market and have good communication skills. It offers opportunities for growth and advancement.

Conclusion of What Entry Level Jobs Pay the Most Without Degree

There are several well-paying entry-level jobs that do not require a college degree. These jobs offer opportunities for growth and advancement and can be a good career choice. However, it is important to have the required skills and knowledge to excel in these jobs. Consider your interests and strengths, and choose a job that aligns with them. With hard work and dedication, you can excel in any of these entry-level jobs and achieve success.
