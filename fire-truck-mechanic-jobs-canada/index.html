Training and Qualifications for Fire Truck Mechanic Jobs Canada

To become a fire truck mechanic in Canada, you'll need to have a high school diploma or equivalent, as well as a certificate or diploma in automotive or heavy equipment mechanics. Some employers may require additional training or certifications, such as a Red Seal certification or a commercial driver's license.

It's also important to have strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail, as well as the ability to work well under pressure. Fire truck mechanics must be able to diagnose and repair issues quickly to ensure that emergency vehicles are always in top working order.

Salary and Job Outlook for Fire Truck Mechanic Jobs Canada

The salary for fire truck mechanics in Canada can vary depending on experience and location, but typically ranges from $50,000 to $80,000 per year. The job outlook for this field is positive, with a high demand for skilled technicians in the emergency vehicle maintenance industry.
