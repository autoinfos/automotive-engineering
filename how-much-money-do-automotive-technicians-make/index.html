How Much Money Do Automotive Technicians Make

Written by Luffy Oct 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering a career as an automotive technician? If so, you're probably wondering how much money you can expect to make in this profession. The good news is that automotive technicians can earn a decent living, but the amount you earn will depend on various factors.

One of the biggest pain points related to how much money do automotive technicians make is that the pay can vary widely depending on where you work, your level of experience, and the type of vehicles you work on. Additionally, some technicians are paid hourly while others are paid a salary, which can also affect how much you earn.

The average hourly wage for an automotive technician is around $20 per hour, but this can range from $12 to $30 per hour depending on the factors mentioned above. On an annual basis, the average salary for an automotive technician is around $40,000 per year, but this can range from $25,000 to $60,000 per year.

In summary, how much money you can make as an automotive technician depends on various factors, including your experience, location, and the type of vehicles you work on. However, on average, you can expect to earn around $20 per hour or $40,000 per year.

How Much Can You Earn as an Entry-Level Automotive Technician?

When I first started as an automotive technician, I was earning around $12 per hour. However, within a few years, I was able to increase my hourly wage to around $18 per hour. As an entry-level technician, you can expect to earn around $12 to $15 per hour, depending on where you work and your level of experience.

How Much Can You Earn as a Master Automotive Technician?

As a master technician with several years of experience, you can expect to earn around $25 to $30 per hour or more. However, keep in mind that becoming a master technician requires additional training and certifications, which can take time and money.

The Importance of Specializing in a Niche

If you want to earn more money as an automotive technician, consider specializing in a niche area. For example, technicians who specialize in luxury vehicles or diesel engines can earn more money than those who work on standard vehicles. Additionally, technicians who are skilled in computer diagnostics or hybrid vehicles can also earn more money.

Negotiating Your Salary

If you're not satisfied with your current salary, it's important to negotiate with your employer. Research the average salary for automotive technicians in your area and bring this information to your employer. Additionally, highlight your skills and experience and explain how they benefit the company.

Conclusion of How Much Money Do Automotive Technicians Make

In conclusion, automotive technicians can earn a decent living, but the amount you earn will depend on various factors. On average, you can expect to earn around $20 per hour or $40,000 per year, but this can vary widely depending on your experience, location, and the type of vehicles you work on. If you're considering a career as an automotive technician, it's important to research the industry and understand the earning potential before making a decision.

Question and Answer

Q: How much do automotive technicians make per hour?

A: The average hourly wage for an automotive technician is around $20 per hour, but this can range from $12 to $30 per hour depending on various factors.

Q: How much do master automotive technicians make?

A: As a master technician with several years of experience, you can expect to earn around $25 to $30 per hour or more.

Q: What factors can affect the amount of money an automotive technician earns?

A: Factors that can affect how much money an automotive technician earns include their level of experience, location, type of vehicles they work on, and whether they are paid hourly or on salary.
