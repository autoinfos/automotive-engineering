Best Auto Movers The Top Companies To Trust With Your Vehicle

Written by Christine Nov 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to moving, there are few things more stressful than figuring out how to transport your car. Whether you're moving across the country or just a few states away, finding a reliable auto moving company is crucial. From hidden fees to damaged vehicles, there are a lot of potential headaches that can come with choosing the wrong company. That's why we've done the research for you and put together a list of the best auto movers in the business.

Transporting your car can be expensive, time-consuming, and risky. It's important to choose a company that understands those pain points and works to alleviate them. The best auto movers are those that prioritize customer service, transparency, and quality of service. They should offer clear pricing and communication, as well as insurance to protect your vehicle.

So, who are the best auto movers in the business? Our top picks include Montway Auto Transport, AmeriFreight, and Ship a Car Direct. These companies have earned their reputations through years of providing excellent service to their customers.

Montway Auto Transport: The Best Option for Door-to-Door Service

When it comes to door-to-door service, Montway Auto Transport is the clear winner. They offer a wide range of services, including open and enclosed transport, expedited shipping, and even international shipping. Their customer service is top-notch, and they're known for their competitive pricing.

Personally, I've used Montway Auto Transport multiple times and have always been impressed with their professionalism and attention to detail. They kept me updated throughout the entire process and my car arrived in perfect condition.

AmeriFreight: The Best Option for Discounts

If you're looking for the best deal, AmeriFreight is the way to go. They offer a variety of discounts, including military, student, and first responder discounts. They also have a price match guarantee, so you can be sure you're getting the best price possible.

When I needed to transport my car for a move, I chose AmeriFreight and was very happy with the experience. They were able to get my car to its destination quickly and safely, all at a great price.

Ship a Car Direct: The Best Option for Protection

Ship a Car Direct is known for their emphasis on vehicle protection. They only work with carriers that meet their strict standards for safety and security, and they require carriers to have a $500,000 liability insurance policy. They also offer additional insurance options for even more protection.

While I haven't personally used Ship a Car Direct, their commitment to vehicle protection is impressive. If you're looking for added peace of mind when transporting your car, they're a great option to consider.

Tips for Choosing the Right Auto Mover

When choosing an auto mover, there are a few key factors to keep in mind. First, do your research and read reviews from previous customers. Look for companies with a track record of excellent service and customer satisfaction. Second, ask about insurance and make sure you understand what's covered and what's not. Finally, don't be afraid to ask questions and get a clear understanding of the process and pricing.

Question and Answer

Q: How far in advance should I book an auto mover?

A: It's best to book your auto mover as soon as you know your moving dates. This will give you plenty of time to research and compare companies, and ensure that your preferred company has availability.

Q: Should I choose open or enclosed transport?

A: This depends on your personal preference and the value of your vehicle. Open transport is less expensive, but exposes your car to the elements. Enclosed transport is more expensive, but provides added protection.

Q: How do I prepare my car for transport?

A: Remove all personal items from the car, make note of any existing damage, and ensure that the car is in good working condition. You may also want to consider taking photos of the car before and after transport.

Q: What should I do if my car is damaged during transport?

A: If your car is damaged during transport, contact the auto mover and your insurance company immediately. Make note of any damage and take photos for evidence.

Conclusion of Best Auto Movers

Transporting your car can be a stressful process, but choosing the right auto mover can make all the difference. By prioritizing customer service, transparency, and quality of service, you can ensure that your car arrives safely and on time. With companies like Montway Auto Transport, AmeriFreight, and Ship a Car Direct, you can trust that your vehicle is in good hands.
