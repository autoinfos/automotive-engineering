Is Aws Devops Certification Worth It

Written by Petter Aug 01, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you considering obtaining an AWS DevOps certification? With the rise of cloud computing and the increasing demand for DevOps engineers, it's no surprise that many professionals in the tech industry are asking themselves this question. In this blog post, we'll explore the benefits and drawbacks of obtaining an AWS DevOps certification and help you determine whether it's worth your time, effort, and money.

Pain Points of AWS DevOps Certification

Before we dive into the advantages and disadvantages of obtaining an AWS DevOps certification, let's first address some common pain points that professionals may face when considering this certification. For one, the cost of the certification can be a deterrent for some, as it can range from a few hundred to a few thousand dollars. Additionally, some may question whether the certification is truly necessary for their current job or future career goals.

Answering the Question

So, is AWS DevOps certification worth it? The answer depends on your individual circumstances and goals. If you're looking to advance your career in the cloud computing or DevOps fields, obtaining an AWS DevOps certification can certainly boost your credibility and marketability. It shows that you have a deep understanding of AWS tools and services, as well as the ability to implement DevOps practices in an AWS environment.

However, if you're content with your current job or not interested in pursuing a career in the cloud computing or DevOps fields, the certification may not be necessary. It's important to weigh the potential benefits against the cost and time commitment of obtaining the certification.

Main Points to Consider

Some key points to consider when deciding whether to pursue an AWS DevOps certification include:

  • The potential for career advancement and increased earning potential
  • The cost and time commitment required to obtain the certification
  • The relevance of the certification to your current job or future career goals

Personal Experience with AWS DevOps Certification

As a DevOps engineer who recently obtained an AWS DevOps certification, I can attest to the benefits of obtaining this certification. Not only did it increase my marketability to potential employers, but it also deepened my understanding of AWS tools and services, allowing me to better implement DevOps practices in an AWS environment.

Increased Credibility and Marketability

One of the most significant benefits of obtaining an AWS DevOps certification is the increased credibility and marketability it provides. Employers are always on the lookout for candidates with specialized skills and certifications, and an AWS DevOps certification can certainly set you apart from other candidates.

Deep Understanding of AWS Tools and Services

Another benefit of obtaining an AWS DevOps certification is the deep understanding of AWS tools and services that it provides. The certification covers a wide range of topics, including AWS CloudFormation, AWS Elastic Beanstalk, and AWS OpsWorks, among others. This knowledge can be invaluable for DevOps engineers who work in an AWS environment.

How to Obtain an AWS DevOps Certification

If you've decided that obtaining an AWS DevOps certification is worth it for you, the next step is to prepare for the certification exam. AWS offers a variety of resources to help you study, including online courses, whitepapers, and practice exams. It's important to dedicate enough time to studying and practicing before taking the exam to ensure the best chance of success.

Personal Experience Continued

Since obtaining my AWS DevOps certification, I've noticed an increase in job opportunities and higher salary offers. The certification has definitely been worth it for me, but it's important to carefully consider your own goals and circumstances before making the investment.

Question and Answer

Here are some common questions and answers related to AWS DevOps certification:

Q: Is AWS DevOps certification difficult to obtain?

A: The difficulty level of the certification exam can vary depending on your experience and knowledge of AWS tools and services. However, with enough preparation and practice, most professionals can obtain the certification.

Q: How long does it take to obtain an AWS DevOps certification?

A: The time it takes to obtain the certification can vary depending on your study habits and schedule. However, most professionals can obtain the certification within a few months of dedicated studying.

Q: Can obtaining an AWS DevOps certification lead to higher earning potential?

A: Yes, obtaining an AWS DevOps certification can lead to higher earning potential. Employers are often willing to pay a premium for candidates with specialized skills and certifications.

Q: Is AWS DevOps certification worth it for someone who is not in the tech industry?

A: It depends on your individual goals and circumstances. If you're interested in pursuing a career in the tech industry, obtaining an AWS DevOps certification can certainly provide a competitive edge. However, if you're not interested in the tech industry, the certification may not be necessary.

Conclusion of AWS DevOps Certification

Overall, whether or not AWS DevOps certification is worth it depends on your individual circumstances and goals. If you're looking to advance your career in the cloud computing or DevOps fields, obtaining the certification can certainly boost your credibility and marketability. However, if you're content with your current job or not interested in pursuing a career in these fields, the certification may not be necessary. It's important to carefully consider the potential benefits and drawbacks before making the investment.
