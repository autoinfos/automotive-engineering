The Truth About Diploma Mechanical Engineer Fresher Salary In Dubai

Are you a diploma mechanical engineer fresher looking for a job in Dubai? You may have heard mixed opinions about the salary prospects for this career path. Some say the pay is good, while others say it's not worth the effort. In this post, we'll explore the truth about diploma mechanical engineer fresher salary in Dubai.

First, let's address the pain points. As a fresher, it can be difficult to find a job in Dubai, let alone one that pays well. With the influx of qualified candidates, the competition is fierce. Additionally, many companies prefer to hire experienced engineers, which can make finding a job as a fresher even more challenging.

So, what is the target salary for diploma mechanical engineer freshers in Dubai? According to salary comparison sites, the average salary for a fresher in this field is around AED 60,000 per year. However, this can vary depending on factors such as the company, industry, and your level of experience.

To summarize, while it may be challenging to find a job as a diploma mechanical engineer fresher in Dubai, the average salary is around AED 60,000 per year. Keep in mind that your salary may vary depending on various factors.

The Reality of Diploma Mechanical Engineer Fresher Salary in Dubai

When I first graduated with my diploma in mechanical engineering, I was excited to start my career in Dubai. However, I quickly realized how tough it was to find a job as a fresher. I applied to various companies but didn't receive many responses. Eventually, I landed a job at a small engineering firm that paid me AED 50,000 per year.

While the pay was lower than I expected, I was grateful for the opportunity to gain experience in the field. After a year, I applied to a larger company and was offered a job that paid AED 70,000 per year, which was more in line with my expectations.

Tips for Finding a High-Paying Job as a Diploma Mechanical Engineer Fresher in Dubai

If you're a diploma mechanical engineer fresher looking for a high-paying job in Dubai, here are some tips that may help:

1. Network

Attend industry events and connect with professionals in your field. This can help you learn about job opportunities and gain valuable contacts.

2. Gain Experience

Consider internships or part-time jobs to gain experience in your field. This can make you a more competitive candidate when applying for full-time positions.

The Importance of Negotiating Your Salary

When you receive a job offer, don't be afraid to negotiate your salary. Research the average pay for your position and industry, and use this information to make a case for a higher salary. Remember, the worst they can say is no.

My Experience with Negotiating My Salary

When I received my job offer for AED 70,000 per year, I was hesitant to negotiate my salary. However, after researching the average pay for my industry, I realized that I was being offered a lower-than-average salary. I spoke with my employer and was able to negotiate a higher salary of AED 85,000 per year.

Question and Answer

A: It can take anywhere from a few weeks to several months to find a job as a fresher in this field. It's important to be persistent and keep applying to various companies.

Q: What qualifications do I need to become a diploma mechanical engineer in Dubai?

A: To become a diploma mechanical engineer in Dubai, you typically need a diploma or degree in mechanical engineering, as well as relevant work experience.

Conclusion of Diploma Mechanical Engineer Fresher Salary in Dubai

While finding a job as a diploma mechanical engineer fresher in Dubai can be challenging, the average salary is around AED 60,000 per year. By gaining experience, networking, and negotiating your salary, you can increase your pay prospects and build a successful career in this field.
