Question and Answer

Q. How do I know which battery is right for my car?

A. You can consult your car's manual or speak to a professional mechanic to determine the right battery for your car based on its size, power requirements, and other specifications.

Q. How often should I replace my car battery?

A. It is recommended to replace your car battery every 3-5 years, depending on its usage, condition, and maintenance.

Q. What are the signs that my car battery needs to be replaced?

A. Some signs that your car battery needs to be replaced include slow engine cranking, dimming headlights, and frequent dead batteries.

Q. How can I extend the life of my car battery?

A. You can extend the life of your car battery by avoiding extreme temperatures, minimizing short trips, keeping it clean and dry, and ensuring proper maintenance.
