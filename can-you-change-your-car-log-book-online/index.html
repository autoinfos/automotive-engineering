Can You Change Your Car Log Book Online

Written by Robby Aug 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of the hassle of visiting a physical office to change your car log book? Do you wish you could do it online from the comfort of your home? Well, the good news is that you can now change your car log book online. This article will delve deeper into the topic and provide you with all the information you need to know.

Changing your car log book can be a tedious and time-consuming process. It often involves visiting a DVLA office or sending your log book through the post, which can take weeks to process. This can be frustrating, especially if you need to use your car urgently or you have just purchased a new one.

Fortunately, you can now change your car log book online. The Driver and Vehicle Licensing Agency (DVLA) has launched an online service that allows car owners to change their log book details quickly and easily over the internet.

In summary, changing your car log book online is a straightforward and hassle-free process that can save you time and effort. With the DVLA's online service, you can change your log book details from the comfort of your home without having to visit a physical office or send your log book through the post.

How to Change Your Car Log Book Online

First, you need to visit the DVLA's online service and log in using your Government Gateway ID. If you do not have one, you can create an account using your personal details and a valid email address.

Next, you will need to provide some basic information about your car, including its make, model, registration number, and current log book details. You will also need to upload a digital copy of your V5C certificate or your new keeper supplement if you have just purchased the car.

Once you have provided all the necessary information, you can submit your application and pay the fee online using a debit or credit card. The fee is currently £25 for standard applications and £55 for priority applications.

You will receive a confirmation email from the DVLA once your application has been received. It can take up to five working days for your new log book to arrive, so make sure you keep your old one until your new one arrives.

Benefits of Changing Your Car Log Book Online

Changing your car log book online comes with several benefits. Firstly, it saves you time and effort as you can do it from the comfort of your home without having to visit a physical office or send your log book through the post. Secondly, it is a more convenient option if you need to change your log book details urgently. Thirdly, it is a safer option as you do not need to worry about your log book getting lost in the post or stolen.

Question and Answer

Q: How long does it take to change your car log book online?

A: It can take up to five working days for your new log book to arrive once you have submitted your application online.

Q: How much does it cost to change your car log book online?

A: The fee is currently £25 for standard applications and £55 for priority applications.

Q: Do you need to send your old log book when you change it online?

A: No, you do not need to send your old log book when you change it online. You should keep your old log book until your new one arrives.

Q: Is it safe to change your car log book online?

A: Yes, it is safe to change your car log book online. The DVLA's online service is secure and encrypted, and all your personal information is protected.

Conclusion of Can You Change Your Car Log Book Online

Changing your car log book online is a convenient and hassle-free option that can save you time and effort. With the DVLA's online service, you can change your log book details from the comfort of your home without having to visit a physical office or send your log book through the post. It is a safe and secure option that comes with several benefits, including convenience, speed, and safety.
