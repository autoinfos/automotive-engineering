How Much Does Mechanical Engineer Make In Canada A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Oct 14, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you considering a career in mechanical engineering and wondering how much you could potentially make in Canada? The answer is quite complex and depends on several factors such as experience, industry, and location. In this post, we will explore the average salary of mechanical engineers in Canada and provide you with a comprehensive guide to help you make an informed decision.

As a mechanical engineer, it can be challenging to negotiate a salary that meets your expectations. Some of the pain points that many mechanical engineers face in Canada include low starting salaries, lack of benefits, and limited opportunities for career advancement. However, with the right skills, education, and experience, mechanical engineers can earn a lucrative salary and enjoy a fulfilling career.

So, how much does a mechanical engineer make in Canada? According to the latest data from PayScale, the average salary for mechanical engineers in Canada is CAD 65,000 per year. However, this figure can vary widely depending on several factors such as years of experience, industry, and location.

In general, mechanical engineers with less than five years of experience can expect to earn an average salary of CAD 55,000 per year. Those with five to ten years of experience can earn an average of CAD 75,000 per year, while those with over ten years of experience can earn an average of CAD 100,000 per year.

What Factors Affect the Salary of Mechanical Engineers in Canada?

Several factors can affect the salary of mechanical engineers in Canada, including:

Industry

The industry you work in can have a significant impact on your salary as a mechanical engineer. For instance, those working in the oil and gas industry tend to earn higher salaries than those working in the manufacturing industry.

Location

Your location within Canada can also affect your salary as a mechanical engineer. For instance, those working in major cities such as Toronto, Vancouver, and Montreal tend to earn higher salaries than those working in smaller cities or rural areas.

Education and Certification

Having advanced degrees or certifications can help you earn a higher salary as a mechanical engineer in Canada. For instance, those with a Master's degree in mechanical engineering tend to earn more than those with a Bachelor's degree. Additionally, having certifications in areas such as project management or Six Sigma can also help you earn a higher salary.

Experience

As mentioned earlier, experience is a significant factor in determining the salary of mechanical engineers in Canada. Generally, those with more experience tend to earn higher salaries than those just starting in the field.

Personal Experience: How Much I Earn as a Mechanical Engineer in Canada

As a mechanical engineer with over ten years of experience working in the manufacturing industry in Toronto, I can attest that the average salary for mechanical engineers in Canada is accurate. When I started in the industry, my starting salary was around CAD 50,000 per year. However, as I gained experience and took on more responsibilities, my salary increased to an average of CAD 100,000 per year.

How to Negotiate a Higher Salary as a Mechanical Engineer in Canada

If you're looking to negotiate a higher salary as a mechanical engineer in Canada, there are several things you can do:

1. Research Salary Trends

Before negotiating your salary, it's important to research salary trends for mechanical engineers in your industry and location. This can help you determine a reasonable salary range to negotiate from.

2. Highlight Your Skills and Experience

During the negotiation process, be sure to highlight your skills and experience and how they can benefit the company. This can help justify a higher salary.

3. Consider Additional Benefits

If the company is unable to offer you a higher salary, consider asking for additional benefits such as more vacation time, flexible work hours, or a bonus structure.

Conclusion of How Much Does Mechanical Engineer Make in Canada

While the average salary for mechanical engineers in Canada may not be as high as some other professions, it's important to remember that with experience, education, and skills, mechanical engineers can earn a lucrative salary and enjoy a fulfilling career.
