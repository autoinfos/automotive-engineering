What Will You Learn in Auto Glass Technician Training Ontario?

In auto glass technician training in Ontario, you will learn a wide range of skills and knowledge related to the installation, repair, and replacement of automotive glass. Some of the topics covered in the program include:

Types of Glass:

You will learn about the different types of automotive glass, including laminated, tempered, and specialty glass, as well as their unique properties and uses. This knowledge will allow you to choose the right type of glass for each vehicle and situation.

Adhesives:

You will learn about the different types of adhesives used in the industry, as well as their characteristics and applications. This knowledge will enable you to select the appropriate adhesive for each job and ensure a secure and long-lasting bond.

How to Gain Practical Experience in Auto Glass Technician Training Ontario?

These programs allow you to work alongside experienced professionals in the industry and apply your knowledge and skills in a real-world setting. You can also gain experience by working part-time or volunteering at an auto glass shop or dealership.
