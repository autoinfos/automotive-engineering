The Truth About Diesel Motor Mechanic Salary In New Zealand

Written by Aprilia Aug 02, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you considering a career as a diesel motor mechanic in New Zealand? While it can be a fulfilling and rewarding career, it's important to understand the realities of the job, especially when it comes to salary.

The truth is, diesel motor mechanic salaries in New Zealand can vary greatly depending on a number of factors, including location, experience, and qualifications. It's not uncommon for entry-level mechanics to earn around $40,000 per year, while experienced mechanics can earn over $80,000 per year.

So, what can you do to increase your earning potential as a diesel motor mechanic in New Zealand? Here are a few tips:

1. Get Certified

One way to increase your salary potential as a diesel motor mechanic is to get certified. Certifications can demonstrate to potential employers that you have the skills and knowledge necessary to perform the job at a high level. In New Zealand, there are a number of certifications available for diesel motor mechanics, including the National Certificate in Motor Industry (Automotive Electrical and Mechanical Engineering) and the National Certificate in Motor Industry (Automotive Heavy Engineering).

2. Consider Specializing

Another way to increase your earning potential as a diesel motor mechanic is to consider specializing in a particular area. For example, you could focus on working with heavy machinery or specialize in a particular brand of diesel engine. By developing expertise in a particular area, you can set yourself apart from other mechanics and potentially command a higher salary.

3. Look for Opportunities in High-Demand Areas

If you're willing to relocate, you may be able to find higher-paying opportunities in areas where there is a high demand for diesel motor mechanics. For example, areas with a large mining or agricultural industry may offer higher salaries for diesel motor mechanics due to the demand for heavy machinery repairs.

4. Negotiate Your Salary

Finally, don't be afraid to negotiate your salary when starting a new job or asking for a raise. Do your research on average salaries for diesel motor mechanics in your area and use that information to make a case for why you deserve a higher salary.

Personal Experience

As a diesel motor mechanic for the past 10 years, I've found that specializing in a particular area has been the most effective way to increase my earning potential. By becoming an expert in repairing and maintaining heavy machinery, I've been able to command a higher salary than other mechanics with less experience in this area.

Job Outlook

The job outlook for diesel motor mechanics in New Zealand is positive. According to the Ministry of Business, Innovation and Employment, there is a high demand for diesel motor mechanics due to the country's reliance on heavy machinery in industries such as agriculture, forestry, and mining. This means that there will likely be plenty of job opportunities for qualified mechanics in the coming years.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I earn a high salary as an entry-level diesel motor mechanic?

A: It's unlikely that you'll earn a high salary as an entry-level diesel motor mechanic. However, with experience and additional certifications, you can increase your earning potential.

Q: Are there any areas of New Zealand where diesel motor mechanics earn higher salaries?

A: Salaries for diesel motor mechanics can vary depending on location. Areas with a high demand for heavy machinery repairs, such as mining or agricultural regions, may offer higher salaries.

Q: How long does it take to become a certified diesel motor mechanic in New Zealand?

A: The length of time it takes to become certified as a diesel motor mechanic in New Zealand can vary depending on the program you choose. Some programs can be completed in as little as six months, while others may take several years.

Q: Is it possible to negotiate my salary as a diesel motor mechanic?

A: Yes, it's always worth negotiating your salary when starting a new job or asking for a raise. Do your research on average salaries in your area and use that information to make a case for why you deserve a higher salary.

Conclusion of Diesel Motor Mechanic Salary in New Zealand

While diesel motor mechanic salaries in New Zealand may not be as high as some other professions, there are plenty of opportunities to increase your earning potential through experience, certifications, and specialization. By following these tips, you can set yourself up for a rewarding and financially stable career as a diesel motor mechanic in New Zealand.
