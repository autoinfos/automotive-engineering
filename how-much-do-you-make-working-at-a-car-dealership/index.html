How Much Do You Make Working At A Car Dealership

Written by Christine Sep 06, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you considering a job at a car dealership but wondering how much you can expect to make? This is a common concern for many people, especially those who are just starting out in the industry. While there is no one-size-fits-all answer to this question, there are some important things to consider when it comes to how much you can expect to earn at a car dealership.

One of the biggest pain points when it comes to working at a car dealership is the unpredictable nature of the industry. Sales can fluctuate greatly from month to month, and even from day to day. As a result, your income can also fluctuate greatly. This can make it difficult to plan your finances and make it challenging to manage your budget.

So, how much can you expect to make working at a car dealership? The answer to this question depends on a number of factors, including your role at the dealership, your experience level, and the location of the dealership. Generally speaking, salespeople can earn anywhere from minimum wage to six figures, depending on their performance and the dealership they work for. Service technicians and mechanics can also earn a decent salary, with some earning over $50,000 a year.

In summary, how much you can expect to make at a car dealership depends on a number of factors. While there is no one-size-fits-all answer to this question, it is possible to earn a decent income in the industry if you are willing to work hard and stay focused on your goals.

What to Expect as a Salesperson at a Car Dealership

As a former car salesperson, I can tell you from personal experience that the income potential at a car dealership can be quite high. However, this is largely dependent on your ability to sell cars and meet your sales goals. In my experience, the most successful salespeople at the dealership were those who were able to build strong relationships with customers and consistently hit their sales targets.

One thing to keep in mind is that your income as a salesperson will likely be heavily dependent on commissions. This means that your income will fluctuate based on your sales performance. If you are able to consistently sell a high volume of cars, you can expect to earn a higher income than someone who is struggling to meet their sales goals.

What to Expect as a Service Technician at a Car Dealership

As a service technician at a car dealership, your income will be less dependent on commissions and more dependent on your experience level and skillset. In general, service technicians can expect to earn a decent salary, with some earning over $50,000 a year. However, this is also dependent on the location of the dealership and the demand for service technicians in that area.

One thing to keep in mind is that working as a service technician can be physically demanding, as you will be required to lift heavy objects and spend a lot of time on your feet. However, if you have a passion for cars and are skilled in diagnosing and repairing issues, this can be a rewarding and lucrative career choice.

Factors That Affect Your Income at a Car Dealership

When it comes to determining how much you can expect to make at a car dealership, there are a number of factors to consider. These include:

  • Your role at the dealership
  • Your experience level
  • The location of the dealership
  • The demand for your role in that area
  • Your ability to sell or perform services

By taking these factors into account, you can get a better idea of what to expect when it comes to your income at a car dealership.

Tips for Maximizing Your Income at a Car Dealership

If you are looking to maximize your income at a car dealership, there are several things you can do. These include:

  • Building strong relationships with customers
  • Staying up-to-date on the latest trends and technologies in the industry
  • Consistently hitting your sales or service goals
  • Providing exceptional customer service
  • Continuing to develop your skills and knowledge through training and education

By following these tips, you can increase your chances of earning a higher income at a car dealership.

Question and Answer

Q: How much do salespeople typically make at a car dealership?

A: Salespeople can earn anywhere from minimum wage to six figures, depending on their performance and the dealership they work for.

Q: How much do service technicians typically make at a car dealership?

A: Service technicians can expect to earn a decent salary, with some earning over $50,000 a year, depending on their experience level and the demand for service technicians in their area.

Q: What factors affect your income at a car dealership?

A: Your income at a car dealership is affected by your role at the dealership, your experience level, the location of the dealership, the demand for your role in that area, and your ability to sell or perform services.

Q: How can you maximize your income at a car dealership?

A: You can maximize your income at a car dealership by building strong relationships with customers, staying up-to-date on the latest trends and technologies in the industry, consistently hitting your sales or service goals, providing exceptional customer service, and continuing to develop your skills and knowledge through training and education.

Conclusion of How Much Do You Make Working at a Car Dealership

While there is no one-size-fits-all answer to how much you can expect to make at a car dealership, there are several factors that can influence your income. By understanding these factors and taking steps to maximize your income potential, you can create a successful and rewarding career in the automotive industry.
