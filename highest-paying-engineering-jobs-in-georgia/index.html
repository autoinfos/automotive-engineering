Software Engineering

Software engineering is one of the most in-demand engineering jobs in Georgia. Many companies, such as The Home Depot and Coca-Cola, are investing heavily in software development and need talented engineers to build and maintain their digital platforms. As a software engineer in Georgia, you can expect to earn an average salary of $96,000 per year, according to Glassdoor.

Electrical Engineering

Electrical engineering is another lucrative field in Georgia. Companies such as Georgia Power and Gulfstream Aerospace need electrical engineers to design and maintain their electrical systems. As an electrical engineer in Georgia, you can expect to earn an average salary of $87,000 per year, according to Glassdoor.

Aerospace Engineering

Georgia is home to many aerospace engineering companies, including Lockheed Martin and Delta Air Lines. These companies need engineers to design and test aircraft and other aerospace products. As an aerospace engineer in Georgia, you can expect to earn an average salary of $91,000 per year, according to Glassdoor.

Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical engineering is another high-paying field in Georgia. Companies such as Caterpillar and The Coca-Cola Company need mechanical engineers to design and maintain their products and facilities. As a mechanical engineer in Georgia, you can expect to earn an average salary of $81,000 per year, according to Glassdoor.
