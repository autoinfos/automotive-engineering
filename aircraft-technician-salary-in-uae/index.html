The average salary for an aircraft technician in the UAE is around AED 120,000 per year. However, this can vary depending on experience, qualifications, and the company you work for. Additionally, some companies offer benefits such as health insurance, paid time off, and retirement plans.

Factors that Affect Aircraft Technician Salary in UAE

Several factors can affect aircraft technician salary in UAE. These include:
