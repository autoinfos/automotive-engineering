How Much Does A Computer Support Specialist Make An Hour

Written by Luffy Jun 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Have you ever wondered how much a computer support specialist makes an hour? If you're thinking about pursuing a career in this field, or if you're just curious, then keep reading to find out more.

One of the main pain points for those interested in the field of computer support is the uncertainty about pay. It can be difficult to know what salary to expect, especially when starting out.

According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median hourly wage for computer support specialists is $24.36 per hour. This means that half of all computer support specialists earn more than this amount, while the other half earns less.

In summary, the hourly wage for computer support specialists is around $24.36. However, there are many factors that can affect this amount, including experience, education level, and location.

What Affects the Hourly Wage of a Computer Support Specialist?

When it comes to determining the hourly wage of a computer support specialist, there are several factors to consider. One of the biggest factors is experience. Those with more experience in the field are likely to earn more than those who are just starting out.

Education level is another important factor. Those with a bachelor's degree in computer science or a related field may be able to earn a higher hourly wage than those with just a high school diploma or an associate's degree.

Location can also play a role in determining the hourly wage of a computer support specialist. Those working in large cities or areas with a high cost of living may be able to earn more than those working in rural areas or smaller towns.

My Personal Experience as a Computer Support Specialist

As a computer support specialist with over five years of experience in the field, I can attest to the fact that experience and education level do have a significant impact on hourly wages. When I first started out, I was earning around $18 per hour. However, as I gained more experience and completed additional training courses, my hourly wage increased to over $30 per hour.

How to Increase Your Hourly Wage as a Computer Support Specialist

If you're interested in increasing your hourly wage as a computer support specialist, there are several steps you can take. One of the most important is to gain as much experience as possible in the field. This can be done by taking on new projects, working on a variety of different systems, and seeking out additional training opportunities.

Another way to increase your hourly wage is to pursue additional education. This could mean earning a bachelor's degree in computer science or a related field, or pursuing industry certifications that demonstrate your knowledge and expertise.

What Other Jobs Are Related to Computer Support?

There are several other jobs that are related to computer support, including network administrators, IT managers, and systems analysts. These jobs typically require more advanced education and experience, and may come with higher hourly wages.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the entry-level hourly wage for computer support specialists?

A: The entry-level hourly wage for computer support specialists is typically around $15 per hour.

Q: How much can computer support specialists earn with experience?

A: Computer support specialists with several years of experience can earn upwards of $30 per hour.

Q: What education is required to become a computer support specialist?

A: While a high school diploma may be sufficient for some computer support specialist jobs, many employers prefer candidates with an associate's degree or higher in computer science or a related field.

Q: Are there any certifications that can help increase a computer support specialist's hourly wage?

A: Yes, there are several industry certifications that can help demonstrate a computer support specialist's knowledge and expertise, including CompTIA A+, Network+, and Security+ certifications.

Conclusion of How Much Does a Computer Support Specialist Make an Hour

Overall, the hourly wage for computer support specialists is around $24.36 per hour. However, there are several factors that can affect this amount, including experience, education level, and location. By gaining experience, pursuing additional education, and seeking out industry certifications, computer support specialists can increase their hourly wage and advance their careers.
