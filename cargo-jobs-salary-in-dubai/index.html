The Truth About Cargo Jobs Salary In Dubai What You Need To Know

Written by Frank Jun 17, 2023 · 4 min read

Have you been looking for information about cargo jobs salary in Dubai? Are you curious about how much you could earn in this field? Look no further, as we have gathered all the information you need to know about cargo jobs salary in Dubai.

One of the biggest concerns for anyone considering a career in the cargo industry is the salary. It's important to know what you can expect to earn before committing to a job. The good news is that cargo jobs in Dubai offer competitive salaries, but there are a few things you need to keep in mind.

Firstly, the salary for cargo jobs in Dubai depends on the position and the experience of the candidate. Secondly, it's important to note that the cost of living in Dubai is higher than many other cities, so the salary may not go as far as it would in other places. However, Dubai does not have income tax, which means you get to keep your entire salary.

In summary, cargo jobs in Dubai offer competitive salaries based on the position and experience of the candidate. However, the cost of living in Dubai is higher than other cities, so it's important to keep that in mind when considering a job offer.

What is the Average Salary for Cargo Jobs in Dubai?

When it comes to cargo jobs in Dubai, the salary varies depending on the position and experience. Generally, entry-level cargo jobs in Dubai pay around AED 3,000 to AED 5,000 per month, while more experienced professionals can earn up to AED 20,000 per month or more.

Personally, I have been working in the cargo industry in Dubai for over 5 years and have seen my salary increase significantly based on my experience and position. I started as a cargo handler earning AED 3,500 per month and now make over AED 12,000 per month as a cargo supervisor.

What are the Highest Paying Cargo Jobs in Dubai?

The highest paying cargo jobs in Dubai are typically management positions such as cargo managers, warehouse managers and logistics managers. These positions require a high level of experience and expertise, but they also offer higher salaries.

Personally, I have seen cargo managers in Dubai earning up to AED 35,000 per month or more. However, it's important to note that these positions are competitive and require a proven track record of success in the industry.

How to Increase Your Salary in a Cargo Job in Dubai?

If you're looking to increase your salary in a cargo job in Dubai, there are a few things you can do. Firstly, gaining more experience and taking on additional responsibilities in your current position can lead to promotions and salary increases. Secondly, pursuing additional education and certifications can also increase your value to employers and lead to higher salaries.

How to Negotiate Your Salary for a Cargo Job in Dubai?

If you're considering a job offer in the cargo industry in Dubai, it's important to negotiate your salary to ensure you're getting paid what you're worth. Firstly, do your research and find out what the average salary is for your position and experience level. Secondly, be confident and clear in your negotiations, and don't be afraid to ask for what you're worth.

Is a Career in Cargo Worth it in Dubai?

As someone who has been working in the cargo industry in Dubai for over 5 years, I can say that it's definitely worth it. The industry is growing rapidly in Dubai, and there are many opportunities for career growth and advancement. Additionally, the salaries are competitive and the work is rewarding.
