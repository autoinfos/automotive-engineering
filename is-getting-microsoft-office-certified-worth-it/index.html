Is Getting Microsoft Office Certified Worth It

Written by Luffy Nov 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering getting Microsoft Office certified? Are you wondering if it's worth the time, effort, and money? In this article, we'll explore the benefits of Microsoft Office certification and help you determine if it's the right decision for you.

Using Microsoft Office is a crucial skill in today's job market. However, many people lack the necessary knowledge and skills to use this software effectively. This can lead to frustration, errors, and wasted time. Additionally, employers are looking for candidates who have a solid understanding of Microsoft Office, and being certified can give you an edge over other applicants.

The short answer is yes, getting Microsoft Office certified is worth it. Certification can enhance your skills, improve your job prospects, and increase your earning potential. It can also give you a sense of accomplishment and boost your confidence.

Microsoft Office certification demonstrates your proficiency in one or more of the Microsoft Office applications, such as Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, and Access. Certification is available at different levels, from beginner to advanced, and can be obtained through online courses, training programs, or exams.

My Personal Experience with Microsoft Office Certification

When I was applying for a job as an administrative assistant, I noticed that most of the job postings required Microsoft Office skills. Although I had used Microsoft Office before, I wasn't confident in my abilities. I decided to take an online course and get certified in Word and Excel.

The online course was comprehensive and easy to follow. I learned many new features and shortcuts that I had never used before. After completing the course, I took the certification exams and passed with flying colors. When I applied for the administrative assistant job, I included my certifications on my resume and cover letter.

During the interview, the hiring manager was impressed with my certifications and asked me about my experience with Microsoft Office. I was able to answer confidently and demonstrate my knowledge of the software. I got the job and have been using Microsoft Office on a daily basis. I feel more confident and efficient in my work, thanks to my certifications.

The Benefits of Microsoft Office Certification

Here are some of the benefits of getting Microsoft Office certified:

Enhanced Skills

Microsoft Office certification can improve your skills and help you become more proficient in using the software. You'll learn new features, shortcuts, and best practices that can save you time and make you more productive. You'll also be able to troubleshoot issues and solve problems more effectively.

Better Job Prospects

Employers are looking for candidates who have a solid understanding of Microsoft Office. Being certified can give you an edge over other applicants and increase your chances of getting hired. Additionally, certification can open up new job opportunities that require Microsoft Office skills.

How to Get Microsoft Office Certified

Here are the steps to get Microsoft Office certified:

Choose Your Certification

Decide which Microsoft Office application you want to get certified in. You can choose from Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, Access, and more.

Take a Course or Training Program

You can take an online course or training program to learn the skills and knowledge required for certification. These programs are usually self-paced and can be completed from anywhere.

Conclusion of Is Getting Microsoft Office Certified Worth It?

Getting Microsoft Office certified is definitely worth it. Certification can enhance your skills, improve your job prospects, and increase your earning potential. It can also give you a sense of accomplishment and boost your confidence. If you're considering getting certified, take the first step and choose your certification today.

Question and Answer

Q: How long does it take to get Microsoft Office certified?

A: The duration depends on the certification you choose and the training program you take. Some programs can be completed in a few weeks, while others may take several months.

Q: Is Microsoft Office certification expensive?

A: The cost of certification varies depending on the level of certification, the training program, and the exam. However, the cost is usually reasonable and can be a worthwhile investment in your career.

Q: Can I get Microsoft Office certified online?

A: Yes, you can take online courses and exams to get Microsoft Office certified. Online programs are flexible and can be completed from anywhere.

Q: Do I need to renew my Microsoft Office certification?

A: Yes, Microsoft Office certification is valid for a certain period, usually two years. To maintain your certification, you'll need to renew it by taking a recertification exam or completing a certain number of continuing education units.
