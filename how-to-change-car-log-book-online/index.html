How To Change Your Car Log Book Online A Stress Free Guide

Written by Oliver Oct 02, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of the long queues and endless paperwork involved in changing your car log book? Do you wish there was an easier way to get it done? Well, good news – you can now change your car log book online! In this article, we’ll show you how to change your car log book online in a stress-free manner.

Anyone who has ever tried to change their car log book knows the frustration of the process. First, you have to fill out a bunch of forms, then you need to wait in long queues just to submit them. And even when you finally submit the forms, you may still have to wait for weeks or even months to get your new log book in the mail. It’s no wonder most people dread this process!

The good news is that you can now change your car log book online, and it’s a lot easier than you might think. Here’s what you need to do:

First, go to the DVLA website and navigate to the “Change Vehicle Details” section. From there, you will be prompted to enter your vehicle registration number and the 11-digit reference number found on your V5C registration certificate. Once you’ve entered this information, you’ll be taken to a page where you can update your vehicle details, including your log book. Simply follow the prompts to complete the process, and voila – you’re done!

In summary, changing your car log book online is now easier than ever. Simply go to the DVLA website, enter your vehicle details, and follow the prompts to update your log book. No more long queues or endless paperwork – just a stress-free process that can be completed from the comfort of your own home.

Why Change Car Log Book Online?

Changing your car log book online has many benefits. For starters, it’s a lot faster than the traditional paper-based process. Instead of waiting in long queues and filling out endless forms, you can update your log book in just a few clicks. Additionally, changing your log book online is more convenient, as you can complete the process from the comfort of your own home. And finally, it’s a lot less stressful – no more worrying about lost forms or delayed mail!

Step-by-Step Guide:

Step 1: Go to the DVLA website

The first step in changing your car log book online is to go to the DVLA website. From there, navigate to the “Change Vehicle Details” section.

Step 2: Enter your vehicle details

Once you’re in the “Change Vehicle Details” section, you’ll be prompted to enter your vehicle registration number and the 11-digit reference number found on your V5C registration certificate. Make sure to double-check the information you enter to avoid any errors.

Step 3: Update your log book

After you’ve entered your vehicle details, you’ll be taken to a page where you can update your log book. Simply follow the prompts to complete the process, and you’re done!

Tips:

Make sure to double-check the information you enter to avoid any errors. If you do make a mistake, you may have to start the process over again.

FAQ:

Q: How long does it take to change my car log book online?

A: The process typically takes less than 10 minutes to complete.

Q: What information do I need to change my car log book online?

A: You’ll need your vehicle registration number and the 11-digit reference number found on your V5C registration certificate.

Q: Do I need to send in my old log book?

A: No, you can keep your old log book. The DVLA will automatically update their records with your new information.

Q: How will I receive my new log book?
