Are you considering a career as a CAD drafter but unsure about the salary per hour? You're not alone. Many people are curious about the earning potential in this field.

The salary of a CAD drafter can vary depending on several factors such as experience, location, and industry. However, some common pain points are the lack of information about salary expectations and the fear of being underpaid.

According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median hourly wage for a CAD drafter in 2020 was $28.47. This means that half of the workers in this field earned more than this amount, while the other half earned less. The lowest 10 percent earned less than $17.25 per hour, while the highest 10 percent earned more than $51.04 per hour.

In conclusion, the salary of a CAD drafter can be competitive and rewarding. It is important to research and negotiate your salary based on your experience and industry standards.

How to Negotiate Your Salary as a CAD Drafter?
