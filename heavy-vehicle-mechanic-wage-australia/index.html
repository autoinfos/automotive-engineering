Heavy Vehicle Mechanic Wage Australia A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Jul 25, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you considering a career as a heavy vehicle mechanic in Australia? If so, you may be wondering about the potential earnings in this field. The reality is that the heavy vehicle mechanic wage in Australia can vary depending on several factors, including location, experience, and industry demand. In this article, we will explore the topic of heavy vehicle mechanic wage Australia in detail, so you can make an informed decision about your career path.

One of the main pain points related to heavy vehicle mechanic wage in Australia is the perception that it is a low-paying profession. While it is true that some entry-level positions may offer lower salaries, experienced heavy vehicle mechanics can earn a comfortable living wage. In addition, this profession offers stability and job security, as the demand for skilled mechanics is always high.

So, how much can you expect to earn as a heavy vehicle mechanic in Australia? According to the latest data from PayScale, the average salary for this profession is around AU$65,000 per year, with the highest earners making over AU$100,000 per year. Keep in mind that these figures are based on factors such as location, experience, and industry demand, so your earnings may vary.

In summary, becoming a heavy vehicle mechanic in Australia can be a lucrative career choice, with the potential to earn a comfortable living wage. Factors such as location, experience, and industry demand can affect your earnings, so it is important to research your options and make an informed decision.

Types of Heavy Vehicle Mechanic Jobs

There are several types of heavy vehicle mechanic jobs available in Australia, each with its own unique set of requirements and potential earnings. Some of the most common types of heavy vehicle mechanic jobs include:

1. Diesel Mechanic

A diesel mechanic is responsible for repairing and maintaining diesel engines in heavy vehicles such as trucks, buses, and construction equipment. This type of mechanic may also work on other parts of the vehicle, such as the brakes, suspension, and electrical systems. According to PayScale, the average salary for a diesel mechanic in Australia is around AU$70,000 per year.

How to become a Diesel Mechanic:

To become a diesel mechanic in Australia, you will need to complete a Certificate III in Heavy Commercial Vehicle Mechanical Technology or a similar qualification. You may also need to obtain a Heavy Vehicle Licence and pass a background check.

2. Heavy Equipment Mechanic

A heavy equipment mechanic is responsible for repairing and maintaining heavy equipment such as bulldozers, backhoes, and excavators. This type of mechanic may work in construction, mining, or other industries that use heavy equipment. According to PayScale, the average salary for a heavy equipment mechanic in Australia is around AU$75,000 per year.

How to become a Heavy Equipment Mechanic:

To become a heavy equipment mechanic in Australia, you will need to complete a Certificate III in Heavy Commercial Vehicle Mechanical Technology or a similar qualification. You may also need to obtain a Heavy Vehicle Licence and pass a background check.
