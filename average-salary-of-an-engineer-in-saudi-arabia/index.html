Average Salary Of An Engineer In Saudi Arabia What You Need To Know

Written by Eveline Jun 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you an engineer looking to work in Saudi Arabia? Or are you already working in Saudi Arabia and wondering if your salary is competitive? The average salary of an engineer in Saudi Arabia is a topic that many people are curious about. In this post, we will discuss the average salary of an engineer in Saudi Arabia and provide insights on what you can expect.

While Saudi Arabia is known for its high salaries in the oil and gas industry, salaries for engineers can vary depending on the type of engineering and level of experience. For example, a mechanical engineer with 5 years of experience can expect to earn a different salary than a civil engineer with 10 years of experience. It's important to do your research and have realistic salary expectations before accepting a job offer.

The average salary of an engineer in Saudi Arabia is around SAR 168,000 per year ($44,800). This salary can vary depending on factors such as type of engineering, level of experience, and company size. Engineers in the oil and gas industry tend to earn higher salaries than those in other industries. Additionally, engineers with more experience and higher education levels can expect to earn higher salaries.

In summary, the average salary of an engineer in Saudi Arabia is around SAR 168,000 per year. However, it's important to keep in mind that salaries can vary depending on the type of engineering, level of experience, and company size. It's crucial to do your research and have realistic salary expectations before accepting a job offer.

My Experience with Engineer Salaries in Saudi Arabia

As an engineer working in Saudi Arabia, I can attest to the fact that salaries can vary greatly depending on the industry and level of experience. I have worked in both the oil and gas industry and the construction industry, and have seen firsthand the differences in salaries. In the oil and gas industry, salaries tend to be higher due to the high demand for engineers in this field. However, in the construction industry, salaries can be lower.

Factors that Affect Engineer Salaries in Saudi Arabia

There are several factors that can affect the salary of an engineer in Saudi Arabia. As previously mentioned, the type of engineering and level of experience are key factors. Additionally, the size of the company can also impact salaries. Larger companies tend to have more resources and may be able to offer higher salaries. Finally, the location of the job can also play a role in determining the salary. Salaries in major cities like Riyadh and Jeddah tend to be higher due to the higher cost of living.

Types of Engineering Salaries in Saudi Arabia

Salaries for engineers can vary depending on the type of engineering. In Saudi Arabia, some of the highest paying engineering jobs are in the oil and gas industry. Petroleum engineers, for example, can earn up to SAR 500,000 per year ($133,000). Civil engineers, on the other hand, tend to earn lower salaries.

How to Negotiate Your Engineer Salary in Saudi Arabia

Negotiating your engineer salary in Saudi Arabia can be challenging, but it's important to do your research and be prepared. Before accepting a job offer, research the average salary for your position and level of experience. Additionally, consider the cost of living in the city where you will be working. If you feel that the salary being offered is too low, consider negotiating for a higher salary or additional benefits.

Conclusion of Average Salary of an Engineer in Saudi Arabia

Overall, the average salary of an engineer in Saudi Arabia is around SAR 168,000 per year. However, salaries can vary depending on the type of engineering, level of experience, and company size. It's important to do your research and have realistic salary expectations before accepting a job offer. Additionally, consider negotiating for a higher salary or benefits if you feel that the offer is too low.
