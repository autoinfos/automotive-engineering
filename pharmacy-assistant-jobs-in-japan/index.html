Skills Needed for Pharmacy Assistant Jobs in Japan

If you're considering pursuing a career as a pharmacy assistant in Japan, there are several key skills and qualifications you'll need to have. These include:

Language Skills:

As mentioned earlier, being able to communicate effectively in Japanese is essential for pharmacy assistant jobs in Japan. This includes being able to read and write kanji, as well as being able to understand medical terminology and instructions.

Medical Knowledge:

While you don't necessarily need a medical degree to work as a pharmacy assistant in Japan, you will need to have a basic understanding of anatomy, physiology, and pharmacology. This will help you to understand how different medications work and how to advise patients on proper usage.

Attention to Detail:

As a pharmacy assistant, you'll need to be extremely detail-oriented and organized. This includes being able to accurately measure and dispense medications, as well as keeping track of inventory and patient information.
