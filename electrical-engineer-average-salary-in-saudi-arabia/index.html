Exploring Electrical Engineer Average Salary In Saudi Arabia

Written by Eveline Oct 26, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you considering a career in electrical engineering in Saudi Arabia but unsure about the average salary? Do you want to know the earning potential of an electrical engineer in this country? If yes, then this post is for you.

Being an electrical engineer in Saudi Arabia comes with its own set of challenges, including long working hours, demanding job requirements, and the need for continuous learning and staying updated with new technologies. However, the salary paid to electrical engineers in Saudi Arabia can make up for these challenges.

The average salary of an electrical engineer in Saudi Arabia is around SAR 146,000 per year. However, this amount can vary depending on various factors such as experience, location, qualifications, and employer. For instance, an entry-level electrical engineer can earn around SAR 85,000 per year, while a senior-level electrical engineer can make up to SAR 250,000 per year.

In summary, electrical engineers in Saudi Arabia can earn a decent salary compared to other professionals. The earning potential can increase with experience, qualifications, and other factors such as location and employer.

Personal Experience with Electrical Engineer Average Salary in Saudi Arabia

As an electrical engineer with five years of experience in Saudi Arabia, I can attest to the fact that the salary paid to electrical engineers in this country is reasonable. When I started my career, my salary was around SAR 90,000 per year. However, as I gained more experience and skills, my salary increased to SAR 180,000 per year.

Factors that Affect Electrical Engineer Average Salary in Saudi Arabia

Several factors can affect the average salary of an electrical engineer in Saudi Arabia. These include:

Experience

The more experience an electrical engineer has, the higher the salary they can expect. Entry-level positions pay less compared to senior-level positions.

Qualifications

Electrical engineers with advanced degrees or certifications can earn more than those without them.

Employer and Location

The salary paid to electrical engineers can also vary depending on the employer and location. For instance, electrical engineers working for multinational companies in major cities like Riyadh and Jeddah can earn more than those working for small companies in rural areas.

Job Outlook for Electrical Engineers in Saudi Arabia

The demand for electrical engineers in Saudi Arabia is high, especially in the oil and gas, construction, and manufacturing industries. As the country continues to diversify its economy and invest in infrastructure, the demand for electrical engineers is expected to increase further.

Conclusion of Electrical Engineer Average Salary in Saudi Arabia

Electrical engineering is a lucrative career in Saudi Arabia, with a decent salary and positive job outlook. The salary paid to electrical engineers can vary depending on several factors, including experience, qualifications, employer, and location. However, with the right skills and qualifications, electrical engineers in Saudi Arabia can earn a good living and have a fulfilling career.
