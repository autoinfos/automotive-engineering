What are mechanical engineering jobs in Germany Quora?

As a mechanical engineer in Germany, you will be responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining mechanical systems and equipment. This can include anything from automotive parts and machinery to robotics and aerospace technology. The industry is diverse and constantly evolving, with new technologies and innovations emerging all the time.

What qualifications do you need for mechanical engineering jobs in Germany Quora?

In order to work as a mechanical engineer in Germany, you will typically need a degree in mechanical engineering or a related field. Additionally, you may need to be fluent in German, particularly if you plan to work in a German-speaking company or industry.
