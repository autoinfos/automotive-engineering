Question and Answer

Q: What is the average cost of auto transport in Florida?

A: The cost of auto transport in Florida varies depending on several factors, such as the distance, the type of transport, and the time of year. On average, you can expect to pay between $500 and $1,500.

Q: How long does auto transport take in Florida?

A: Again, the transport time depends on several factors. However, on average, you can expect your vehicle to be transported within 3-10 days.

Q: Do I need to prepare my vehicle for transport?

A: Yes, you should prepare your vehicle for transport by removing any personal items, securing loose parts, and ensuring that your vehicle is in good working condition.

Q: What if my vehicle gets damaged during transport?

A: If your vehicle gets damaged during transport, you should file a claim with the transport company's insurance provider. Make sure to take pictures of the damage and report it as soon as possible.
