Best Motor Movers Make Your Life Easier With These Top Picks

Written by Aprilia Oct 02, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of struggling to move your motorhome or caravan? Do you find it difficult to get your vehicle in position, or to manoeuvre it into tight spaces? If so, you're not alone.

Many motorhome and caravan owners experience frustration when it comes to moving their vehicles. This can be especially challenging if you're travelling alone or have limited mobility. Fortunately, there are now a range of motor movers available that can help make your life easier.

What are Motor Movers?

Motor movers are devices that help you to move your motorhome or caravan with ease. They attach to your vehicle's wheels, allowing you to manoeuvre it using a remote control. This means you can easily move your vehicle forwards, backwards, left, and right, without having to physically push it.

Motor movers are especially useful for those who struggle with physical mobility, as they eliminate the need for manual pushing or pulling. They're also great for those who need to move their motorhome or caravan into tight spaces, as they allow for precise manoeuvring.

Top Picks for Motor Movers

There are many different motor movers available on the market, each with their own unique features and benefits. Here are some of the top picks:

1. Powrtouch Evolution

The Powrtouch Evolution is a popular choice among motorhome and caravan owners. It's easy to install and comes with a lightweight remote control. The Powrtouch Evolution is also known for its precision, allowing for accurate manoeuvring even in tight spaces.

2. Reich Move Control

The Reich Move Control is another great option for those looking for a reliable motor mover. It comes with a range of safety features, including automatic cut-off and soft start. The Reich Move Control is also easy to install, making it a great choice for those who want a hassle-free solution.

3. Truma XT

The Truma XT is a high-end motor mover that's packed with features. It comes with a remote control that has a range of up to 30 metres, making it easy to manoeuvre your vehicle from a distance. The Truma XT also has a soft start feature, which helps to protect your vehicle's tyres.

4. Purple Line Enduro

The Purple Line Enduro is a popular choice for those who want a reliable and affordable motor mover. It's easy to install and comes with a range of safety features, including automatic cut-off and soft start. The Purple Line Enduro is also known for its durability, making it a great investment for those who want a long-lasting solution.

Benefits of Using a Motor Mover

There are many benefits to using a motor mover, including:

1. Easier Manoeuvrability

With a motor mover, you can easily move your motorhome or caravan in any direction, without having to physically push or pull it. This makes manoeuvring your vehicle much easier, especially in tight spaces.

2. Increased Safety

Moving your motorhome or caravan manually can be dangerous, especially if you have limited mobility. With a motor mover, you can move your vehicle safely and accurately, without putting yourself at risk.

3. Reduced Strain

Using a motor mover eliminates the need for manual pushing or pulling, which can be physically demanding. This is especially important for those with limited mobility or health issues.

4. Precise Control

Motor movers allow for precise control, making it easy to manoeuvre your vehicle exactly where you want it. This is especially useful when parking in tight spaces or manoeuvring around obstacles.

Question and Answer

Q. How do I choose the right motor mover for my vehicle?

A. When choosing a motor mover, you'll need to consider the weight and size of your vehicle, as well as your budget. Be sure to choose a motor mover that's compatible with your vehicle's wheels and that has the features you need.

Q. Can I install a motor mover myself?

A. While it is possible to install a motor mover yourself, it's generally recommended that you hire a professional to do the job. This will ensure that the motor mover is installed correctly and safely.

Q. Will a motor mover damage my vehicle's tyres?

A. No, a motor mover should not damage your vehicle's tyres. In fact, many motor movers come with features that help to protect your tyres, such as soft start.

Q. How much does a motor mover cost?

A. The cost of a motor mover can vary depending on the brand and model. Generally, you can expect to pay anywhere from £500 to £1500 for a motor mover.

Conclusion of Best Motor Movers

If you're tired of struggling to move your motorhome or caravan, a motor mover could be the solution you've been looking for. With a range of top picks available on the market, you're sure to find a motor mover that meets your needs and budget.

Whether you're looking for easier manoeuvrability, increased safety, or reduced strain, a motor mover can help make your life easier. So why not invest in a motor mover today and start enjoying the benefits?
