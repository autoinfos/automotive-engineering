Answer to How much do Helicopter Mechanics make in Canada

According to the Government of Canada Job Bank, the median salary for helicopter mechanics in Canada is $32.50 per hour. This equates to an annual salary of approximately $67,600. However, this is just a median and salaries can range from as low as $20 per hour to as high as $48 per hour depending on various factors.
