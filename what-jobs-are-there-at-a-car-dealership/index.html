What Jobs Are There At A Car Dealership

Written by Aprilia Nov 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Have you ever thought about working at a car dealership but aren't sure what jobs are available? There are a variety of roles that contribute to the smooth running of a dealership, from sales to service and everything in between. In this post, we'll explore the different jobs that you can find at a car dealership and what they entail.

Working at a car dealership can be a challenging but rewarding experience. It's a fast-paced environment where you'll be working with a diverse group of people, from customers to colleagues. However, it's not always easy to know where to start when it comes to job hunting in the automotive industry. With so many different roles available, it can be overwhelming to try and figure out which one is right for you.

What Jobs Are There at a Car Dealership?

Let's start by looking at the different types of jobs you'll find at a car dealership:

Sales Jobs

Sales jobs are some of the most common roles at a car dealership. Salespeople are responsible for selling vehicles to customers, and they typically work on a commission-based pay structure. Sales jobs may require a lot of customer interaction, as well as a strong knowledge of the cars being sold.

Finance and Insurance

Another type of sales job involves finance and insurance. These roles are responsible for selling financing and insurance products to customers. This may include things like car loans, extended warranties, and gap insurance. Finance and insurance jobs require a strong understanding of financial products and regulations, as well as excellent sales skills.

Service Jobs

Service jobs are focused on maintaining and repairing vehicles. This may involve anything from basic maintenance tasks like oil changes to more complex repairs. Service jobs may require a lot of technical knowledge, as well as strong communication skills to work with customers effectively.

Parts Jobs

Parts jobs are focused on ordering and maintaining the inventory of parts needed for repairs. This may involve working with customers to identify the parts they need, as well as managing the supply chain to ensure that parts are available when needed. Parts jobs require strong organizational skills and an understanding of automotive parts and systems.

My Experience Working at a Car Dealership

I've worked in a few different roles at car dealerships over the years, including sales and service positions. One of the things I've enjoyed most about working in the automotive industry is the variety of jobs available. No matter what your interests or skills are, there's likely a role that will suit you.

One thing to keep in mind when considering a job at a car dealership is that it can be a challenging environment. There are often high expectations for performance, and you'll need to be comfortable working in a fast-paced, high-pressure environment. However, if you're up for the challenge, it can be a rewarding career path.

How to Get a Job at a Car Dealership

If you're interested in working at a car dealership, there are a few things you can do to increase your chances of getting hired:

Get Educated

Many jobs in the automotive industry require some level of education or training. Look for programs that offer certification or degrees in areas like automotive technology, sales, or business.

Build Your Skills

Even if you don't have formal education or training, you can still build the skills needed to work at a car dealership. Look for opportunities to gain experience in sales, customer service, or automotive repair.

FAQs

What is a typical day like for a salesperson at a car dealership?

A typical day for a salesperson may involve meeting with customers to discuss their needs, showing them different vehicles, and negotiating prices and financing options.

What skills are needed for a service job at a car dealership?

Service jobs require strong technical skills, as well as excellent communication skills to work with customers and colleagues.

How can I stand out when applying for a job at a car dealership?

One way to stand out is to highlight any relevant education or experience you have. Additionally, be sure to emphasize your skills in areas like customer service, sales, or automotive repair.

What is the average salary for jobs at a car dealership?

Salaries can vary widely depending on the role and location. Sales jobs may be commission-based, while service jobs may be hourly or salaried.

Conclusion of what jobs are there at a car dealership

Working at a car dealership can be a challenging but rewarding experience. There are a variety of roles available, from sales to service and everything in between. Whether you're interested in working with customers, repairing vehicles, or managing parts inventory, there's likely a job that will suit your skills and interests. By getting educated, building your skills, and emphasizing your strengths, you can increase your chances of landing a job at a car dealership.
