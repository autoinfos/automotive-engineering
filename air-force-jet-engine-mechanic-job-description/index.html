What Does an Air Force Jet Engine Mechanic Do?

As an air force jet engine mechanic, your main responsibility is to ensure the engines of military aircraft are in top condition. This includes conducting routine inspections and testing engines to ensure they are working properly. How Can You Become an Air Force Jet Engine Mechanic?

To become an air force jet engine mechanic, you'll need to have a high school diploma or GED and complete basic military training.
