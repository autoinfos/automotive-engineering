Question and Answer

Q: How much do driving lessons cost in Gurgaon?

A: The cost of driving lessons in Gurgaon can vary widely depending on the school, the instructor, and the location. On average, you can expect to pay anywhere from Rs. 500 to Rs. 2000 per hour for driving lessons.

Q: How long does it take to learn to drive in Gurgaon?

A: The length of time it takes to learn to drive in Gurgaon can vary depending on your individual needs and skill level. On average, it takes around 20-30 hours of practice to gain the skills and confidence needed to pass your driving test.

Q: Do I need to have a learner's permit to take driving lessons in Gurgaon?

A: Yes, you must have a valid learner's permit to take driving lessons in Gurgaon. You can apply for a learner's permit at your local RTO office by providing proof of age, identity, and residence, as well as passing a written test.

Q: Can I take driving lessons in Gurgaon if I don't have a car?

A: Yes, most driving schools in Gurgaon provide cars for their students to practice in. However, you may need to pay an additional fee for the use of the school's car.
