What is an Automotive Painter's Helper?

An automotive painter's helper is an entry-level position in the automotive industry. This person assists professional automotive painters with tasks such as prepping vehicles for paint, sanding, cleaning, and masking. They may also assist with mixing paint and performing quality control checks.
