Types of Questions in IC Engine Interview Questions PDF

The IC engine interview questions PDF covers a wide range of topics related to IC engines. Some of the most common types of questions include:

1. What is an IC engine, and how does it work?

Answer: An IC engine is a heat engine that converts chemical energy into mechanical energy. It works by burning fuel to produce high-pressure gases, which in turn move a piston. This movement is then converted into rotational motion, which powers the vehicle.

2. What are the different types of IC engines?

Answer: There are two main types of IC engines: petrol engines and diesel engines. Petrol engines use a spark to ignite the fuel, while diesel engines use compression to ignite the fuel.

3. What is the difference between a petrol engine and a diesel engine?

Answer: The main difference between a petrol engine and a diesel engine is the way in which fuel is ignited. In a petrol engine, fuel is ignited by a spark, while in a diesel engine, fuel is ignited by compression.

4. What are the advantages and disadvantages of using an IC engine?

Answer: The advantages of using an IC engine include high efficiency, good power output, and low cost. The disadvantages include high emissions, noise pollution, and the need for regular maintenance.
