Medical Laboratory Technologist Jobs In Kenya 2021 A Look At Career Opportunities

Written by William Sep 21, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you considering a career in medical laboratory technology? Have you been searching for medical laboratory technologist jobs in Kenya 2021? Look no further. In this blog post, we will explore the job market for medical laboratory technologists in Kenya and provide insights into this exciting and rewarding career path.

As with any profession, there are challenges that come with being a medical laboratory technologist. For instance, the work can be physically demanding and mentally exhausting, and there is always the risk of exposure to harmful chemicals and infectious diseases. Additionally, like many healthcare professions, medical laboratory technologists are in high demand but there may be limited job openings in certain areas.

Despite these challenges, there are many reasons to pursue a career in medical laboratory technology, and the job outlook for medical laboratory technologist jobs in Kenya 2021 is promising. According to the Kenya Medical Laboratory Technicians and Technologists Board, there are currently over 12,000 registered medical laboratory technologists in the country, and this number is expected to grow significantly in the coming years.

In summary, medical laboratory technologist jobs in Kenya 2021 offer a wide range of opportunities for those interested in pursuing a career in this field. With a growing demand for medical laboratory services, there is no shortage of job openings for qualified professionals. Whether you are just starting out in your career or looking to make a change, medical laboratory technology is a rewarding and fulfilling profession that offers a bright future.

What are the Requirements for Medical Laboratory Technologist Jobs in Kenya 2021?

Aspiring medical laboratory technologists must meet certain educational and professional requirements in order to practice in Kenya. These requirements include:

- A minimum of a diploma in medical laboratory sciences from a recognized institution.

- Registration with the Kenya Medical Laboratory Technicians and Technologists Board.

- Completion of a one-year internship program at a recognized medical laboratory.

- Continuing education and professional development to maintain registration with the board.

Additionally, medical laboratory technologists must possess certain skills and qualities in order to excel in their profession. These include attention to detail, critical thinking and problem-solving skills, strong communication and interpersonal skills, and a commitment to accuracy and precision.

What is the Salary Range for Medical Laboratory Technologist Jobs in Kenya 2021?

The salary range for medical laboratory technologist jobs in Kenya 2021 varies depending on a number of factors, including level of experience, location, and type of employer. On average, medical laboratory technologists can expect to earn between Ksh 40,000 and Ksh 80,000 per month.

What is the Job Market Like for Medical Laboratory Technologist Jobs in Kenya 2021?

The job market for medical laboratory technologists in Kenya is growing, with increasing demand for laboratory services in both public and private healthcare settings. As such, there are many job opportunities available for qualified professionals, particularly in major urban centers such as Nairobi, Mombasa, and Kisumu.

How Can I Prepare for a Career in Medical Laboratory Technology?

If you are interested in pursuing a career in medical laboratory technology, there are a number of steps you can take to prepare yourself for this field. These include:

- Pursuing a diploma or degree in medical laboratory sciences from a recognized institution.

- Gaining practical experience through internships or work-study programs.

- Participating in continuing education and professional development activities to stay up-to-date with the latest advances in the field.

- Developing strong communication and interpersonal skills, as well as attention to detail and critical thinking abilities.
