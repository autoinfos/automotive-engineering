Top Rated Suvs For Safety And Reliability

Written by Robby Oct 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you in the market for a new SUV but unsure of which models offer the best safety and reliability ratings? Look no further! We've compiled a list of the top rated SUVs for safety and reliability to make your decision easier.

When it comes to choosing an SUV, safety and reliability are two of the most important factors to consider. After all, you want a vehicle that will protect you and your loved ones in the event of an accident, as well as one that will last for years to come without breaking down or requiring constant repairs.

So, what are the top rated SUVs for safety and reliability? According to the experts at Consumer Reports, the following models are among the best:

Toyota RAV4

As one of the most popular SUVs on the market, the Toyota RAV4 is well-known for its reliability and safety features. With a spacious interior, comfortable ride, and excellent fuel economy, it's no wonder the RAV4 is a top pick for families and commuters alike.

Honda CR-V

The Honda CR-V is another top rated SUV for safety and reliability. With its impressive crash test ratings, strong resale value, and affordable price point, the CR-V is a smart choice for anyone in the market for a new SUV.

Subaru Forester

If you're looking for an SUV that can handle all types of weather and road conditions, the Subaru Forester is a great option. With standard all-wheel drive and advanced safety features like EyeSight driver assist technology, the Forester is a top pick for those who prioritize safety and reliability.

Mazda CX-5

Finally, the Mazda CX-5 is another SUV that consistently receives high marks for safety and reliability. With its sleek design, fun-to-drive handling, and impressive fuel economy, the CX-5 is a great choice for those who want a practical yet stylish vehicle.

Personal Experience

As someone who has owned both a Toyota RAV4 and a Honda CR-V, I can attest to the safety and reliability of both models. In my experience, both vehicles were able to handle a variety of road conditions and kept me and my passengers safe in the event of an accident.

Factors to Consider

When choosing a top rated SUV for safety and reliability, there are several factors to consider. These include:

Crash Test Ratings

Look for SUVs that have high crash test ratings from organizations like the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA) and the Insurance Institute for Highway Safety (IIHS).

Reliability Ratings

Check out reliability ratings from sources like Consumer Reports and J.D. Power to ensure that the SUV you're considering has a good track record for quality and dependability.

Personal Experience

Another factor to consider is the cost of maintenance and repairs. In my experience, both the Toyota RAV4 and Honda CR-V were relatively affordable to maintain and didn't require many repairs over the years.

Question and Answer

Q: Are SUVs generally safer than other types of vehicles?

A: SUVs tend to be safer than smaller cars due to their larger size and weight. However, not all SUVs are created equal when it comes to safety ratings.

Q: What are some common safety features to look for in an SUV?

A: Some common safety features to look for include airbags, anti-lock brakes, traction and stability control, and backup cameras.

Q: How often should I have my SUV serviced to ensure its safety and reliability?

A: It's important to follow your manufacturer's recommended maintenance schedule, which typically includes regular oil changes, tire rotations, and other important checks.

Q: Can I save money on car insurance by choosing a top rated SUV for safety?

A: Some insurance companies may offer discounts for vehicles with high safety ratings, so it's worth checking with your provider to see if you qualify.

Conclusion of Top Rated SUVs for Safety and Reliability

When it comes to choosing a new SUV, safety and reliability should be your top priorities. By considering factors like crash test ratings, reliability ratings, and cost of maintenance, you can make an informed decision and choose a vehicle that will keep you and your loved ones safe for years to come.
