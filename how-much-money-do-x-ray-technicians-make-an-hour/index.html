How Much Money Do X Ray Technicians Make An Hour

Written by Petter Aug 20, 2023 Â· 3 min read

As you consider a career in healthcare, one thing that may be on your mind is the potential salary. X ray technicians, also known as radiologic technologists, play an important role in diagnosing and treating patients. But how much money do x ray technicians make an hour?

When it comes to healthcare careers, there are several factors that can impact your earning potential. For x ray technicians, factors such as experience, location, and education can all play a role in determining your salary.

On average, x ray technicians in the United States make around $28 per hour. However, this can vary depending on where you work and how much experience you have in the field. Some x ray technicians may make closer to $40 per hour, while others may start out closer to $20 per hour.

My Experience with X Ray Technician Salaries

When I was considering a career as an x ray technician, I was curious about the earning potential. After completing my education and gaining some experience in the field, I found that my salary was on the higher end of the scale. I was making around $35 per hour, which was a comfortable living wage for me.

However, I also knew other x ray technicians who were making less than I was. Some of them were just starting out in their careers, while others worked in areas with lower average salaries. It really does depend on a variety of factors.

Factors That Impact X Ray Technician Salaries

As mentioned earlier, there are several factors that can impact how much money x ray technicians make per hour. Here are a few to consider:

Experience:

Like many careers, x ray technician salaries can increase with experience. As you gain more experience and become more skilled in your work, you may be able to negotiate a higher salary.

Location:

The location where you work can also impact your salary. Generally, areas with higher costs of living may offer higher salaries to compensate for the increased expenses. Additionally, some states or cities may have higher average salaries for healthcare workers in general.

Education and Certification:

Having a degree or certification in radiologic technology can also impact your salary. Employers may be willing to pay more for candidates who have completed a formal education program or who hold specialized certifications in the field.

FAQs about X Ray Technician Salaries:

1. What is the average salary for x ray technicians?

The average salary for x ray technicians in the United States is around $28 per hour.

2. How much can x ray technicians make with experience?

X ray technician salaries can increase with experience. Some x ray technicians may make closer to $40 per hour with several years of experience.

3. What factors can impact x ray technician salaries?

Factors such as location, experience, and education can all impact how much money x ray technicians make per hour.

4. Is it possible to negotiate a higher salary as an x ray technician?

Yes, it may be possible to negotiate a higher salary as an x ray technician, especially if you have specialized skills or certifications.

Conclusion of How Much Money Do X Ray Technicians Make an Hour

While x ray technician salaries can vary depending on several factors, it is possible to make a comfortable living wage in this field. By gaining experience, pursuing education and certification, and working in areas with higher average salaries, you may be able to increase your earning potential as an x ray technician.
