Top Paying Truck Driving Jobs in North Dakota

One of the top paying truck driving jobs in North Dakota is hauling crude oil. With the state's booming oil industry, there is a high demand for drivers who can transport crude oil from drilling sites to refineries. This job typically requires a CDL (Commercial Driver's License) and experience in the oil and gas industry. Drivers can expect to earn an average of $80,000 to $100,000 per year.

Another high paying truck driving job in North Dakota is hauling water to drilling sites. This job also requires a CDL and experience in the oil and gas industry. Drivers can expect to earn an average of $70,000 to $90,000 per year.

Getting Certified for High Paying Truck Driving Jobs in North Dakota

How to Find High Paying Truck Driving Jobs in North Dakota

FAQs About High Paying Truck Driving Jobs in North Dakota

Q: What is the average salary for a truck driver in North Dakota?

A: To get a CDL in North Dakota, you must be at least 18 years old and have a valid driver's license. You must also pass a written test and a driving test.

A: Some of the top companies hiring truck drivers in North Dakota include Halliburton, Schlumberger, and Baker Hughes.
