What is an Engine Mechanic?

An engine mechanic is a professional who is responsible for inspecting, repairing, and maintaining various types of engines, including those in vehicles, aircraft, and other machinery. Engine mechanics use a variety of tools and techniques to diagnose and fix engine problems, and they must remain up-to-date with the latest technologies and techniques in order to stay competitive in this field.
