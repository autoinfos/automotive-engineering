Autozone is one of the leading automotive parts and accessories retailers in the United States. When it comes to hourly pay rates, the automotive industry is known for its competitiveness. Many employees in this field work hard but struggle to earn a fair wage. This is why it's essential to research a company's pay rates before accepting a job offer.

According to various sources, the average hourly pay for Autozone employees is $12.11. However, this pay rate can vary depending on factors such as location, experience level, and job position. For instance, a sales associate at Autozone can expect to make around $10-$12 per hour, while a store manager can earn up to $20 per hour.

In summary, Autozone pays its employees an average hourly pay of $12.11. However, this can vary depending on several factors such as location, experience level, and job position.

Autozone offers various job positions, from entry-level sales associates to store managers. Naturally, employees with more experience and expertise in their roles are likely to earn more than those with less experience.
