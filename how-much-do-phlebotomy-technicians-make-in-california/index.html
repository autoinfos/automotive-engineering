When it comes to understanding how much phlebotomy technicians make in California, there are several factors that come into play. These include location, education, and experience.

Education and Training Required to Become a Phlebotomy Technician in California

In order to become a phlebotomy technician in California, you'll need to complete a phlebotomy training program. These programs typically take several months to complete and include both classroom and hands-on training.

How to Advance Your Career as a Phlebotomy Technician in California
