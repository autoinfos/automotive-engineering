What Is The Average 3D Cad Designer Salary In Dubai

Written by Daniel Jul 26, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you an aspiring 3D CAD designer looking to work in Dubai? Do you want to know the average salary range for this profession in the city? Look no further as we delve into the details of 3D CAD designer salary in Dubai.

As a 3D CAD designer, you may encounter several pain points when it comes to salary expectations in Dubai. For instance, you may be unsure of how much you should expect to earn, what factors affect your salary, and whether or not you will receive benefits such as health insurance and paid time off.

The average 3D CAD designer salary in Dubai ranges from AED 63,000 to AED 216,000 per year. The actual figure depends on several factors such as your experience, company size, and industry. For instance, entry-level 3D CAD designers typically earn a lower salary compared to those with several years of experience in the field.

Personal Experience with 3D CAD Designer Salary in Dubai

As a 3D CAD designer with three years of experience working in the architecture industry in Dubai, I can attest to the fact that salary expectations vary widely depending on the company and industry. In my experience, working for a large architecture firm in Dubai meant that I earned more than my counterparts working for smaller firms.

Factors that Affect 3D CAD Designer Salary in Dubai

Several factors can affect the average 3D CAD designer salary in Dubai. For instance, your level of experience in the field plays a crucial role in determining your salary range. Other factors include the size of the company you work for, the industry you work in, and the location of the company in Dubai.

Experience Level

As mentioned earlier, entry-level 3D CAD designers typically earn a lower salary compared to those with several years of experience in the field. The more experience you have, the higher your salary range.

Industry

Another factor that affects 3D CAD designer salary in Dubai is the industry you work in. For instance, working in the architecture or engineering industry may offer a higher salary range compared to working in the manufacturing industry.

Highest Paying Companies for 3D CAD Designers in Dubai

Some of the highest paying companies for 3D CAD designers in Dubai include architecture and engineering firms such as AECOM, KEO International Consultants, and HOK. Other companies include manufacturing firms such as Siemens and General Electric.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the average salary range for entry-level 3D CAD designers in Dubai?

A: The average salary range for entry-level 3D CAD designers in Dubai is around AED 63,000 per year.

Q: How does the size of the company affect 3D CAD designer salary in Dubai?

A: Working for a larger company in Dubai may offer a higher salary range compared to working for a smaller company.

Q: What are some of the highest paying industries for 3D CAD designers in Dubai?

A: Some of the highest paying industries for 3D CAD designers in Dubai include architecture, engineering, and manufacturing.

Q: How does location affect 3D CAD designer salary in Dubai?

A: Working for a company located in a prime area in Dubai may offer a higher salary range compared to working for a company located in a less desirable area.

Conclusion of 3D CAD Designer Salary in Dubai

As a 3D CAD designer, it is crucial to research the average salary range and factors that affect it before accepting a job offer in Dubai. Keep in mind that the actual salary range depends on several factors such as experience, company size, and industry. However, working for a larger company in a prime location may offer a higher salary range compared to working for a smaller company in a less desirable area.
