Tesla Field Technical Specialist Salary All You Need To Know

Written by Daniel May 29, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you considering a career as a Tesla Field Technical Specialist but wondering about the salary? Look no further, we have got you covered. As a Tesla Field Technical Specialist, you are responsible for providing technical support to Tesla's electric vehicle customers. The role comes with its unique challenges, and one of the most important things you want to know is how much you will be earning. In this post, we will dive deep into the salary structure of a Tesla Field Technical Specialist and answer any questions you may have.

The salary of a Tesla Field Technical Specialist varies depending on several factors, such as level of experience, education, and location. According to Glassdoor, the average base pay for a Tesla Field Technical Specialist is $62,000 per year. However, with bonuses and other forms of compensation, the total pay can range from $46,000 to $114,000 per year. While these figures may seem low compared to other positions in the tech industry, it is important to note that Tesla offers several benefits to its employees, such as stock options and health insurance.

Tesla Field Technical Specialist Salary: Explained

Let's break down the salary structure of a Tesla Field Technical Specialist. The base pay of $62,000 per year may seem low, but it is important to note that this is only the starting point. Tesla offers several opportunities for career advancement, which means that as you gain more experience and skills, your salary will also increase.

In addition to the base pay, Tesla offers several forms of compensation, such as bonuses and stock options. Bonuses are typically given to employees who meet or exceed their performance targets. Stock options, on the other hand, allow employees to purchase Tesla stocks at a discounted price. This is a great way to invest in the company's future and potentially make a profit.

My Personal Experience

I have been working as a Tesla Field Technical Specialist for over three years now, and I can say that the salary structure is quite fair.
