FAQs About Car Driving School Near Me Price

Q: How much does it cost to take driving lessons?

A: The cost of driving lessons can vary depending on several factors, such as location, instructor, and type of car used. On average, driving lessons can cost anywhere from $50 to $100 per hour.

Q: Is it cheaper to take group lessons or individual lessons?

A: Group lessons tend to be cheaper than individual lessons. However, individual lessons allow for more personalized instruction and attention.

Q: How many driving lessons do I need to take before taking the driving test?

A: The number of driving lessons required varies depending on the individual. However, it is recommended to take at least 20 hours of lessons before taking the driving test.

Q: Can I negotiate the price of driving lessons?

A: Some driving schools may offer discounts or package deals that you can take advantage of. However, it is essential to prioritize quality of instruction over cost when choosing a driving school.
