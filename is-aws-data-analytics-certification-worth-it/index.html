Is Aws Data Analytics Certification Worth It

Written by Aprilia Jul 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering a career in data analytics and wondering if getting certified in AWS (Amazon Web Services) is worth it? With the rise of big data and the increasing demand for skilled professionals in this field, it's important to know if investing time and money in a certification program will pay off in the long run.

Many professionals in the data analytics industry face the challenge of keeping up with the latest technology and constantly evolving practices. The need for certifications is often driven by the desire to stay relevant and competitive in the job market. However, it's important to consider the cost and time commitment required for earning a certification and whether it will truly benefit your career.

The short answer is yes, an AWS Data Analytics Certification is definitely worth it. Here's why:

Benefits of AWS Data Analytics Certification

Firstly, earning an AWS Data Analytics Certification demonstrates a high level of expertise in the field. It shows potential employers that you have a strong understanding of data analytics and the ability to work with AWS tools and services. This can make you stand out from other candidates and increase your chances of getting hired or promoted.

Secondly, AWS is one of the most widely used cloud platforms for data analytics. Having a certification in this area provides a competitive advantage and can open up new job opportunities. It also allows you to work with a variety of clients across different industries who use AWS for their data analytics needs.

Thirdly, the demand for professionals with AWS Data Analytics Certification is high. According to a report by Burning Glass Technologies, AWS-certified professionals earn an average of 25% higher salaries than those without certification. This is a significant incentive for investing in a certification program.

My Personal Experience with AWS Data Analytics Certification

As someone who has worked in data analytics for several years, I can attest to the value of earning an AWS Data Analytics Certification. Not only did it increase my confidence in my skills, but it also opened up new job opportunities and allowed me to work with a wider range of clients.

During the certification program, I learned how to work with various AWS tools such as Amazon Redshift, Amazon Athena, and Amazon EMR. These skills have proven invaluable in my work and have allowed me to provide better insights and solutions for my clients.

How to Get Certified in AWS Data Analytics

The AWS Data Analytics Certification program consists of two levels: Associate and Specialty. The Associate level requires passing the AWS Certified Data Analytics - Specialty exam, while the Specialty level requires passing the AWS Certified Advanced Analytics - Specialty exam.

It's important to note that prior experience in data analytics and AWS is recommended before attempting these exams. AWS offers a variety of training courses and resources to help prepare for the exams, including online courses, practice exams, and study guides.

FAQs about AWS Data Analytics Certification

Q: How long does it take to get certified in AWS Data Analytics?

A: The amount of time it takes to earn an AWS Data Analytics Certification depends on your level of experience and the time commitment you can dedicate to studying. On average, it can take anywhere from 2-6 months to prepare for and pass the exams.

Q: How much does it cost to get certified in AWS Data Analytics?

A: The cost of AWS Data Analytics Certification exams varies by location and level. The Associate level exam costs $300, while the Specialty level exam costs $300-400. Additionally, training courses and study materials may incur additional costs.

Q: Is AWS Data Analytics Certification recognized globally?

A: Yes, AWS Data Analytics Certification is recognized globally and can open up job opportunities in various countries and industries.

Q: Can I renew my AWS Data Analytics Certification?

A: Yes, AWS Data Analytics Certifications must be renewed every two years to maintain their validity. This requires passing a recertification exam or earning a higher-level certification.

Conclusion of Is AWS Data Analytics Certification Worth It

In conclusion, an AWS Data Analytics Certification is definitely worth the investment if you're looking to advance your career in data analytics. It demonstrates expertise in the field, opens up new job opportunities, and can lead to higher salaries. However, it's important to consider the cost and time commitment required for earning the certification and ensure you have the necessary experience and knowledge before attempting the exams.
