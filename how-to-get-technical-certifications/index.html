How To Get Technical Certifications A Beginner s Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 26, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you interested in pursuing a career in technology and wondering how to get technical certifications? You're not alone. The tech industry is constantly evolving, and obtaining technical certifications can give you a competitive edge in the job market. In this post, we'll explore the steps you need to take to get technical certifications and advance your career.

The Pain Points of Getting Technical Certifications

One of the biggest obstacles to getting technical certifications is figuring out where to start. With so many different certifications available, it can be overwhelming to determine which ones are right for you. Additionally, the certification process can be time-consuming and expensive, requiring you to invest a significant amount of time and money into studying and taking exams.

How to Get Technical Certifications

The first step in getting technical certifications is to determine which certifications are best suited to your career goals. Research different certifications and their requirements, and consider consulting with a career counselor or mentor to help guide your decision-making process.

Once you've identified the certifications you want to pursue, you'll need to invest time and energy into studying for the exams. Consider taking courses or working with a tutor to prepare for the exams, and create a study schedule to help you stay on track.

When you're ready to take the exams, be sure to register in advance and arrive at the testing center with plenty of time to spare. Bring any necessary materials, such as identification and a calculator, and try to stay calm and focused during the exam.

Main Points to Remember

To summarize, getting technical certifications requires you to:

  • Research certifications and their requirements
  • Invest in studying and preparation
  • Register for exams in advance
  • Arrive at the testing center with all necessary materials

Personal Experience: My Journey to Getting Technical Certifications

When I first started out in the tech industry, I was overwhelmed by the number of certifications available and unsure of where to start. After consulting with a mentor and doing some research, I decided to pursue the CompTIA A+ certification.

To prepare for the exam, I enrolled in an online course and set aside time each day to study. I also purchased practice exams and worked through them regularly to build my confidence and identify areas where I needed to improve.

On the day of the exam, I arrived at the testing center early and brought all necessary materials. Despite feeling nervous, I was able to stay focused and complete the exam within the allotted time. A few weeks later, I received my results: I had passed the exam and earned my certification!

The Benefits of Getting Technical Certifications

Obtaining technical certifications can provide a number of benefits for your career, including:

  • Increased job opportunities
  • Higher earning potential
  • Greater credibility and professional recognition
  • Opportunities for professional development and networking

Choosing the Right Certification

When selecting which technical certifications to pursue, it's important to consider your career goals and the skills and knowledge you want to develop. Some popular certifications include:

  • CompTIA A+
  • Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA)
  • Microsoft Certified Solutions Expert (MCSE)
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS) Certified Solutions Architect

Studying for the Exam

Studying for technical certification exams can be challenging, but there are a number of resources available to help you prepare. Consider:

  • Taking courses or working with a tutor
  • Using study guides and practice exams
  • Joining study groups or online forums

Personal Experience: Overcoming Exam Anxiety

As someone who struggles with anxiety, I was worried about how I would perform on the technical certification exams. To manage my anxiety, I developed a self-care routine that included exercise, meditation, and deep breathing exercises. I also worked with a therapist to develop coping strategies for when I felt overwhelmed or stressed.

On the day of the exam, I arrived at the testing center feeling calm and focused. I was able to use the coping strategies I had developed to manage my anxiety and complete the exam successfully.

Question and Answer

Q: How much do technical certification exams cost?

A: The cost of technical certification exams varies depending on the exam and the certification provider. Prices can range from a few hundred to a few thousand dollars.

Q: Do I need to take courses to prepare for technical certification exams?

A: While taking courses can be helpful, it is not always necessary. There are many resources available, such as study guides and practice exams, that can help you prepare for the exams on your own.

Q: How long does it take to prepare for a technical certification exam?

A: The amount of time it takes to prepare for a technical certification exam varies depending on the exam and your level of experience. It can take anywhere from a few weeks to several months to prepare adequately.

Q: How often do I need to renew my technical certifications?

A: The renewal requirements for technical certifications vary depending on the certification and the provider. Some certifications require renewal every few years, while others may be valid for life.

Conclusion of How to Get Technical Certifications

Obtaining technical certifications can be a valuable investment in your career, providing you with increased job opportunities, higher earning potential, and professional recognition. By researching different certifications, investing in preparation, and staying focused during the exam, you can successfully earn the certifications you need to take your career to the next level.
