How to Prepare Your Vehicle for Shipping

Before your vehicle is transported, there are several steps you should take to prepare it for shipping. First, remove any personal belongings from the car, as they are not covered by insurance and could be damaged during transport. Second, wash the car and take pictures of any existing damage to document its condition before transport. Finally, make sure the car has a quarter tank of gas and is in good running condition.
