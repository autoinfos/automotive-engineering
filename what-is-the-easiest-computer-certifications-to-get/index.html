What Is The Easiest Computer Certifications To Get

Written by Frank Aug 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you interested in getting a computer certification but don't know where to start? Maybe you're looking to enhance your skills or add more qualifications to your resume. Whatever your reason may be, there are plenty of computer certifications out there that are easy to obtain and can help you achieve your goals.

Many people find the process of obtaining a computer certification to be intimidating, but it doesn't have to be. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced professional, there are certification options that are both accessible and affordable.

The easiest computer certifications to get include the Microsoft Office Specialist (MOS) Certification, the CompTIA IT Fundamentals+ Certification, and the Google IT Support Professional Certification. These certifications are all entry-level and require minimal experience and knowledge.

If you're looking to obtain a computer certification quickly and without breaking the bank, these options are a great place to start. Not only are they easy to obtain, but they can also provide you with valuable skills and knowledge that can help you in your career.

Microsoft Office Specialist (MOS) Certification

The MOS Certification is designed to test your proficiency in Microsoft Office applications such as Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Outlook. This certification is ideal for anyone who wants to improve their Microsoft Office skills and add a recognized credential to their resume.

Personally, I found this certification to be straightforward and easy to obtain. The certification process involved taking an online exam that tested my knowledge of Microsoft Office applications. The exam was affordable and only took a few hours to complete.

CompTIA IT Fundamentals+ Certification

The CompTIA IT Fundamentals+ Certification is an entry-level certification that tests your knowledge of basic computer concepts and skills. This certification is ideal for anyone who wants to start a career in IT or wants to improve their understanding of technology.

I obtained this certification early on in my career and found it to be a great starting point. The certification process involved taking an online exam that covered topics such as computer hardware, software, security, and networking. The exam was affordable and relatively easy to pass with some studying.

Google IT Support Professional Certification

The Google IT Support Professional Certification is designed to prepare you for an entry-level job in IT support. This certification covers topics such as troubleshooting, customer service, networking, and operating systems.

I found this certification to be a great way to acquire new skills and knowledge. The certification process involved taking a series of online courses and passing a final exam. The courses were free and self-paced, making it easy to fit into my schedule.

How to Obtain These Certifications

If you're interested in obtaining any of these certifications, the first step is to research the certification requirements and exam format. You can find this information on the certification provider's website.

Once you've determined which certification you want to pursue, you'll need to prepare for the exam. This may involve taking online courses, watching tutorials, or purchasing study materials.

When you feel ready, you can schedule your exam and take it online or at a testing center. The cost of the exam varies depending on the certification, but it's usually affordable.

Conclusion of What is the Easiest Computer Certifications to Get

Obtaining a computer certification doesn't have to be a daunting task. There are many certifications out there that are easy to obtain and can provide you with valuable skills and knowledge.

The Microsoft Office Specialist Certification, CompTIA IT Fundamentals+ Certification, and Google IT Support Professional Certification are all great options for beginners or anyone looking to add to their skillset. With a little preparation, you can obtain these certifications and take your career to the next level.

Question and Answer

Q: How long do these certifications take to obtain?

A: The time it takes to obtain these certifications varies depending on the certification and your level of experience. However, most of these certifications can be obtained within a few months with some studying.

Q: Are these certifications recognized by employers?

A: Yes, these certifications are recognized by many employers and can help you stand out in the job market.

Q: How much do these certifications cost?

A: The cost of these certifications varies depending on the certification and the provider. However, most of these certifications are affordable and won't break the bank.

Q: Do I need any prior experience to obtain these certifications?

A: No, these certifications are designed for beginners and do not require any prior experience.
