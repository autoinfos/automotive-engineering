Question and Answer

Q: How often should I get my brakes serviced?

A: You should have your brakes inspected at least once a year or whenever you notice any issues like strange noises or reduced braking performance.

Q: How much should I expect to pay for brake repairs?

A: The cost of brake repairs can vary depending on the type of repair needed and the make and model of your car. You should always get a clear quote for your repairs before any work is done.

Q: What are some signs that my brakes need to be serviced?

A: Some signs that your brakes may need to be serviced include strange noises when braking, reduced braking performance, or a soft or spongy brake pedal.
