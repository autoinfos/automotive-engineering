Designer Salary In Dubai How Much Can You Expect To Earn

Written by Daniel Jun 19, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're a designer in Dubai, you may be wondering how much you can expect to earn. It's no secret that the cost of living in Dubai is high, and salaries can vary greatly depending on your experience and the type of design work you do. In this post, we'll take a look at designer salaries in Dubai, and what you can expect to earn as a designer in this city.

As a designer in Dubai, you'll likely face some challenges when it comes to salary. Many companies in Dubai are known for offering low salaries, and designers are no exception. Additionally, there is often a lack of transparency when it comes to salary negotiations, which can make it difficult to determine what you should be earning.

So, how much can you expect to earn as a designer in Dubai? The answer depends on a number of factors, including your experience, the type of design work you do, and the company you work for. According to Payscale, the average salary for a graphic designer in Dubai is AED 67,000 per year, while a UX designer can expect to earn around AED 156,000 per year.

In conclusion, designer salaries in Dubai can vary greatly depending on a number of factors. If you're a designer in Dubai, it's important to do your research and negotiate your salary to ensure that you're being compensated fairly for your work.

What is the Average Designer Salary in Dubai?

According to Payscale, the average salary for a graphic designer in Dubai is AED 67,000 per year. This is lower than the global average for graphic designers, which is around $50,000 per year. However, the cost of living in Dubai is also higher than many other cities, which can impact salaries. Additionally, the type of design work you do can also impact your salary – for example, a UX designer can expect to earn around AED 156,000 per year in Dubai.

My Experience with Designer Salaries in Dubai

As a graphic designer in Dubai, I've found that negotiating salary can be a challenge. Many companies in Dubai are known for offering low salaries, and there is often a lack of transparency when it comes to salary negotiations. However, I've also found that there are some companies that are willing to pay competitive salaries for experienced designers.

How to Negotiate a Higher Designer Salary in Dubai

If you're a designer in Dubai, there are a few things you can do to negotiate a higher salary. First, do your research and find out what other designers in Dubai are earning. Use this information to determine what you should be earning based on your experience and the type of work you do.

Next, be prepared to negotiate. Don't be afraid to ask for a higher salary, and be prepared to explain why you believe you deserve it. Finally, be willing to walk away if you can't reach an agreement on salary – it's better to find a company that values your work and is willing to pay you what you're worth.
