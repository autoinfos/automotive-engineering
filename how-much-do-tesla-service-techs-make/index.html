How Much Do Tesla Service Techs Make

Written by Eveline Jun 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Thinking of becoming a Tesla service technician? One of the first questions that come to mind is how much do Tesla service techs make? It's no secret that Tesla is a highly successful company, but what does that mean for the salaries of its service technicians?

Working as a service technician for any car company can be a challenging job. Tesla service techs are responsible for repairing and maintaining some of the most advanced electric vehicles on the market. However, the job can also be highly rewarding, both financially and personally.

What is the Average Salary for a Tesla Service Technician?

The average salary for a Tesla service technician is around $60,000 per year. This is a highly competitive salary, especially considering that the average salary for a service technician in the United States is around $40,000 per year.

However, Tesla service technicians also have the opportunity to earn bonuses and commissions, which can push their salaries even higher. Additionally, Tesla offers a comprehensive benefits package that includes health insurance, retirement plans, and paid time off.

What Factors Affect the Salary of a Tesla Service Technician?

The salary of a Tesla service technician can vary depending on several factors, including their level of experience, location, and performance. Technicians who have been with the company for a longer period of time and have gained more experience tend to earn higher salaries. Additionally, technicians who work in areas with a higher cost of living may earn more.

Performance-based bonuses and commissions can also play a significant role in a Tesla service technician's overall compensation. Technicians who consistently meet or exceed their performance goals can earn substantial bonuses and commissions, which can significantly increase their salaries.

Is Working as a Tesla Service Technician a Good Career Choice?

Working as a Tesla service technician can be a highly rewarding career choice, both financially and personally. Tesla is a highly innovative and successful company, and service technicians have the opportunity to work with some of the most advanced electric vehicles on the market.

Additionally, Tesla offers comprehensive benefits packages and opportunities for career advancement. Service technicians who perform well and gain more experience can move up the ranks to become service managers or even service center directors.

How Can I Become a Tesla Service Technician?

If you're interested in becoming a Tesla service technician, you'll need to have a combination of technical skills and customer service experience. Tesla typically requires candidates to have a degree or certificate in automotive technology, as well as experience working as a service technician for another car company.

You can also increase your chances of being hired by networking with other Tesla employees and staying up-to-date with the latest advancements in electric vehicle technology.

Conclusion of How Much Do Tesla Service Techs Make

Overall, working as a Tesla service technician can be a highly rewarding career choice. With a competitive salary, comprehensive benefits package, and opportunities for career advancement, it's no wonder that so many people are interested in becoming Tesla service technicians.
