How Much Does An Auto Glass Tech Make

Written by Oliver Sep 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Have you ever wondered how much an auto glass tech makes? If you're considering a career in the field, or simply curious about the earning potential, this post will provide all the information you need.

Auto glass techs face several pain points when it comes to their salary. Some people believe that auto glass techs are underpaid compared to other skilled trades. Additionally, some may feel that the job is not worth the risk, as working with glass can be dangerous and result in injury.

So, how much does an auto glass tech make? According to Glassdoor, the average salary for an auto glass technician in the United States is around $42,000 per year. However, this can vary based on factors such as experience, location, and the company you work for.

In summary, the average salary for an auto glass tech is around $42,000 per year, but can vary based on several factors.

What Affects an Auto Glass Tech's Salary?

Several factors can affect an auto glass tech's salary, including:

Experience: Those with more experience tend to earn a higher salary.

Location: Salaries can vary depending on where you live and work. For example, auto glass techs in California tend to earn more than those in other states.

Company: Some companies may offer higher salaries and better benefits than others.

Based on my personal experience, I have found that location plays a significant role in an auto glass tech's salary. When I moved from a rural area to a larger city, I saw a significant increase in my salary.

Is Becoming an Auto Glass Tech Worth It?

Ultimately, the decision to become an auto glass tech depends on your personal goals and priorities. If you enjoy working with your hands and have an interest in cars, it can be a rewarding career. However, it is important to weigh the potential risks and the average salary before making a decision.

The Future of Auto Glass Tech Salaries

As the demand for skilled trades continues to grow, it is likely that salaries for auto glass techs will increase. Additionally, as technology advances, there may be new opportunities for auto glass techs to specialize in areas such as electric or self-driving cars.

How to Increase Your Auto Glass Tech Salary

If you are already working as an auto glass tech, there are several ways to increase your salary. One option is to seek out additional training and certifications, which can lead to higher pay and more job opportunities. Additionally, moving to a company that offers better benefits and salary can also be an option.

My Personal Experience

When I first started as an auto glass tech, I was making around $35,000 per year. However, after gaining more experience and moving to a larger city, my salary increased to over $50,000 per year. While the job can be challenging at times, I find it to be a fulfilling and rewarding career.

Question and Answer

Q: Do auto glass techs receive benefits?

A: Yes, many auto glass companies offer benefits such as health insurance, 401k matching, and paid time off.

Q: Is there room for advancement in the auto glass industry?

A: Yes, auto glass techs can move up to roles such as lead technician or manager.

Q: Are there any risks to working as an auto glass tech?

A: Yes, working with glass can be dangerous and result in cuts or other injuries. It is important for auto glass techs to follow proper safety protocols and wear protective gear.

Q: How does an auto glass tech's salary compare to other skilled trades?

A: Auto glass techs may earn less than some other skilled trades such as electricians or plumbers, but the earning potential can increase with experience and specialization.

Conclusion of How Much Does an Auto Glass Tech Make

Overall, the average salary for an auto glass tech is around $42,000 per year, but can vary based on several factors such as experience, location, and company. While the job can be challenging and potentially risky, it can also be a fulfilling and rewarding career with room for growth and advancement.
