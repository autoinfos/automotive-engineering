Types of Emirates Airline Engineering Jobs

Emirates Airline Engineering offers a diverse range of roles across various departments, including:

Aircraft Maintenance

Aircraft maintenance is one of the core functions of Emirates Airline Engineering, and involves ensuring that all aircraft are safe, reliable, and in compliance with regulatory standards. Roles in this field include Aircraft Maintenance Technician, Aircraft Mechanic, and Avionics Engineer.

Avionics Engineer

As an Avionics Engineer, your main responsibility is to ensure that the electrical and electronic systems of the aircraft are functioning correctly. This includes everything from navigation and communication systems to cockpit displays and entertainment systems. You will need to have a degree in electrical or electronics engineering, as well as experience in aircraft maintenance and repair.

Qualifications and Skills for Emirates Airline Engineering Jobs

To be considered for a role in Emirates Airline Engineering, you will need to have the following qualifications and skills:

Education and Certification

Most roles in Emirates Airline Engineering require a degree in engineering or a related field, such as mechanical or electrical engineering. You may also need to have specific certifications, such as an FAA Aircraft Maintenance Technician certificate or an EASA Part 66 license.

Experience

In addition to education and certification, you will need to have relevant work experience in the aviation industry. This can include internships, apprenticeships, or previous employment in aircraft maintenance or repair.
