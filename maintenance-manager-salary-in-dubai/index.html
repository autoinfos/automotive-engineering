So, what can you expect in terms of maintenance manager salary in Dubai? The truth is, it varies depending on a variety of factors. Factors That Affect Maintenance Manager Salary in Dubai

When it comes to maintenance manager salary in Dubai, there are several factors that can affect how much you earn. Here are some of the most important factors to consider:

Years of experience: As with most roles, the more experience you have, the higher your salary is likely to be. This is because you bring more value to the company and can take on more responsibility.

Industry: Depending on the industry you work in, your salary may be higher or lower. For example, maintenance managers in the oil and gas industry tend to earn more than those in the hospitality industry.

Company size: Larger companies tend to pay higher salaries than smaller companies, as they have more resources to allocate to employee salaries.

Qualifications: If you have additional qualifications or certifications, such as a degree in engineering or a certification in project management, you may be able to command a higher salary.
