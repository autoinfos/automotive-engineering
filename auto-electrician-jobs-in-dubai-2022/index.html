What is an Auto Electrician?

An auto electrician is a skilled mechanic who specializes in electrical systems in vehicles. This can include anything from the battery and starter motor to the lights and sound systems. Auto electricians are responsible for diagnosing and repairing electrical faults in vehicles, as well as installing and maintaining electrical systems.
