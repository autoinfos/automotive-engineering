Assistant Sales Manager Car Dealership Job Description What You Need To Know

Written by William Sep 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering a career as an assistant sales manager at a car dealership? This can be an exciting and rewarding job, but it can also come with challenges. In this post, we'll explore what the job entails, what skills you'll need to succeed, and how to excel in this role.

As an assistant sales manager at a car dealership, you'll be responsible for a variety of tasks. You'll work closely with the sales team to ensure that customers have a positive experience, from the moment they walk in the door to the moment they drive off the lot. You'll also be responsible for managing inventory, setting sales goals, and training new salespeople.

To succeed in this role, you'll need a variety of skills. You should be able to communicate effectively with customers and colleagues, be a strong leader, and have excellent organizational and time management skills. You'll also need to be comfortable working in a fast-paced environment and be able to handle multiple tasks at once.

What Does the Job Entail?

As we mentioned earlier, the job of an assistant sales manager at a car dealership involves a variety of tasks. One of your primary responsibilities will be to work with the sales team to ensure that customers have a positive experience. This will involve greeting customers, answering their questions, and helping them find the right vehicle for their needs and budget.

In addition to working with customers, you'll also be responsible for managing inventory. This will involve keeping track of the cars on the lot, ordering new inventory when necessary, and making sure that vehicles are properly cleaned and maintained.

Another important aspect of the job is setting sales goals. You'll work with the sales team to set monthly and yearly sales targets, and you'll be responsible for tracking progress and making adjustments as needed. You'll also be responsible for training new salespeople and helping them develop the skills they need to succeed.

Skills You'll Need to Succeed

To excel as an assistant sales manager at a car dealership, you'll need a variety of skills. Here are some of the most important:

Communication Skills

You'll need to be able to communicate effectively with customers and colleagues. This means being able to listen actively, ask questions, and provide clear and concise information.

Leadership Skills

You'll be responsible for leading a team of salespeople, so you'll need to be a strong leader. This means being able to inspire and motivate your team, set clear expectations, and hold people accountable.

Organizational Skills

You'll be responsible for managing inventory, setting sales goals, and training new salespeople, so you'll need to have excellent organizational skills. This means being able to prioritize tasks, manage time effectively, and stay on top of multiple projects at once.

Customer Service Skills

You'll be working with customers every day, so you'll need to have excellent customer service skills. This means being able to build rapport with customers, understand their needs and concerns, and provide solutions that meet their needs.

How to Excel in This Role

If you want to excel as an assistant sales manager at a car dealership, there are a few things you can do:

Set Clear Expectations

Make sure that your team knows what is expected of them. Set clear goals and expectations, and provide regular feedback to help your team improve.

Lead by Example

If you want your team to be successful, you need to lead by example. Set an example for your team by being a hard worker, showing up on time, and being a positive role model.

Stay Up-to-Date

The automotive industry is constantly changing, so it's important to stay up-to-date on the latest trends, technologies, and best practices. Attend industry conferences, read industry publications, and network with other professionals to stay ahead of the curve.

Question and Answer

Q: What kind of education do I need to become an assistant sales manager at a car dealership?

A: While there's no specific education requirement for this role, many employers prefer candidates with a bachelor's degree in business, marketing, or a related field.

Q: What kind of training will I receive as an assistant sales manager?

A: Most dealerships provide on-the-job training for new assistant sales managers. You'll work closely with experienced managers to learn the ins and outs of the job.

Q: What kind of salary can I expect as an assistant sales manager?

A: According to Glassdoor, the average salary for an assistant sales manager at a car dealership is $47,500 per year.

Q: What kind of career advancement opportunities are available?

A: With experience, you may be able to advance to a higher-level management position, such as general sales manager or general manager.

Conclusion

Being an assistant sales manager at a car dealership can be a challenging and rewarding job. To succeed in this role, you'll need excellent communication, leadership, organizational, and customer service skills. By setting clear expectations, leading by example, and staying up-to-date on industry trends, you can excel in this exciting field.
