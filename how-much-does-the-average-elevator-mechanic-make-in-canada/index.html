How Much Does The Average Elevator Mechanic Make In Canada

Written by Bardi Nov 13, 2023 · 5 min read

Have you ever wondered how much elevator mechanics make in Canada? It’s a question that many people have, especially those who are considering a career in the elevator industry. While becoming an elevator mechanic can be a lucrative career path, there are also some challenges that come with the job.

One of the biggest pain points for elevator mechanics is the physical demands of the job. Elevator mechanics are required to work in confined spaces and at heights, which can be stressful and dangerous. Additionally, the job can be physically demanding, requiring mechanics to lift heavy equipment and work in awkward positions for extended periods of time.

The average elevator mechanic in Canada makes around $70,000 per year. However, this can vary depending on a number of different factors, including experience, location, and the company you work for. In general, elevator mechanics who work in larger cities tend to make more money than those who work in smaller towns or rural areas.

In summary, becoming an elevator mechanic in Canada can be a lucrative career, but it also comes with its own set of challenges. It’s important to consider the physical demands of the job and the location where you plan to work when deciding whether or not this is the right career path for you.
