Question and Answer

Q1. What are the different types of motor insurance policies in the UAE?
A1. The different types of motor insurance policies in the UAE include third-party liability insurance, comprehensive insurance, and personal accident insurance.

Q2. What is third-party liability insurance?
A2. Third-party liability insurance covers the policyholder against any legal liability arising from damage to third-party property or injury to third-party individuals.
