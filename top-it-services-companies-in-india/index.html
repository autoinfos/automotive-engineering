One of the top IT services companies in India is Tata Consultancy Services (TCS). Founded in 1968, TCS is the largest IT services provider in India and has a global presence in over 46 countries. The company offers a wide range of services, including application development and maintenance, infrastructure management, and digital transformation.

Personally, I have worked with TCS on several projects over the years, and I can attest to their professionalism, expertise, and commitment to delivering high-quality solutions. TCS has a team of over 469,000 employees worldwide, and their experience and expertise in the industry are unparalleled.

Another top IT services company in India is Infosys. Founded in 1981, Infosys is a global leader in consulting, technology, and outsourcing solutions. How can you choose the right IT services company in India?

Choosing the right IT services company in India can be a daunting task, but there are a few key factors to consider.
