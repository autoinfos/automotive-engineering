If you want to become a red seal carpenter in Ontario, you need to follow a few simple steps. Here's how you can do it:

To become a red seal carpenter in Ontario, you need to complete a carpentry apprenticeship program. The apprenticeship program will teach you the skills and knowledge you need to become a carpenter. After completing your apprenticeship, you need to pass the certification exam. Once you pass the exam, you will be a certified red seal carpenter in Ontario.

Requirements for the Apprenticeship Program

To qualify for a carpentry apprenticeship program in Ontario, you need to meet certain requirements. These requirements may vary depending on the program, but generally include:

  • Having a valid driver's license

If you meet these requirements, you can start looking for a carpentry apprenticeship program in Ontario.
