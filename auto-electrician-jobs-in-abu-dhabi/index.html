What is an Auto Electrician?

An auto electrician is a professional who specializes in the electrical systems of vehicles. This includes everything from the battery and starter motor to the audio and navigation systems, as well as the wiring and circuitry that connects them. Auto electricians are responsible for ensuring the safety and reliability of these systems, as well as diagnosing and repairing any issues that arise.
