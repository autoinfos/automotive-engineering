Interior Design Salary In Dubai A Guide To Earning Potential

Written by Robby Oct 29, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you considering a career in interior design in Dubai? Or are you an experienced designer wondering if your salary is competitive? Look no further, as we delve into the world of interior design salary in Dubai.

Interior design is a highly competitive field, and salary is a common concern for both beginners and experienced designers. Dubai, being a hub for luxurious architecture and design, offers an extensive range of opportunities for aspiring designers. However, the high cost of living and competition for jobs can make it challenging to earn a decent salary.

So, what can you expect to earn as an interior designer in Dubai? According to Payscale.com, the average salary for an interior designer in Dubai is AED 97,000 per year. However, salaries can vary depending on factors such as experience, education, job position, and company size.

In general, junior designers can expect to earn between AED 50,000 to AED 70,000 per year, while senior designers can earn between AED 120,000 to AED 200,000 per year. Freelance designers can earn anywhere from AED 100 to AED 500 per hour, depending on their experience and reputation.

My Personal Experience with Interior Design Salary in Dubai

As a freelance interior designer in Dubai, I have found that salaries can vary greatly depending on the project and client. Some projects offer a fixed fee, while others pay an hourly rate. It is important to negotiate your fee and ensure that you are getting paid fairly for your time and expertise.

Networking and building relationships with clients and other designers can also lead to higher-paying opportunities. It is essential to stay up-to-date with industry trends and continuously improve your skills to stand out in a competitive market.

How to Increase Your Interior Design Salary in Dubai

To increase your earning potential as an interior designer in Dubai, consider the following tips:

Specialize in a Niche Area

Specializing in a particular niche, such as sustainable design or hospitality design, can make you a sought-after expert in your field, leading to higher-paying opportunities.

Continuously Improve Your Skills

Attend workshops, seminars, and courses to stay up-to-date with the latest design trends and techniques. This will make you more valuable to clients and lead to higher-paying projects.

Salary Negotiation in Dubai

When negotiating your salary in Dubai, it is essential to research industry standards and be realistic about your experience and qualifications. Be confident in your skills and be prepared to negotiate for a fair salary.

Conclusion of Interior Design Salary in Dubai

Interior design can be a lucrative career in Dubai, but it requires hard work, dedication, and continuous learning. By specializing in a niche area, continuously improving your skills, and negotiating your salary, you can increase your earning potential in this competitive field.
