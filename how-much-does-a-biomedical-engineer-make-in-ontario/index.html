One of the biggest pain points for biomedical engineers in Ontario is the high competition for jobs. The average salary for a biomedical engineer in Ontario is around $75,000 to $80,000 per year. In summary, the salary for a biomedical engineer in Ontario can range from $75,000 to $100,000 or more per year, depending on various factors. The field offers a promising career path with ample opportunities for growth and development.
