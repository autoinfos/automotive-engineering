Is Pharmacy Technician A Good Job

Written by Bardi Oct 19, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you considering a career as a pharmacy technician? Do you wonder if it's a good job or not? If you're looking for a stable job with good pay and benefits, then read on to find out more about becoming a pharmacy technician.

As with any job, there are pros and cons to being a pharmacy technician. Some of the pain points that people might mention include long hours on your feet, high stress levels, and dealing with difficult customers. However, if you enjoy working in a fast-paced environment and helping people, then becoming a pharmacy technician could be a great fit for you.

The short answer is yes, being a pharmacy technician can be a good job. According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median pay for pharmacy technicians in 2020 was $34,020 per year or $16.35 per hour. The job outlook is also positive, with a projected growth rate of 4% from 2019 to 2029.

In summary, becoming a pharmacy technician can be a good job choice for those interested in the field. With good pay and benefits, stable job growth, and opportunities for advancement, it's a career worth considering.

What Does a Pharmacy Technician Do?

As a pharmacy technician, you will work under the supervision of a licensed pharmacist. Your job duties will include:

1. Filling prescriptions accurately and efficiently

2. Counting pills and measuring medications

3. Labeling prescriptions and ensuring they are correct

4. Maintaining patient records and insurance information

5. Assisting customers with questions and concerns

My Personal Experience as a Pharmacy Technician

I have been working as a pharmacy technician for the past six years and have found it to be a rewarding career. While the job can be stressful at times, I enjoy the fast-paced environment and the opportunity to help people. I have also had the chance to advance in my career by becoming certified and taking on more responsibilities.

The Benefits of Being a Pharmacy Technician

One of the main benefits of being a pharmacy technician is the pay. With a median salary of over $34,000 per year, it's a good-paying job that can provide financial stability. Additionally, most pharmacy technician jobs offer benefits such as health insurance, paid time off, and retirement plans.

Advancement Opportunities

Another benefit of being a pharmacy technician is the opportunity for advancement. With additional education and certification, you can become a certified pharmacy technician or even a licensed pharmacist. There are also opportunities for management positions within the pharmacy.

How to Become a Pharmacy Technician

To become a pharmacy technician, you typically need a high school diploma or equivalent. Some states also require certification or licensure. You can obtain certification by taking an exam through organizations such as the Pharmacy Technician Certification Board or the National Healthcareer Association.

Is Pharmacy Technician a Good Job? - Q&A

Q: What are the hours like for a pharmacy technician?

A: Pharmacy technicians typically work full-time or part-time hours, which may include evenings, weekends, and holidays.

Q: What skills do I need to become a pharmacy technician?

A: Some of the skills that are important for pharmacy technicians include attention to detail, good communication skills, and the ability to work well under pressure.

Q: Is the job outlook good for pharmacy technicians?

A: Yes, the job outlook is positive, with a projected growth rate of 4% from 2019 to 2029.

Q: What are some of the challenges of being a pharmacy technician?

A: Some of the challenges of being a pharmacy technician include dealing with difficult customers, working long hours on your feet, and handling stressful situations.

Conclusion of Is Pharmacy Technician a Good Job

Becoming a pharmacy technician can be a good job choice for those interested in the field. With good pay and benefits, stable job growth, and opportunities for advancement, it's a career worth considering. While there are challenges to the job, many people find it to be a rewarding and fulfilling career.
