What to Look for in a Radiology Tech Program in Utah

When you're searching for a radiology tech program in Utah, there are several important factors to consider. First and foremost, you'll want to make sure that the program is accredited by the Joint Review Committee on Education in Radiologic Technology (JRCERT). Accreditation ensures that the program meets high standards of quality and that you'll be eligible to sit for the certification exam after graduation.

In addition to accreditation, you'll also want to consider factors such as cost, location, and program length. Some programs may be more expensive than others, while others may be more convenient for students who live in certain areas of the state. It's also important to consider the length of the program and whether it fits your schedule and career goals.
