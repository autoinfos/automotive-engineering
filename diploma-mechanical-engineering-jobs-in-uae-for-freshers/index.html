Diploma Mechanical Engineering Jobs in UAE for Freshers: The Ultimate Guide to Kick-starting Your Career in 2021

If you are a fresh graduate in mechanical engineering, finding a job can be an uphill task. The competition in the job market is stiff, and getting a foot in the door can be daunting. However, with the right information, it is possible to land your dream diploma mechanical engineering job in UAE.

First, you need to understand that the Gulf region is a hotbed of mechanical engineering jobs. The booming construction industry, oil and gas sector, and numerous manufacturing plants require mechanical engineers to keep their operations running smoothly. This demand has created numerous job opportunities for fresh graduates.

In this ultimate guide, we will explore everything you need to know about diploma mechanical engineering jobs in UAE for freshers. We will cover the job market, the best companies to work for, and tips on how to land your dream job. Read on to learn more.

Diploma Mechanical Engineering Jobs in UAE for Freshers: Job Market Overview

The job market in the UAE is promising for mechanical engineers. The country has a rapidly growing economy, and the government is heavily investing in infrastructure projects such as airports, highways, and railways. This has resulted in a high demand for mechanical engineers who can work on these projects.

Additionally, the UAE is home to numerous oil and gas companies, which are always in need of mechanical engineers to operate and maintain their machinery. The manufacturing sector is also growing, and mechanical engineers are in demand in this sector as well.

How to Land Your Dream Diploma Mechanical Engineering Job in UAE

Here are some tips for fresh graduates looking to land their dream diploma mechanical engineering job in UAE:

1. Network

Networking is crucial in the UAE job market. Attend job fairs, join professional organizations, and connect with people on LinkedIn. Building relationships can help you learn about job openings and get referrals.

2. Tailor Your CV and Cover Letter

Make sure your CV and cover letter are tailored to the job you are applying for. Highlight your skills and experience that match the job requirements.

3. Be Prepared for Interviews

Research the company and the job you are applying for. Prepare answers to common interview questions and practice your responses.

Best Companies to Work for in UAE

Here are some of the best companies to work for in UAE as a mechanical engineer:

1. Emirates Global Aluminium

Emirates Global Aluminium is a leading aluminium producer in the UAE. The company has a strong commitment to sustainability and innovation and offers numerous job opportunities for mechanical engineers.

2. Abu Dhabi National Oil Company

Abu Dhabi National Oil Company is the largest oil company in the UAE. The company offers numerous job opportunities for mechanical engineers in the oil and gas sector.

3. Dubai Electricity and Water Authority

Dubai Electricity and Water Authority is responsible for providing electricity and water to the residents of Dubai. The company offers numerous job opportunities for mechanical engineers to work on their infrastructure projects.

Question and Answer

1. What is the average salary for a diploma mechanical engineer in UAE?

The average salary for a diploma mechanical engineer in UAE is around AED 60,000 per year.

2. What skills do I need to have to get a diploma mechanical engineering job in UAE?

You should have a strong understanding of mechanical engineering principles, good problem-solving skills, and proficiency in technical software.

3. What are the working hours like for diploma mechanical engineers in UAE?

Working hours for diploma mechanical engineers in UAE are typically 8-10 hours per day, 5-6 days a week.

4. Do I need a UAE driving license to work as a mechanical engineer in UAE?

Not necessarily, but having a UAE driving license can be an added advantage as some jobs may require traveling.

Conclusion of Diploma Mechanical Engineering Jobs in UAE for Freshers

Landing a diploma mechanical engineering job in UAE for freshers may seem daunting, but with the right information and strategies, it is possible. The UAE job market is promising for mechanical engineers, and there are numerous job opportunities available. Follow the tips outlined in this guide, and you will be on your way to a successful career in mechanical engineering.
