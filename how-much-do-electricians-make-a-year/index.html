Have you ever considered becoming an electrician? If so, you may be wondering how much money you could make in this profession. So, how much do electricians make a year? According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median annual salary for electricians was $56,180 as of May 2020. However, it's important to note that this figure can vary quite a bit depending on where you live and work.

Factors That Affect Electrician Salaries

There are a number of factors that can affect how much money an electrician can make. Some of the most important include:
