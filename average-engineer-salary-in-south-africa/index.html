Are you curious about the average engineer salary in South Africa? Do you want to know how much you can earn as an engineer in this country? If so, then you’ve come to the right place. The average engineer salary in South Africa varies depending on a number of factors, including your level of experience, your qualifications, and the industry you work in. In conclusion, the average engineer salary in South Africa is R390,000 per year, but this figure can vary depending on a number of factors.
