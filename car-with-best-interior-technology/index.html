This means you can adjust the temperature, play music, and even browse the internet all from the comfort of your driver’s seat.

And if you’re worried about range anxiety, the Model S has a range of up to 402 miles on a single charge, making it perfect for long road trips.

Question and Answer

Q: How much does the Tesla Model S cost?

A: The price of the Tesla Model S varies depending on the model and features you choose. The base model starts at around $75,000, while the high-end models can cost over $100,000.

Q: How long does it take to charge the Tesla Model S?

A: The charging time for the Tesla Model S depends on the charger you use. With a Supercharger, you can get up to 200 miles of range in as little as 15 minutes. With a standard home charger, it can take up to 10 hours to fully charge the battery.

Q: Can you control the Tesla Model S with your phone?

A: Yes, the Tesla Model S comes with a mobile app that allows you to control various features of the car, such as locking and unlocking the doors, adjusting the temperature, and even starting the car remotely.

Q: Is the Tesla Model S safe?

A: Yes, the Tesla Model S is one of the safest cars on the road. It has earned a 5-star safety rating from the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration and has been praised for its advanced safety features, such as its Autopilot system.
