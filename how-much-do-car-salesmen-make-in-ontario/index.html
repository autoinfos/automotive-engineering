How Much Do Car Salesmen Make In Ontario

Written by Eveline Jun 01, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you considering a career in car sales but wondering how much money you can actually make in Ontario? You're not alone. Many people are attracted to the idea of selling cars, but are unsure if it's a financially viable career choice.

One of the biggest pain points for those considering a career in car sales is the uncertainty around earning potential. Without knowing how much you can realistically make, it's difficult to make an informed decision about whether or not to pursue this career path.

The answer to the question of how much car salesmen make in Ontario is not a simple one. Salaries can vary widely based on a number of factors, including experience, location, and the dealership you work for. However, we can provide some general guidelines to give you an idea of what you might expect to earn.

In general, car salesmen in Ontario can expect to earn a base salary of around $30,000-$40,000 per year. However, this is just the starting point. In addition to their base salary, car salesmen can earn commissions on the cars they sell. This commission can range from 5-20% of the sale price of the car, depending on the dealership and the specific salesperson.

To maximize their earnings, car salesmen will often work long hours and weekends in order to be available when potential customers are looking to buy. They may also need to work hard to build a loyal customer base who will come back to them when it's time to buy a new car.

The Importance of Negotiation Skills in Car Sales

One of the key factors that will impact how much money you can make in car sales is your ability to negotiate effectively with customers. The more skilled you are at convincing customers to buy, the more cars you will sell, and the more money you will make.

When I first started out in car sales, I struggled with negotiation. I found it difficult to know when to push and when to back off, and I often lost sales as a result. However, over time I learned some valuable tactics for negotiating effectively, and my sales (and earnings) started to increase.

The Importance of Building Relationships in Car Sales

Another important factor in car sales is the ability to build strong relationships with customers. When customers feel like they can trust you and rely on you, they are more likely to buy from you and refer their friends and family to you as well.

One of the best ways to build these relationships is to be genuinely interested in your customers and their needs. Ask questions, listen carefully to their answers, and work hard to find the perfect car for them. When customers feel like you're truly invested in helping them, they are much more likely to become loyal customers for life.

Other Factors that Impact Earnings in Car Sales

In addition to negotiation skills and relationship building, there are other factors that can impact how much money you can make in car sales. Some of these include:

  • The type of cars you're selling
  • The location of the dealership
  • The commission rate offered by the dealership
  • The number of cars you sell each month/quarter/year

Tips for Maximizing Your Earnings in Car Sales

If you're serious about making a career in car sales, there are some tips you can follow to maximize your earnings. These include:

  • Invest in your negotiation skills by taking courses or practicing with a mentor
  • Build strong relationships with customers by being genuinely interested in their needs
  • Work hard to build a loyal customer base who will come back to you when it's time to buy a new car
  • Consider specializing in selling high-end or luxury cars, which can offer higher commissions

Question and Answer

Q: What is the average salary for a car salesman in Ontario?

A: Car salesmen in Ontario can expect to earn a base salary of around $30,000-$40,000 per year.

Q: How much can car salesmen make in commissions?

A: Commissions can range from 5-20% of the sale price of the car, depending on the dealership and the specific salesperson.

Q: What are some factors that can impact how much money a car salesman can make?

A: Factors that can impact earnings include the type of cars being sold, the location of the dealership, the commission rate offered by the dealership, and the number of cars sold each month/quarter/year.

Q: What are some tips for maximizing earnings in car sales?

A: Tips include investing in negotiation skills, building strong relationships with customers, working hard to build a loyal customer base, and considering specializing in selling high-end or luxury cars.

Conclusion of How Much Do Car Salesmen Make in Ontario

While there is no one-size-fits-all answer to the question of how much money car salesmen can make in Ontario, it's clear that there is potential for significant earnings in this field. By investing in negotiation skills, building strong relationships with customers, and working hard to build a loyal customer base, car salesmen can maximize their earnings and build a successful career in this exciting industry.
