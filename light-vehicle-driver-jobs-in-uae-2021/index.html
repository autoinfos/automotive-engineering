Light Vehicle Driver Jobs In Uae 2021 Opportunities And Challenges

Are you looking for a job as a light vehicle driver in UAE? With the country's booming economy, there are plenty of opportunities for drivers to earn a living. However, the job comes with its own set of challenges. Read on to learn more about light vehicle driver jobs in UAE 2021 and what you can expect if you choose to pursue this career.

Driving in UAE can be a challenging experience, especially for those who are not familiar with the roads and traffic rules. The hot climate and long working hours can also take a toll on drivers' health and well-being. Additionally, the competition for jobs is high, and many employers require drivers to have a valid UAE driving license and a minimum of two years of experience.

The target of light vehicle driver jobs in UAE 2021 is anyone who is interested in pursuing a career as a driver in the country. Whether you are a local resident or an expatriate, there are opportunities available if you meet the required qualifications. The job can be a good fit for those who enjoy driving and have a passion for exploring new places.

In summary, light vehicle driver jobs in UAE 2021 offer both opportunities and challenges. While the job can provide a steady income and a chance to explore the country, it also comes with its own set of difficulties. If you are interested in pursuing this career, it is important to be aware of the requirements and the realities of the job.

Driving for Ride-Hailing Services: A Personal Experience

As an expatriate living in UAE, I was looking for a job that would allow me to explore the country while earning a living. I decided to become a driver for a ride-hailing service and found the job to be both rewarding and challenging. On one hand, I had the flexibility to choose my own hours and work in different parts of the city. On the other hand, I had to deal with heavy traffic and long hours in the scorching heat.

One of the main benefits of driving for a ride-hailing service is that you can earn a decent income without having to invest in your own vehicle. The company provides the car and covers the cost of maintenance and insurance. However, you have to pay a commission to the company for each ride you complete.

Working as a Delivery Driver: Another Option to Consider

Another option for those interested in light vehicle driver jobs in UAE 2021 is working as a delivery driver for a local company. This job requires you to transport goods and packages from one location to another, often within a specific time frame. It can be a good fit for those who prefer a more structured work environment and enjoy working independently.

One of the challenges of working as a delivery driver is dealing with heavy traffic and navigating through unfamiliar areas. You also have to be physically fit and able to lift heavy packages. However, the job can provide a steady income and a chance to work for a reputable company.

Requirements for Light Vehicle Driver Jobs in UAE 2021

To be eligible for light vehicle driver jobs in UAE 2021, you must have a valid UAE driving license and a minimum of two years of driving experience. Some employers may also require you to have a clean driving record and a good command of English or Arabic.

Additionally, you must be able to work long hours and in hot weather conditions. You should also be familiar with the traffic rules and regulations in UAE and have good communication and customer service skills.

How to Find Light Vehicle Driver Jobs in UAE 2021

If you are interested in pursuing a career as a light vehicle driver in UAE, there are several ways to find job opportunities. You can search online job portals, such as Bayt, GulfTalent, and Naukrigulf, or visit local recruitment agencies that specialize in driver jobs.

You can also consider working for ride-hailing services, such as Uber or Careem, or local delivery companies, such as Aramex or Fetchr. These companies are always looking for reliable and experienced drivers to join their team.

FAQs about Light Vehicle Driver Jobs in UAE 2021

Q: What is the average salary for a light vehicle driver in UAE?
A: The average salary for a light vehicle driver in UAE ranges from AED 2,000 to AED 4,000 per month, depending on the company and the type of job.

Q: What are the working hours for a light vehicle driver in UAE?
A: The working hours for a light vehicle driver in UAE can vary depending on the type of job and the company. Some drivers work eight-hour shifts, while others work 12-hour shifts or longer.

Q: What are the benefits of working as a light vehicle driver in UAE?
A: Some of the benefits of working as a light vehicle driver in UAE include a steady income, the opportunity to explore the country, and the chance to work for a reputable company.

Q: What are the challenges of working as a light vehicle driver in UAE?
A: Some of the challenges of working as a light vehicle driver in UAE include dealing with heavy traffic, working long hours in hot weather conditions, and navigating through unfamiliar areas.

Conclusion of Light Vehicle Driver Jobs in UAE 2021

Light vehicle driver jobs in UAE 2021 offer plenty of opportunities for those interested in pursuing a career as a driver. However, the job comes with its own set of challenges, including heavy traffic, long working hours, and competition for jobs. If you are interested in becoming a driver in UAE, it is important to be aware of the requirements and the realities of the job before you apply.
