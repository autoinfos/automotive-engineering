Question and Answer

Q. What is the average salary for a light vehicle driver in Abu Dhabi?
A. The average salary for a light vehicle driver in Abu Dhabi is AED 3000 to AED 5000 per month.

Q. What are the qualifications required to become a light vehicle driver in Abu Dhabi?
A. To become a light vehicle driver in Abu Dhabi, you must have a valid UAE driving license, knowledge of traffic rules and regulations, and excellent driving skills.

Q. What are the working hours for a light vehicle driver in Abu Dhabi?
A. The working hours for a light vehicle driver in Abu Dhabi vary depending on the employer and sector. However, most employers require their employees to work 8 to 10 hours per day.
