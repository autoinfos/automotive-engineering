Vehicle Product Specialist Job Description What You Need To Know

Written by Bardi Aug 01, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you passionate about cars and have a knack for sales? Do you enjoy working with people and helping them find the perfect vehicle to fit their lifestyle? If so, you may be interested in becoming a vehicle product specialist. However, before you jump into this career path, it's important to understand what the job entails and what skills and experience are required.

One of the biggest pain points of being a vehicle product specialist is that it's a highly competitive field. To be successful, you need to have a deep understanding of the automotive industry, including the latest trends and technologies. You also need to be able to build strong relationships with customers and be able to communicate effectively with them about the features and benefits of different vehicles.

The target of a vehicle product specialist job description is to help customers find the right vehicle to meet their needs and budget. This involves conducting test drives, answering questions about vehicle features and specifications, and providing guidance and advice throughout the sales process.

In summary, a vehicle product specialist is responsible for providing expert advice to customers looking to purchase a new or used vehicle. They must have a deep understanding of the automotive industry and be able to build strong relationships with customers.

What Does a Vehicle Product Specialist Do?

As a vehicle product specialist, I have had the opportunity to work with a wide range of customers, from first-time buyers to seasoned car enthusiasts. My job is to help customers find the perfect vehicle to fit their lifestyle and budget, and to provide guidance and advice throughout the sales process.

One of the most important aspects of my job is conducting test drives. This allows customers to experience the vehicle firsthand and get a sense of how it handles on the road. I also provide information about the features and specifications of different vehicles, and help customers compare and contrast different models to find the one that best meets their needs.

What Skills and Experience Are Required?

To be successful as a vehicle product specialist, you need to have a deep understanding of the automotive industry, including the latest trends and technologies. You also need to be able to build strong relationships with customers and be able to communicate effectively with them about the features and benefits of different vehicles.

Additionally, you need to have strong sales skills, including the ability to identify customer needs and provide solutions that meet those needs. You also need to be able to work independently and manage your time effectively.

How to Become a Vehicle Product Specialist

To become a vehicle product specialist, you typically need to have a high school diploma or equivalent. Some employers may also require a college degree in a related field, such as marketing or business.

Most vehicle product specialists receive on-the-job training, which typically includes learning about the features and specifications of different vehicles, as well as sales techniques and customer service skills.

How Much Can You Earn as a Vehicle Product Specialist?

The salary for a vehicle product specialist can vary depending on a number of factors, including your level of experience, the size of the dealership you work for, and the location of the dealership. According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median annual wage for retail sales workers, which includes vehicle product specialists, was $25,760 in May 2020.

Is a Career as a Vehicle Product Specialist Right for You?

If you're passionate about cars and enjoy working with people, a career as a vehicle product specialist may be a great fit for you. However, it's important to keep in mind that this is a highly competitive field, and you will need to have a deep understanding of the automotive industry and strong sales skills to be successful.
