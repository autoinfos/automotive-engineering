Question and Answer

Q: What kinds of refrigeration systems do maintenance technicians typically work on?

A: Maintenance technicians in Kenya today work on a wide range of refrigeration systems, including commercial refrigerators, freezers, walk-in coolers, and more.

Q: How often should businesses schedule maintenance for their refrigeration systems?

A: It's generally recommended that businesses schedule maintenance for their refrigeration systems at least twice a year, or more frequently for systems that see heavy use.

Q: What are some common issues that maintenance technicians encounter when working on refrigeration systems?

A: Some common issues include refrigerant leaks, compressor failures, thermostat problems, and electrical issues.

Q: How can businesses minimize downtime when their refrigeration system needs repairs?

A: To minimize downtime, businesses can work with a maintenance technician to develop a plan for emergency repairs and have a backup refrigeration system on hand if possible.
