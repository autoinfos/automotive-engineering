How Much Do Certified Mechanics Make A Year

Written by Daniel Jul 14, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you interested in becoming a certified mechanic but unsure of how much money you could potentially earn? The salary of a mechanic is a common concern for those considering a career in this field.

Many factors can affect the salary of a certified mechanic, including location, experience, and industry. However, the average annual salary for a mechanic in the United States is around $42,000 to $55,000 per year.

In addition to a base salary, many mechanics may receive overtime pay, bonuses, and commissions. This can significantly increase their overall yearly earnings.

To earn a higher salary as a mechanic, it's important to gain experience and training in specialized areas such as diesel engines, hybrid vehicles, or specific car brands. Mechanics who work for larger companies or dealerships may also have greater earning potential.

My Experience

As a certified mechanic with five years of experience, I can attest to the fact that earning potential increases with experience and specialized training. I started my career earning around $40,000 per year, but after obtaining additional certifications and working for a larger dealership, my salary has increased to over $60,000 per year.

How Can You Increase Your Earnings?

If you're interested in increasing your earnings as a certified mechanic, consider pursuing specialized training and certifications in areas such as diesel engines or specific car brands. Additionally, working for larger companies or dealerships can provide greater earning potential.

The Importance of Location

It's important to note that location can also significantly affect a mechanic's salary. Mechanics in larger cities or areas with a higher cost of living may earn a higher salary compared to those in smaller towns or rural areas.

Negotiating Your Salary

When starting a new job or negotiating a salary increase, it's important to do your research and be prepared to discuss your experience, certifications, and specialized training. Don't be afraid to ask for what you're worth and make sure to have a clear understanding of the job's responsibilities and expectations.

Conclusion

While the salary of a certified mechanic can vary based on numerous factors, the average annual salary in the United States falls between $42,000 to $55,000 per year. However, with experience, specialized training, and location, it's possible to increase your earning potential in this rewarding career field.
