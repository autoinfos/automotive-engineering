How do Automotive AC Systems Work?

An automotive AC system is a complex system that consists of several components, including the compressor, condenser, evaporator, and expansion valve. The system works by compressing and expanding refrigerant gas to remove heat from the air inside the car and transfer it outside. The refrigerant gas is then cooled and compressed again, and the cycle continues.
