Building Maintenance Technician Salary In Dubai What You Need To Know

Written by Bardi Jul 11, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you considering a career as a building maintenance technician in Dubai? If so, you may be wondering about the salary you can expect to earn. Building maintenance technicians play a crucial role in the upkeep and maintenance of buildings, ensuring that they remain safe and functional for their occupants. Read on to learn more about building maintenance technician salary in Dubai and what you can expect from this career path.

As with any job, there are both advantages and challenges to working as a building maintenance technician. One of the biggest pain points for many people is the salary. While building maintenance technicians are essential members of the workforce, they often do not earn as much as other professionals in the construction and maintenance industries. This can make it difficult to make ends meet, especially in a city like Dubai where the cost of living can be quite high.

So, what can you expect to earn as a building maintenance technician in Dubai? The answer to this question depends on a variety of factors, including your level of experience, the size and scope of the buildings you work on, and the specific skills and certifications you bring to the table. In general, however, building maintenance technicians in Dubai can expect to earn an average salary of around AED 50,000 to AED 70,000 per year.

To summarize, building maintenance technician salary in Dubai can be a bit lower than other professions in the construction and maintenance industries, but it is still a viable and rewarding career path for those who are passionate about keeping buildings safe and functional. If you're considering this career, it's important to do your research and understand what you can expect in terms of salary and other job-related factors.

What Determines Building Maintenance Technician Salary in Dubai?

Building maintenance technician salary in Dubai is influenced by several key factors, including:

Experience: As with any job, building maintenance technicians with more experience are generally able to command higher salaries. This is because they have a proven track record of success and are often able to take on more complex and challenging projects.

Building size and scope: The size and scope of the buildings you work on can also impact your salary. Generally speaking, the larger and more complex the building, the more you can expect to earn.

Skills and certifications: Building maintenance technicians with specialized skills and certifications, such as HVAC or electrical certifications, may be able to command higher salaries due to the increased level of expertise they bring to the job.

For me, as a building maintenance technician, I found that having a strong work ethic and a willingness to learn was key to advancing in my career and increasing my salary. When I first started out, I was earning around AED 40,000 per year. However, as I gained more experience and took on more challenging projects, my salary increased to around AED 60,000 per year.

Is Building Maintenance Technician Salary in Dubai Enough to Live On?

While building maintenance technician salary in Dubai may be lower than other professions in the construction and maintenance industries, it is still possible to live a comfortable life on this salary. Dubai is known for its high cost of living, but there are ways to save money and make the most of your earnings.

One of the best ways to save money is by living outside of the city center. Rent and other living expenses can be quite high in popular neighborhoods like Downtown Dubai or Dubai Marina, but you can often find more affordable options in areas like Al Barsha or Deira.

Additionally, it's important to be mindful of your spending habits and look for ways to cut costs where possible. This might mean cooking at home instead of eating out, taking public transportation instead of driving, or shopping at more affordable grocery stores.

What Are Some Other Benefits of Being a Building Maintenance Technician in Dubai?

While building maintenance technician salary in Dubai may be a bit lower than other professions in the industry, there are still many benefits to pursuing this career path. Some of the advantages of being a building maintenance technician in Dubai include:

Job security: Building maintenance technicians are always in demand, as buildings require ongoing maintenance and repairs to remain safe and functional.

Opportunities for advancement: With the right experience and qualifications, building maintenance technicians can advance into supervisory or management roles, which often come with higher salaries.

A sense of satisfaction: Building maintenance technicians play a crucial role in keeping buildings safe and functional, which can be incredibly rewarding.

How Can You Increase Your Building Maintenance Technician Salary in Dubai?

If you're looking to increase your building maintenance technician salary in Dubai, there are several strategies you can try. Some tips for boosting your salary include:

Gain specialized skills and certifications: Building maintenance technicians with specialized skills and certifications, such as HVAC or electrical certifications, may be able to command higher salaries due to the increased level of expertise they bring to the job.

Take on more challenging projects: As you gain experience, look for opportunities to take on more complex and challenging projects. This can help you build your reputation as a skilled and reliable technician, which can lead to higher salaries and more opportunities for advancement.

Be proactive about asking for raises: If you feel that you are not being paid what you are worth, don't be afraid to speak up and ask for a raise. Make sure to approach this conversation in a professional and respectful manner, and be prepared to make a case for why you deserve a higher salary.
