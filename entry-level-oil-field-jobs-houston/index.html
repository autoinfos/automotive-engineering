Entry Level Oil Field Jobs Houston A Guide For Beginners

Written by Daniel Jun 11, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for a career in the oil industry but don't know where to start? Houston, TX is the heart of the oil industry in the United States, with numerous entry level positions available for those who are willing to work hard and learn. In this blog post, we will provide you with a comprehensive guide to entry level oil field jobs in Houston.

Working in the oil industry can be physically demanding and dangerous, but it can also be very rewarding. Some of the pain points associated with entry level oil field jobs include long hours, working in extreme weather conditions, and being away from home for extended periods of time. However, with the right training and attitude, these challenges can be overcome.

The target of entry level oil field jobs in Houston are individuals who are willing to learn and work hard. No prior experience is required, but a high school diploma or GED is usually necessary. Some common entry level positions include floorhands, leasehands, and roustabouts. These positions involve manual labor and provide the opportunity to learn about the industry from the ground up.

In summary, entry level oil field jobs in Houston are a great way to start a career in the oil industry. These jobs provide individuals with an opportunity to learn from experienced professionals and gain valuable skills. With hard work and dedication, entry level oil field jobs can lead to higher paying positions in the industry.

The Benefits of Entry Level Oil Field Jobs Houston

When I first started working in the oil industry, I was a floorhand for a drilling company in Houston. Although the work was physically demanding, I quickly realized the benefits of working in this field. Not only did I learn a lot about the industry, but I also earned a good salary and had opportunities for advancement.

One of the main benefits of entry level oil field jobs in Houston is the potential for career advancement. Many people who start out as floorhands or roustabouts go on to become drillers, toolpushers, or even company executives. In addition, the salaries for entry level positions are often higher than those for other industries with similar education requirements.

The Challenges of Entry Level Oil Field Jobs Houston

Working in the oil industry can be challenging, both physically and mentally. When I was a floorhand, I often worked 12-hour shifts in extreme weather conditions. I also had to be away from my family for weeks at a time, which was difficult. However, I found that with the right mindset and work ethic, these challenges can be overcome.

Another challenge of entry level oil field jobs in Houston is the potential danger. Working with heavy machinery and flammable materials can be hazardous, and safety is always a top priority in the industry. However, with proper training and safety procedures, accidents can be minimized.

Training for Entry Level Oil Field Jobs Houston

To prepare for entry level oil field jobs in Houston, it is important to have a high school diploma or GED. In addition, many companies require their employees to pass a drug test and a physical exam. Training is usually provided on the job, but there are also training programs available through community colleges and vocational schools.

Some of the skills that are necessary for entry level positions include physical strength, mechanical aptitude, and the ability to work in a team. It is also important to have good communication skills and the ability to follow instructions.

How to Find Entry Level Oil Field Jobs Houston

There are several ways to find entry level oil field jobs in Houston. One option is to apply directly to oil companies or drilling contractors. Another option is to use job search websites such as Indeed or Monster, which often have listings for entry level positions.

Networking is also an important part of finding a job in the oil industry. Attending industry events and conferences can provide opportunities to meet people who are already working in the field and learn about job openings.

Conclusion of Entry Level Oil Field Jobs Houston

Entry level oil field jobs in Houston are a great way to start a career in the oil industry. Although these jobs can be physically demanding and require long hours, they also provide opportunities for career advancement and high salaries. With the right training and attitude, entry level positions can lead to a rewarding and fulfilling career in the oil industry.
