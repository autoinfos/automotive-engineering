Are Microsoft Certifications Worth It In 2020

Written by Luffy Jul 14, 2023 · 5 min read

Microsoft certifications have been a topic of discussion for a long time. Many people wonder whether these certifications are worth it or not. In 2020, this question is even more relevant. With the current state of the world, many people are looking for ways to improve their skills and make themselves more marketable. In this blog post, we will explore the topic of whether Microsoft certifications are worth it in 2020.

One of the pain points related to the topic of Microsoft certifications is the cost. Certifications can be expensive, and many people wonder if the cost is worth it. Additionally, some people question whether certifications are necessary in the first place. If you already have experience working with Microsoft products, do you really need a certification?

The answer to the question of whether Microsoft certifications are worth it in 2020 is yes, they are. There are several reasons why certifications are valuable. First, certifications demonstrate to potential employers that you have a certain level of expertise. Second, certifications can help you stand out in a crowded job market. Finally, certifications can help you improve your skills and knowledge in a particular area.

In summary, Microsoft certifications are worth it in 2020. They can help you improve your skills, demonstrate your expertise to potential employers, and stand out in a crowded job market. Now, let's take a deeper dive into the topic.

What Are Microsoft Certifications?

Microsoft certifications are credentials that demonstrate your proficiency in using Microsoft technologies. There are several different types of certifications, including those for developers, IT professionals, and data professionals. Each certification requires passing one or more exams that test your knowledge of Microsoft products and technologies.

Personally, I have found Microsoft certifications to be incredibly valuable in my career. I work in IT, and my certifications have helped me demonstrate my expertise to my colleagues and employers. Additionally, the process of studying for a certification exam has helped me improve my skills and knowledge.

How Do Microsoft Certifications Help You Stand Out?

In a crowded job market, it can be difficult to stand out from the competition. Microsoft certifications can help you do just that. When you apply for a job, having a certification on your resume can help demonstrate to potential employers that you have a certain level of expertise. Additionally, certifications can help you get noticed by recruiters and hiring managers.

Personally, I have had several job interviews where my certifications were mentioned as a reason for being selected for an interview. Employers appreciate candidates who are committed to improving their skills and knowledge, and certifications are a great way to demonstrate that commitment.

What Are the Different Types of Microsoft Certifications?

There are several different types of Microsoft certifications, including those for developers, IT professionals, and data professionals. Each certification is designed to test your knowledge of specific Microsoft products and technologies. Some popular certifications include:

  • Microsoft Certified: Azure Administrator Associate
  • Microsoft Certified: Azure Developer Associate
  • Microsoft Certified: Dynamics 365 Fundamentals
  • Microsoft Certified: Modern Desktop Administrator Associate
  • Microsoft Certified: Power Platform App Maker Associate

How Do You Prepare for a Microsoft Certification Exam?

Preparing for a Microsoft certification exam requires a combination of study and practice. There are many resources available to help you prepare, including online courses, study guides, and practice exams. It's also important to gain hands-on experience with the technologies you will be tested on. Many certification exams include hands-on exercises that test your ability to perform specific tasks.

Personally, I have found that a combination of studying and hands-on experience is the most effective way to prepare for a certification exam. I like to use a variety of resources, including books, online courses, and practice exams, to ensure that I am fully prepared.

Question and Answer

Q: Are Microsoft certifications expensive?

A: Yes, Microsoft certifications can be expensive. The cost varies depending on the type of certification and the number of exams required. However, many employers will pay for their employees to obtain certifications.

Q: Do you need a Microsoft certification if you already have experience working with Microsoft products?

A: No, you don't necessarily need a certification if you already have experience working with Microsoft products. However, a certification can help demonstrate your expertise to potential employers and improve your chances of getting hired.

Q: What is the benefit of having a Microsoft certification?

A: There are several benefits to having a Microsoft certification. Certifications can help you improve your skills and knowledge, demonstrate your expertise to potential employers, and stand out in a crowded job market.

Q: How do you know which Microsoft certification is right for you?

A: The right Microsoft certification for you depends on your career goals and interests. It's important to research the different types of certifications available and choose one that aligns with your career aspirations.

Conclusion of Are Microsoft Certifications Worth It in 2020

In conclusion, Microsoft certifications are definitely worth it in 2020. They can help you stand out in a crowded job market, demonstrate your expertise to potential employers, and improve your skills and knowledge. While certifications can be expensive and time-consuming, the benefits far outweigh the costs. So, if you're looking to improve your career prospects in the world of Microsoft technologies, consider obtaining a certification.
