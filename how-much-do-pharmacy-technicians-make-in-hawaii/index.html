Are you considering a career as a pharmacy technician in Hawaii? If so, you may be wondering how much you can expect to make in this field. The good news is that pharmacy technicians in Hawaii typically earn a competitive salary, with opportunities for growth and advancement.

So, how much do pharmacy technicians make in Hawaii? According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the average hourly wage for pharmacy technicians in Hawaii is $19.53, with an annual salary of $40,630. However, this can vary depending on factors such as experience, location, and employer.
