Best Pharmacy Tech Schools in California and Accreditation

When it comes to choosing a pharmacy tech school in California, accreditation is essential. Accreditation ensures that the school meets high standards of education and training. One of the best accreditation agencies for pharmacy tech schools is the Accreditation Council for Pharmacy Education (ACPE). ACPE accredits pharmacy tech programs that meet strict guidelines regarding curriculum, faculty, and student outcomes.
