Common Interview Questions and Answers

One of the most common interview questions is "tell me about yourself." This question is often asked at the beginning of the interview and can throw some job seekers off guard. A good answer to this question should provide a brief overview of your professional background, highlighting your relevant experience and skills.

How to Answer Behavioral Interview Questions

Behavioral interview questions are designed to assess your past behavior and how it may impact your future performance. These questions typically begin with "tell me about a time when..." To answer these questions effectively, use the STAR method, which stands for Situation, Task, Action, and Result. This technique helps you to provide a structured and detailed answer that highlights your experience and skills.
