Software Developer Salary In Kenya Per Month What You Need To Know

Written by Bardi Oct 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a software developer in Kenya wondering if your salary is up to par with the industry standards? Or maybe you're considering a career in software development and want to know what kind of salary you can expect? Whatever the case may be, understanding the average software developer salary in Kenya per month can help you make informed decisions about your career.

Let's face it, one of the biggest challenges facing software developers in Kenya is the issue of low salaries. Many developers work long hours and have to deal with demanding clients, yet their pay does not reflect the effort they put in. This can lead to frustration and demotivation, and ultimately, a high turnover rate in the industry.

So, what can you expect in terms of software developer salary in Kenya per month? The answer is not straightforward, as salaries can vary depending on a number of factors. These include your level of experience, the industry you work in, and the company you work for. However, a recent survey conducted by BrighterMonday found that the average salary for software developers in Kenya is Ksh. 90,000 per month.

In summary, while software developer salaries in Kenya may not be as high as in other countries, there is still potential to earn a decent salary in the industry. It's important to do your research and negotiate your salary based on your skills and experience.

Factors Affecting Software Developer Salary in Kenya per Month

As mentioned earlier, there are several factors that can affect your software developer salary in Kenya per month. Let's take a closer look at some of these factors:

Experience Level

Generally speaking, the more experience you have as a software developer, the higher your salary will be. Junior developers can expect to earn less than senior developers with several years of experience under their belt.

Education and Training

Having a degree or diploma in computer science or a related field can also increase your earning potential as a software developer in Kenya. Employers tend to value formal education and training when considering candidates for higher-paying positions.

My Personal Experience with Software Developer Salary in Kenya per Month

As a software developer with 5 years of experience in the industry, I can attest to the fact that salaries in Kenya can be quite low compared to other countries. However, I've also learned that it's important to negotiate for a salary that reflects your skills and experience. I've had to turn down job offers that did not offer a fair salary, but I've also landed positions that paid well and allowed me to grow in my career.

Tips for Negotiating Your Software Developer Salary in Kenya per Month

If you're a software developer in Kenya looking to negotiate your salary, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

Do Your Research

Do some research on what other companies are offering for similar positions. This will give you an idea of what the industry standard is and how much you should be asking for.

Highlight Your Skills and Experience

Make sure you highlight your skills and experience during the negotiation process. This will show your employer that you bring value to the company and deserve a higher salary.

The Future of Software Developer Salary in Kenya per Month

While salaries for software developers in Kenya may be low at the moment, there is hope for the future. With the continued growth of the tech industry in Kenya and the increasing demand for skilled developers, it's likely that salaries will rise in the coming years.
