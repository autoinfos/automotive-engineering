Why Do You Want This Job Interview Question And Answer Example

Written by Bardi Aug 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Getting a job can be a nerve-wracking experience, especially when it comes to answering questions in an interview. One common question asked is "Why do you want this job?" This question is crucial because it helps the interviewer assess your motivation and fit for the job. In this blog post, we'll discuss why this question is significant and how to answer it effectively.

Employers want to know that you are genuinely interested in the job and not just looking for any job. They want to see your enthusiasm and passion for the company and the role you are applying for. Therefore, your answer should demonstrate your knowledge about the company and how your skills align with the job responsibilities.

The target of this question is to understand your motivation and assess whether you have done your research on the company and the position. Your answer should highlight your skills, experience, and knowledge that make you the best candidate for the job.

To answer this question effectively, you should research the company and the job description beforehand. This way, you can tailor your answer to the specific requirements of the job. Your answer should demonstrate your understanding of the company's values, mission, and culture.

Personal Experience

When I applied for a marketing job at XYZ company, I researched the company's website and social media pages to understand their values and mission. I also read the job description to identify the key responsibilities and requirements. With this knowledge, I was able to answer the question by highlighting my relevant experience and skills that matched the job requirements. I expressed my passion for the industry and how I would contribute to the company's growth.

How to Answer the Question

When answering this question, you should start by expressing your interest in the job and the company. Then, highlight your relevant skills and experience that match the job requirements. Next, explain how you would contribute to the company's growth and success. Finally, express your enthusiasm and passion for the industry and the opportunity to work for the company.

Research the Company

Researching the company is crucial to answering this question effectively. You should explore the company's website, social media pages, and news articles to understand their mission, values, and culture. This way, you can tailor your answer to the specific requirements of the job and demonstrate your knowledge about the company.

Practice Your Answer

It's essential to practice your answer before the interview to ensure that you are confident and articulate. You can practice with a friend or family member or record yourself answering the question. This way, you can identify areas that need improvement and make adjustments accordingly.

Personal Experience

When I applied for a content writing job, I practiced my answer by recording myself and listening to it. I realized that I was not being specific enough and needed to tailor my answer to the job requirements. After making adjustments, I felt more confident and articulate during the interview.

Question and Answer

Q: Why is the "Why do you want this job?" question important?

A: This question helps the interviewer assess your motivation and fit for the job. It demonstrates your enthusiasm and passion for the company and the role you are applying for.

Q: How can I answer this question effectively?

A: To answer this question effectively, you should research the company and the job description beforehand. Your answer should demonstrate your understanding of the company's values, mission, and culture.

Q: What should I avoid when answering this question?

A: You should avoid generic answers that do not demonstrate your enthusiasm and passion for the job. Also, avoid speaking negatively about your current or previous employer.

Q: Can I use the same answer for different companies and job positions?

A: No, you should tailor your answer to the specific requirements of the job and the company. Your answer should demonstrate your knowledge about the company's values, mission, and culture.

Conclusion of Why Do You Want This Job Interview Question and Answer Example

Answering the "Why do you want this job?" question effectively is crucial to impressing the interviewer and landing the job. Researching the company and the job description beforehand and tailoring your answer to the specific requirements of the job can help you demonstrate your knowledge and enthusiasm for the company and the role. Practicing your answer and avoiding generic responses can also help you stand out from other candidates. Use these tips to answer this question confidently and effectively.
