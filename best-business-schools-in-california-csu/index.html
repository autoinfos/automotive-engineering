What are the Best Business Schools in California CSU?

California State University has several business schools that offer undergraduate and graduate programs in various fields, including accounting, finance, marketing, and management. The following are some of the best business schools in California CSU:

1. California State University, Fullerton (CSUF)

CSUF's business school is one of the largest business schools in the United States, with over 9,000 students. The school offers several undergraduate and graduate programs, including an MBA program, a Master of Science in Accounting, and a Bachelor of Arts in Business Administration. CSUF's business school is accredited by AACSB International, a prestigious accreditation for business schools.

2. California State Polytechnic University, Pomona (Cal Poly Pomona)

Cal Poly Pomona's business school offers several undergraduate and graduate programs, including a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration (BSBA) and an MBA program. The school is known for its hands-on approach to learning, where students work on real-world projects and engage with local businesses. Cal Poly Pomona's business school is accredited by AACSB International.

3. San Diego State University, Fowler College of Business

The Fowler College of Business at San Diego State University offers several undergraduate and graduate programs in various fields, including accounting, finance, and marketing. The school is known for its diverse student population and its commitment to sustainability and social responsibility. The Fowler College of Business is accredited by AACSB International.

Student life in California CSU's business schools is diverse and vibrant.
