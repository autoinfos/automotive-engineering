Understanding the Scope of HVAC Jobs in Dubai

HVAC jobs in Dubai encompass a wide range of roles, from installation and maintenance to repair and troubleshooting. As an HVAC professional, you could be working on residential or commercial buildings, ensuring that the HVAC systems are operating efficiently and effectively. The job responsibilities can vary depending on the employer and the type of HVAC system you're working on, but some common duties include:

  • Installing and maintaining HVAC systems
  • Performing routine inspections to identify potential issues
  • Replacing or repairing defective components
  • Troubleshooting and diagnosing problems with the HVAC system
  • Providing recommendations for system upgrades or replacements
