Getting stranded on the side of the road is never a fun experience. Especially if you don't know where to turn for help. You may be wondering, "Will AAA tow my car to a dealership?" The answer is yes, and we're here to explain how it works.

If you find yourself in need of a tow to a dealership, it's likely because your car needs a specific type of repair that only the dealership can provide. This can be frustrating and stressful, but AAA is here to help make the process a little easier.

When you call AAA for a tow, simply let them know that you need to be taken to a dealership. They will dispatch a tow truck to your location and transport your vehicle to the dealership of your choice. It's that simple!

In summary, if you need to get your car to a dealership for repairs, AAA can provide a tow to get you there safely and efficiently. Just make sure to let them know your destination when you call for assistance.

Why Choose AAA for Your Dealership Towing Needs?

As a AAA member, you have access to a variety of benefits, including roadside assistance and towing services. When you need to get your car to a dealership, choosing AAA ensures that you'll receive reliable and efficient service.

Personally, I've had to use AAA's towing services to get my car to a dealership for repairs. The process was seamless, and the tow truck driver was friendly and professional. It gave me peace of mind knowing that my car was in good hands.

How to Request a Tow to a Dealership with AAA

If you find yourself in need of a tow to a dealership, follow these steps to request assistance from AAA:

1. Call AAA at their emergency roadside assistance number (found on the back of your membership card).

2. Let the representative know that you need to be towed to a dealership.

3. Provide your location and the name of the dealership you wish to be taken to.

4. Wait for your tow truck to arrive.

It's important to note that AAA's towing services are only available to members. If you're not already a member, consider joining to take advantage of their many benefits.

Understanding AAA's Towing Limits

When you call AAA for a tow to a dealership, it's important to understand their towing limits. Depending on your membership level, you may be limited in the number of tows you can receive each year. Be sure to check your membership benefits to understand your towing limits.

When it comes to choosing a dealership for your car repairs, it's important to do your research. Look for a dealership that specializes in your car's make and model, and has a good reputation for quality service. Read reviews from other customers to get an idea of their experience.
