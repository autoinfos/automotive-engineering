What is Certificate in Mechanical Engineering?

Certificate in Mechanical Engineering is a program that provides students with the skills and knowledge they need to work in the mechanical engineering industry. This certificate program covers a variety of topics, including mechanics, materials science, thermodynamics, and fluid mechanics, among others.

During this program, students learn how to design and analyze mechanical systems, as well as how to use computer-aided design (CAD) software. They also gain hands-on experience through laboratory work and projects.
