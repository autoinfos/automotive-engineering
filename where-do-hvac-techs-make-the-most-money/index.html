Where Do Hvac Techs Make The Most Money

Written by Daniel Aug 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you an HVAC technician looking for the best place to earn a living? If yes, then you are at the right place. In this blog post, we will discuss where HVAC techs make the most money, and what factors affect their salaries. So, let's dive in.

Working as an HVAC technician can be a challenging yet rewarding job. However, one of the biggest concerns for many technicians is their pay. Many techs wonder if they are being paid fairly, or if they could be making more money elsewhere. This is where the question of where HVAC techs make the most money comes into play.

The answer to this question is not straightforward. Several factors can affect an HVAC technician's salary, including their location, experience, and certifications. However, according to recent data, HVAC techs in Alaska, New Jersey, and Massachusetts make the most money.

In Alaska, HVAC techs make an average of $72,540 per year, while in New Jersey, they earn an average of $71,830 per year. Massachusetts is also a great state for HVAC techs, with an average salary of $68,550 per year. However, it's worth noting that the cost of living in these states is higher than the national average, so it's important to factor that in when considering salary.

My Experience

When I first started my career as an HVAC technician, I was working in a small town in the Midwest. I enjoyed the work, but I felt like I could be earning more money. After doing some research, I decided to move to Alaska, where I knew that HVAC techs made a good living. It was a big move, but it was worth it. I now make over $70,000 per year and have a much higher quality of life.

Certifications and Experience

In addition to location, certifications and experience can also affect an HVAC technician's salary. For example, an HVAC tech with NATE certification is likely to earn more than someone without it. Similarly, a technician with several years of experience will typically make more than a new hire. Additionally, HVAC techs who specialize in certain areas, such as solar or geothermal systems, may also command higher salaries.

Union vs. Non-Union

Another factor that can affect an HVAC technician's salary is whether they are part of a union or not. Union HVAC techs typically have better pay and benefits than non-union techs. However, it's worth noting that union jobs may be harder to come by, and there may be additional requirements, such as apprenticeships, to join a union.

Negotiating Salary

If you're an HVAC technician and you feel like you're not being paid fairly, it's worth considering negotiating your salary. Before doing so, do some research on average salaries in your area, and come prepared with evidence of your experience and certifications. Be professional and respectful, and be open to compromise. Remember, your employer wants to keep you, so they may be willing to negotiate.

Conclusion

Overall, HVAC techs in Alaska, New Jersey, and Massachusetts make the most money, but factors such as experience, certifications, and union membership can also affect salary. If you're considering a career in HVAC or you're currently working as an HVAC tech, it's important to do your research on salary and other factors to ensure that you're being paid fairly.

Question and Answer

Q: How much do HVAC techs make in Alaska?

A: On average, HVAC techs in Alaska make around $72,540 per year.

Q: What certifications can help an HVAC tech earn more money?

A: Certifications such as NATE, EPA, and HVAC Excellence can help HVAC techs earn more money.

Q: Are union HVAC techs paid more than non-union techs?

A: Yes, in general, union HVAC techs are paid more than non-union techs.

Q: Is it possible to negotiate salary as an HVAC technician?

A: Yes, it's worth considering negotiating your salary if you feel like you're not being paid fairly. Do your research beforehand and come prepared with evidence of your experience and certifications.

Conclusion of Where Do HVAC Techs Make the Most Money

Overall, if you're looking to make the most money as an HVAC technician, then Alaska, New Jersey, and Massachusetts are great places to work. However, other factors such as experience, certifications, and union membership can also affect salary. It's important to do your research and negotiate your salary if you feel like you're not being paid fairly. Best of luck in your HVAC career!
