How Many Subjects Are There In Diesel Mechanic In Iti

Are you interested in pursuing a career in diesel mechanics and wondering how many subjects are there in diesel mechanic in ITI? Look no further, as we have all the information you need to know about this topic.

Aspiring diesel mechanics often have questions about the curriculum and subjects taught in Industrial Training Institutes (ITI) for diesel mechanics. Many wonder about the number of subjects and the level of difficulty of the course.

In ITI, the diesel mechanic course typically consists of two years of study with four semesters. During this time, students are taught a total of 26 subjects, including both theory and practical classes.

The subjects covered in the diesel mechanic course in ITI include:

The First Year:

The first year of the diesel mechanic course in ITI comprises of the following subjects:

  • Engineering Drawing
  • Engineering Mathematics
  • Engineering Physics
  • Engineering Chemistry
  • Basic Electrical and Electronics Engineering
  • Workshop Practice
  • Welding and Gas Cutting
  • Fitting
  • Sheet Metal Work
  • Machine Shop Practices
  • Basic Computer Skills
  • Basic Safety Measures and First Aid

The Second Year:

The second year of the diesel mechanic course in ITI consists of the following subjects:

  • Advanced Electrical and Electronics Engineering
  • Transmission System in Automobiles
  • Steering, Suspension and Braking System
  • Basic Hydraulics and Pneumatics
  • Engine - Overhauling and Maintenance
  • Fuel System - Overhauling and Maintenance
  • Ignition and Electrical System - Overhauling and Maintenance
  • Vehicle Body Repair and Painting
  • Advanced Computer Skills
  • Entrepreneurship and Communication Skills

Why Choose Diesel Mechanic in ITI?

Studying diesel mechanic in ITI is a great option for those looking to build a career in the automobile industry. The course syllabus is designed to give students a comprehensive understanding of diesel engines, their maintenance, and repair.

ITI courses are designed to provide students with job-ready skills, which is a significant advantage in today's competitive job market. The diesel mechanic course in ITI is no exception, and students who complete the course are equipped with the necessary skills to work in the automobile industry.

FAQs About How Many Subjects in Diesel Mechanic in ITI

Q. What is the duration of the diesel mechanic course in ITI?

A. The diesel mechanic course in ITI is a two-year course with four semesters.

Q. How many subjects are taught in the diesel mechanic course in ITI?

A. A total of 26 subjects are taught in the diesel mechanic course in ITI.

Q. What are the job prospects after completing the diesel mechanic course in ITI?

A. After completing the diesel mechanic course in ITI, students can work in various roles such as diesel mechanic, automobile technician, service engineer, and more.

Q. Is the diesel mechanic course in ITI suitable for girls?

A. Yes, the diesel mechanic course in ITI is suitable for girls who are interested in pursuing a career in the automobile industry.

Conclusion of How Many Subjects in Diesel Mechanic in ITI

Studying diesel mechanic in ITI is an excellent option for those interested in pursuing a career in the automobile industry. The course curriculum is designed to equip students with the necessary skills to work in various roles related to diesel engines, their maintenance, and repair. With a total of 26 subjects taught over two years, the diesel mechanic course in ITI is a comprehensive course that provides students with job-ready skills.
