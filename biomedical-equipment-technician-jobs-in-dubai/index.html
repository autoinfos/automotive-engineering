What is a Biomedical Equipment Technician?

A biomedical equipment technician (BMET) is a professional who is responsible for installing, maintaining and repairing medical equipment. BMETs work in hospitals, clinics, and other healthcare facilities, and are responsible for ensuring that medical equipment is functioning properly and safely.

To become a BMET, you typically need to have a degree or certification in biomedical equipment technology, electronics or a related field. Some employers may also require you to have previous experience working with medical equipment.
