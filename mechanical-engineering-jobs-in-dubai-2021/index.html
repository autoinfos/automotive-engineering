Mechanical Engineering Jobs In Dubai 2021

Written by Petter Jun 24, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you a mechanical engineer looking for a new opportunity? Have you ever considered working in Dubai? With an ever-growing economy and a plethora of job opportunities available, Dubai is a great place to start your career or take it to the next level.

However, finding the right job in a new country can be challenging, especially if you're not familiar with the job market, culture, and visa requirements. This can be particularly frustrating if you're a skilled professional looking for a job that matches your qualifications and salary expectations.

If you're a mechanical engineer looking for a job in Dubai, you're in luck. Dubai is home to many engineering firms and companies that are always on the lookout for skilled professionals to join their teams. In this post, we'll explore some of the best mechanical engineering jobs in Dubai in 2021, and provide you with some tips to help you land your dream job.

The Target of Mechanical Engineering Jobs in Dubai 2021

Mechanical engineering is a broad field that encompasses many sub-disciplines, such as thermodynamics, materials science, robotics, and manufacturing. As a mechanical engineer, you could work in a variety of industries, such as aerospace, automotive, construction, energy, or healthcare.

In Dubai, there are many job opportunities for mechanical engineers, especially in the fields of oil and gas, construction, and manufacturing. Some of the most in-demand mechanical engineering jobs in Dubai in 2021 include:

1. Mechanical Design Engineer

As a mechanical design engineer, you'll be responsible for designing and developing mechanical systems, components, and products. You'll use CAD software and other tools to create 3D models and drawings, and work closely with other engineers, technicians, and project managers to ensure that your designs meet the client's requirements and specifications.

To be a successful mechanical design engineer in Dubai, you should have a degree in mechanical engineering or a related field, as well as experience in designing and testing mechanical systems. You should also be familiar with industry standards and regulations, such as ASME, ISO, and ASTM.

2. HVAC Engineer

As an HVAC engineer, you'll be responsible for designing and installing heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems in buildings, factories, and other structures. You'll work closely with architects, contractors, and project managers to ensure that the HVAC systems meet the client's needs and comply with local regulations.

To be a successful HVAC engineer in Dubai, you should have a degree in mechanical engineering or a related field, as well as experience in designing and installing HVAC systems. You should also be familiar with local regulations and standards, such as Dubai Green Building Regulations and Estidama.

3. Project Engineer

As a project engineer, you'll be responsible for managing and coordinating engineering projects, from conception to completion. You'll work closely with clients, contractors, and other stakeholders to ensure that the project is completed on time, within budget, and to the required quality standards.

To be a successful project engineer in Dubai, you should have a degree in mechanical engineering or a related field, as well as experience in project management and coordination. You should also be familiar with project management software and tools, such as Microsoft Project and Primavera.

How to Land a Mechanical Engineering Job in Dubai 2021

If you're interested in working as a mechanical engineer in Dubai in 2021, here are some tips to help you land your dream job:

1. Research the Job Market

Before you start applying for jobs, it's important to research the job market and identify the companies and industries that are hiring mechanical engineers in Dubai. You can use job search websites, such as Bayt, GulfTalent, and Naukrigulf, to search for mechanical engineering jobs in Dubai, and read job descriptions to understand the skills and qualifications required.

2. Network with Professionals

Networking is a great way to learn about job opportunities and connect with professionals in your field. You can attend industry events, join professional associations, and connect with people on LinkedIn to expand your network and learn about potential job openings.

3. Tailor Your Resume and Cover Letter

When applying for jobs in Dubai, it's important to tailor your resume and cover letter to the job requirements and the culture of the company. Make sure to highlight your relevant skills and experience, and customize your application to show that you're a good fit for the company and the role.

4. Prepare for Interviews

When you get invited for an interview, make sure to prepare thoroughly by researching the company, practicing your answers to common interview questions, and dressing appropriately. You should also be prepared to answer questions about your experience, skills, and qualifications, and show that you're enthusiastic and motivated to work in Dubai.

Question and Answer

Q: Do I need a visa to work in Dubai as a mechanical engineer?

A: Yes, you need a work visa to work in Dubai as a mechanical engineer. You can apply for a work visa through your employer, who will sponsor your visa and provide you with a residence permit.

Q: What is the average salary for a mechanical engineer in Dubai?

A: The average salary for a mechanical engineer in Dubai is around AED 100,000 to AED 200,000 per year, depending on your experience, skills, and qualifications.

Q: What are the working hours for mechanical engineers in Dubai?

A: The working hours for mechanical engineers in Dubai are usually 8 hours per day, 5 days per week. However, some companies may require longer hours or shift work, especially in the construction or oil and gas industries.

Q: What are the benefits of working as a mechanical engineer in Dubai?

A: Some of the benefits of working as a mechanical engineer in Dubai include tax-free income, a high standard of living, a multicultural and vibrant city, and opportunities for professional development and career advancement.

Conclusion of Mechanical Engineering Jobs in Dubai 2021

If you're a mechanical engineer looking for a new challenge, Dubai could be the perfect place for you. With a booming economy, a diverse range of industries, and a high demand for skilled professionals, Dubai offers many opportunities for mechanical engineers to grow their careers and achieve their goals. By following the tips in this post, you can increase your chances of landing your dream job in Dubai and enjoy all the benefits of living and working in this exciting city.
