The Job Outlook for Car Mechanics in Arizona

According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the job outlook for automotive service technicians and mechanics in Arizona is promising. The state is expected to see a 10% increase in job openings in this field between 2018 and 2028, which is higher than the national average for all occupations.
