Requirements for Heavy Vehicle Mechanic Apprenticeship in Ireland

To be eligible for heavy vehicle mechanic apprenticeship in Ireland, you must have completed your Leaving Certificate or an equivalent qualification. Additionally, you must have a valid driver's license and be able to demonstrate a keen interest in mechanics. Some employers may also require you to have completed a pre-apprenticeship course in mechanics.

How to Apply for Heavy Vehicle Mechanic Apprenticeship in Ireland

To apply for heavy vehicle mechanic apprenticeship in Ireland, you can start by researching potential employers who offer apprenticeships.
