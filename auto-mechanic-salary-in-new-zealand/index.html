How Much Can You Really Make As An Auto Mechanic In New Zealand

Written by Oliver Oct 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you interested in becoming an auto mechanic in New Zealand but worried about the salary potential? It's a common concern among those who are considering a career in this field. However, there are plenty of opportunities for auto mechanics in New Zealand, and the salary range can be quite competitive.

Auto mechanic salary in New Zealand can vary depending on several factors. These include your level of experience, the type of work you do, and the location of your job. However, the median salary for an auto mechanic in New Zealand is around $50,000 per year. This salary can go up to $80,000 per year for those with more experience or in a supervisory role.

In summary, auto mechanic salary in New Zealand can be quite lucrative, especially for those with experience in the field. With the right skills and training, there are plenty of job opportunities available that can offer a competitive salary and job security.

What to Expect from Auto Mechanic Salary in New Zealand

When I first started my career as an auto mechanic in New Zealand, I was unsure of what to expect in terms of salary. However, I quickly learned that there are plenty of opportunities for growth and advancement in this field.

One thing to keep in mind is that the type of work you do as an auto mechanic can impact your salary potential. For example, those who specialize in diesel engines or electronics may earn a higher salary than those who work on standard vehicles. Additionally, those who work in supervisory roles or run their own shop can earn a higher salary than entry-level mechanics.

How to Increase Your Auto Mechanic Salary in New Zealand

If you're looking to increase your auto mechanic salary in New Zealand, there are several steps you can take. One is to continue your education and training in the field. This can include obtaining certifications or attending workshops to learn new skills. Additionally, gaining experience in a specialized area of auto mechanics can make you more valuable to employers and increase your earning potential. Finally, consider networking with other professionals in the field to learn about new job opportunities or ways to increase your salary.

Real-Life Examples of Auto Mechanic Salary in New Zealand

When I first started my career as an auto mechanic, I was earning around $40,000 per year. However, as I gained more experience and specialized in diesel engines, my salary increased to $65,000 per year. Additionally, I've seen colleagues who run their own shops earn well over $100,000 per year.
