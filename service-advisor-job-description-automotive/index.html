You will be responsible for communicating with customers about their vehicle's needs and providing them with cost estimates. As a service advisor, you will need to have excellent communication skills and be able to explain complex information in an easy-to-understand way.

Service Advisor Job Description Automotive Skills

How to Become a Service Advisor in the Automotive Industry

If you are interested in becoming a service advisor in the automotive industry, there are a few steps you can take to get started. First, you will need to have a high school diploma or GED. You may also want to consider taking courses in automotive technology or business management.

Next, you will need to gain experience in the automotive industry. This could include working as a mechanic or a parts specialist. You could also consider working as an assistant service advisor to gain experience in the role.

Once you have gained the necessary experience, you can apply for service advisor positions at automotive service centers. You may also want to consider obtaining certification through organizations like the National Institute for Automotive Service Excellence (ASE) to demonstrate your knowledge and skills as a service advisor.
