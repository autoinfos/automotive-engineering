A kitchen helper job in UAE today involves working in a commercial kitchen to assist chefs and cooks with food preparation, cleaning, and other tasks. Kitchen helpers may be responsible for washing dishes, chopping vegetables, and stocking supplies. They may also be required to clean and sanitize kitchen equipment and surfaces. Kitchen helpers work in a fast-paced environment and need to be able to multitask and work well under pressure.
