Example 1: Sustainable Materials

One example of how automotive technology has influenced interior design is through the use of sustainable materials. The automotive industry has long been at the forefront of sustainability, and this has translated to the use of sustainable materials in interior design. By incorporating materials such as bamboo, cork, and recycled plastic, interior designers can create eco-friendly spaces that are both functional and stylish.

As an interior designer, I recently had the opportunity to work on a project that incorporated sustainable materials inspired by automotive technology. We used recycled plastic to create a unique wall covering that not only added texture to the space but also contributed to its sustainability. The end result was a beautiful and functional space that met the needs of our client while also being environmentally conscious.

Example 2: Smart Technology

Another example of how automotive technology has influenced interior design is through the incorporation of smart technology.
