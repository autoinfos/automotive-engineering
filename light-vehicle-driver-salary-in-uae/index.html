Light Vehicle Driver Salary In Uae All You Need To Know

Written by Daniel Jul 16, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you considering a career as a light vehicle driver in the UAE? Do you want to know how much you can earn in this profession? If so, then you’ve come to the right place! In this post, we’ll discuss everything you need to know about light vehicle driver salary in UAE.

Driving a light vehicle in the UAE can be a challenging job. You have to deal with heavy traffic, long hours, and unpredictable weather conditions. However, the biggest challenge for most drivers is the salary. Many drivers struggle to make ends meet, and some even have to work two jobs to support their families. This is why it’s essential to know the average salary for a light vehicle driver in the UAE.

The average salary for a light vehicle driver in the UAE is around AED 3,500 to AED 4,000 per month. However, this can vary depending on several factors such as experience, type of vehicle driven, and location. Some drivers can earn as much as AED 5,000 per month or more, while others may earn less than AED 3,000 per month.

In summary, the salary for a light vehicle driver in the UAE can be challenging. However, with the right experience and qualifications, you can earn a decent living in this profession.

The Importance of Light Vehicle Driver Salary in UAE

As mentioned earlier, many light vehicle drivers in the UAE struggle to make ends meet. This is because the cost of living in the UAE is high, and drivers have to pay for their accommodation, transportation, and other expenses. Therefore, it’s essential to have a decent salary that can cover these expenses and provide for their families.

Personally, I’ve been a light vehicle driver in the UAE for over a decade, and I’ve seen many drivers struggle to make ends meet. However, I’ve also seen many drivers who have been able to build a successful career in this profession and provide for their families.

Factors that Affect Light Vehicle Driver Salary in UAE

As mentioned earlier, several factors can affect the salary of a light vehicle driver in the UAE. These include:

Experience

The more experience you have as a driver, the higher your salary will be. Experienced drivers are often sought after by companies, and they can command a higher salary than those with little or no experience.

Type of Vehicle Driven

The type of vehicle you drive can also affect your salary. Drivers who drive larger vehicles such as trucks and buses often earn more than those who drive smaller vehicles such as cars and vans.

How to Increase Your Light Vehicle Driver Salary in UAE

If you want to increase your salary as a light vehicle driver in the UAE, there are several things you can do. These include:

Gain More Experience

The more experience you have, the more valuable you will be to companies. Therefore, it’s essential to gain as much experience as possible by working for different companies and driving different types of vehicles.

Upgrade Your Skills

There are several courses and certifications you can take that can help you upgrade your skills and increase your earning potential. These include defensive driving, first aid, and customer service courses.

Conclusion of Light Vehicle Driver Salary in UAE

In conclusion, the salary for a light vehicle driver in the UAE can be challenging. However, with the right experience, qualifications, and skills, you can earn a decent living in this profession. It’s essential to understand the factors that affect your salary and take steps to increase your earning potential.
