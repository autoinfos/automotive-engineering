How To Get A Job At An Exotic Car Dealership

Written by Petter Jun 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you passionate about cars and dream of working in a luxurious and exotic automotive environment? Getting a job at an exotic car dealership may seem like a difficult task, but it is possible with the right approach and mindset.

The first step to getting a job at an exotic car dealership is to understand the industry and its requirements. Most exotic car dealerships look for candidates with a strong background in sales, customer service, and automotive knowledge. Additionally, having a degree or certification in business, marketing, or automotive technology can give you an edge over other candidates.

To increase your chances of getting hired at an exotic car dealership, you should also focus on building a strong network within the industry. Attend automotive events, join car clubs, and connect with industry professionals on social media platforms such as LinkedIn. This will not only help you learn more about the industry but also create opportunities for networking and potential job openings.

In summary, to get a job at an exotic car dealership, you need to have a strong background in sales, customer service, and automotive knowledge. Having a degree or certification in business, marketing, or automotive technology is also beneficial. Building a strong network within the industry can also increase your chances of getting hired.

Personal Experience

My passion for cars started when I was a child, and I dreamed of working in an exotic car dealership. After completing my degree in business, I attended several automotive events and joined a local car club. Through networking and job searching, I landed a job as a sales representative at an exotic car dealership. My background in sales and business, combined with my passion for cars, helped me stand out from other candidates.

Tips for Building a Strong Network

Attending automotive events and joining car clubs can help you meet industry professionals and learn more about the industry. Additionally, connecting with professionals on social media platforms such as LinkedIn can create opportunities for networking and job openings. When networking, be sure to introduce yourself and express your interest in the industry. Ask questions and show genuine interest in learning more about the industry and the people you meet.

Skills and Qualifications

To work at an exotic car dealership, you need to have strong sales and customer service skills. Additionally, having a background in automotive technology and knowledge of exotic cars is beneficial. Many dealerships also require a degree or certification in business, marketing, or automotive technology.

Interview Tips

During the interview process, focus on showcasing your passion for cars and your knowledge of the industry. Be sure to research the dealership and its products prior to the interview. Show enthusiasm for the position and ask questions about the dealership and its products. Additionally, be prepared to discuss your sales and customer service experience and how it can benefit the dealership.
