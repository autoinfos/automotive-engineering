Car Dealership Receptionist Salary Per Hour How Much Can You Expect To Make

Written by Oliver Oct 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're considering a career as a car dealership receptionist, you may be wondering how much you can expect to make per hour. After all, salary is an important factor to consider when choosing a job. In this post, we'll explore the topic of car dealership receptionist salary per hour in detail.

One of the biggest pain points for those interested in becoming a car dealership receptionist is the uncertainty surrounding salary. It can be difficult to know what to expect, especially if you're just starting out in the industry or are unfamiliar with the job market.

So, what can you expect to make as a car dealership receptionist? The answer, as with many jobs, is that it depends. Factors such as location, experience, and the specific dealership you work for can all impact your salary. However, we can provide some general information to help give you an idea of what to expect.

In general, car dealership receptionists can expect to make between $10 and $18 per hour. However, some receptionists may make more depending on their experience, location, and other factors. It's also worth noting that many receptionists may be eligible for benefits such as health insurance, paid time off, and retirement savings plans.

My Experience with Car Dealership Receptionist Salary per Hour

When I first started working as a car dealership receptionist, I wasn't sure what to expect in terms of salary. However, I was pleasantly surprised to find that the dealership I worked for offered a competitive hourly wage, as well as benefits such as health insurance and paid time off.

Of course, salary isn't the only factor to consider when choosing a job. As a receptionist, I found that I enjoyed interacting with customers and helping them with their needs. I also appreciated the fast-paced nature of the job and the opportunity to work with a team of friendly and supportive coworkers.

Factors that Impact Car Dealership Receptionist Salary per Hour

As mentioned earlier, there are several factors that can impact how much you can expect to make as a car dealership receptionist. Some of these factors include:

  • Experience: Those with more experience may be eligible for higher pay.
  • Location: Salaries can vary depending on where you live and work.
  • Specific dealership: Certain dealerships may offer higher salaries or better benefits packages.

Why Do Salaries for Car Dealership Receptionists Vary?

The reason why salaries for car dealership receptionists can vary so widely is because there is no set standard for what the job entails. Some receptionists may have more responsibilities than others, such as managing inventory or assisting with sales. Additionally, different dealerships may have different revenue streams, which can impact how much they are able to pay their receptionists.

How Can You Negotiate a Higher Salary as a Car Dealership Receptionist?

If you're interested in negotiating a higher salary as a car dealership receptionist, there are a few things you can do. First, be sure to highlight any relevant experience or skills you have that make you a valuable asset to the dealership. Additionally, you can research salaries for similar positions in your area to get an idea of what is fair compensation. Finally, be confident in your worth and don't be afraid to ask for what you believe you deserve.

Conclusion of Car Dealership Receptionist Salary per Hour

In conclusion, salaries for car dealership receptionists can vary widely depending on a variety of factors. However, in general, receptionists can expect to make between $10 and $18 per hour. If you're interested in pursuing a career as a car dealership receptionist, be sure to research salaries in your area and highlight your relevant skills and experience.
