What Are Good Things To Put On A Resume For Skills

Written by Petter Sep 28, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you struggling to make your resume stand out among the competition? Do you find yourself wondering what are good things to put on a resume for skills? Look no further! In this post, we will explore some of the best skills to include on your resume to make it stand out.

Employers are looking for candidates with specific skills that will benefit their company. Without these skills, you may not even make it past the initial screening process. It's crucial to make sure your resume highlights your strengths and abilities in a way that catches the employer's attention.

So, what are good things to put on a resume for skills? First and foremost, make sure to include hard skills that are relevant to the job you are applying for. Hard skills are specific, teachable abilities that can be measured, such as proficiency in a particular software or programming language. Additionally, soft skills, such as communication and problem-solving, are also important to include on your resume.

In summary, when it comes to what are good things to put on a resume for skills, make sure to include both hard and soft skills that are relevant to the job you are applying for. Highlight your strengths and abilities in a way that catches the employer's attention.

Include Relevant Hard and Soft Skills

When it comes to creating a winning resume, including relevant hard and soft skills is crucial. Hard skills are specific, teachable abilities that can be measured, such as proficiency in a particular software or programming language. On the other hand, soft skills are personal attributes that enable you to work well with others, such as communication, teamwork, and problem-solving skills.

When I was applying for a job as a graphic designer, I made sure to include hard skills such as proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite and HTML/CSS. Additionally, I included soft skills such as teamwork, communication, and time management. These skills showed the employer that I had the technical skills needed for the job as well as the ability to work well with others.

Show Your Leadership Skills

Leadership skills are essential for any job, even if the position isn't a managerial one. Employers want to see that you have the ability to take charge and lead a team if necessary. Some of the top leadership skills to include on your resume are communication, decision-making, and problem-solving.

When I applied for a position as a project manager, I made sure to highlight my leadership skills. I included examples of times when I had to make tough decisions and how I communicated with my team to ensure successful project completion.

Highlight Your Achievements

One of the best ways to stand out on your resume is by highlighting your achievements. Employers want to see that you have a track record of success and that you can bring value to their company. Including achievements shows that you are results-driven and that you are willing to go above and beyond to achieve your goals.

When I applied for a position as a sales representative, I made sure to highlight my achievements. I included examples of times when I exceeded my sales targets and how I went above and beyond to ensure customer satisfaction. These achievements showed the employer that I was a valuable asset to any sales team.

Explain Your Technical Skills

If you are applying for a technical position, it's essential to explain your technical skills in detail. Employers want to see that you have the technical know-how to handle the job. Some of the top technical skills to include on your resume are programming languages, software proficiency, and hardware knowledge.

When I applied for a position as a software developer, I made sure to explain my technical skills in detail. I included a list of programming languages I was proficient in and examples of projects I had worked on. These technical skills showed the employer that I had the knowledge and experience needed for the job.

Question and Answer

Q: What are some soft skills to include on my resume?

A: Some soft skills to include on your resume are communication, teamwork, problem-solving, and time management.

Q: Should I include leadership skills on my resume?

A: Yes, including leadership skills on your resume can show employers that you have the ability to take charge and lead a team if necessary.

Q: What are some technical skills to include on my resume?

A: Some technical skills to include on your resume are programming languages, software proficiency, and hardware knowledge.

Q: Is it important to include achievements on my resume?

A: Yes, including achievements on your resume shows employers that you are results-driven and that you can bring value to their company.

Conclusion of What Are Good Things to Put on a Resume for Skills

In conclusion, when it comes to what are good things to put on a resume for skills, make sure to highlight both hard and soft skills that are relevant to the job you are applying for. Show off your leadership skills and highlight your achievements. By following these tips, you can create a winning resume that will catch the employer's attention.
