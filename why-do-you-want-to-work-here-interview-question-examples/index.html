Why Do You Want To Work Here Interview Question Examples Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Oct 08, 2023 Â· 3 min read

As an interior designer, you may be asked the question, "Why do you want to work here?" during a job interview. It's a common question that can be difficult to answer, but it's also an opportunity to show your knowledge and passion for the field. In this blog post, we'll discuss some tips and tricks for answering this question effectively.

The Pain Points

The "why do you want to work here?" question can be challenging because it requires you to articulate your motivations and goals in a concise and compelling way. You don't want to come across as generic or insincere, but you also don't want to ramble or sound unprepared. Additionally, you may not know much about the company or specific position you're interviewing for, which can make it harder to tailor your response.

The Target

When answering the "why do you want to work here?" question, your goal should be to demonstrate your enthusiasm for the field of interior design and your alignment with the company's values and mission. You should also highlight your relevant skills and experience and explain how they make you a good fit for the role.

Personal Experience

For example, when I was interviewing for my current position as an interior designer at XYZ Firm, I researched the company extensively and was impressed by their commitment to sustainable design and community engagement. I was also excited about the opportunity to work on a range of projects, from residential to commercial to hospitality. I shared my passion for these aspects of the job and explained how my previous experience with sustainable design and collaboration made me a strong candidate.

Related Keywords

Other related keywords that you may want to include in your response to the "why do you want to work here?" question include creativity, problem-solving, attention to detail, communication, and client satisfaction. Think about what sets you apart as an interior designer and how those qualities align with the company's goals.

Tips for Answering the "Why Do You Want to Work Here?" Question

1. Research the company and position beforehand to understand their values and needs.
2. Highlight your relevant skills and experience that make you a good fit for the role.
3. Share your passion for the field of interior design and how it aligns with the company's mission.
4. Use specific examples and anecdotes to illustrate your points.

Conclusion

Answering the "why do you want to work here?" question can be nerve-wracking, but with some preparation and practice, you can deliver a thoughtful and compelling response that showcases your skills and passion for interior design.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What should I research before answering the "why do you want to work here?" question?

You should research the company's values, mission, and current projects, as well as the specific position you're applying for and its requirements.

2. How can I make my answer stand out?

You can make your answer stand out by using specific examples and anecdotes to illustrate your points, highlighting your unique skills and experience, and demonstrating your enthusiasm for the field of interior design and the company's mission.

3. What are some related keywords I should include in my response?

Related keywords include creativity, problem-solving, attention to detail, communication, and client satisfaction. Think about what sets you apart as an interior designer and how those qualities align with the company's goals.

4. How can I tailor my response to the specific company and position?

You can tailor your response by researching the company and position beforehand, understanding their values and needs, and highlighting your relevant skills and experience that make you a good fit for the role.
